TEHRAN – A collection of photos from Iranian nomads are on display during the four-day Festival des civilizations, which started on March 28 in Paris. Entitled “Ashayer”, the collection is on display during the event, which is held on the theme of identity this year. The Festival of civilization is a transdisciplinary festival, composed of conferences, debates, projections, artistic creation and live spectacle, organized by the Inalco, France’s National Institute for Oriental Languages and Civilizations.
