TEHRAN – The secretary of Iran’s Human Rights Council on Saturday asked for appointment of special human rights rapporteurs for the countries which are directly and indirectly involved in suppressing and killing the regional people, including the U.S., Britain, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain and Israel. Mohammad Javad Larijani said the United States is the biggest supporter of crimes against humanity in Iraq, Yemen, Afghanistan, and many other places round the world, Fars reported.
