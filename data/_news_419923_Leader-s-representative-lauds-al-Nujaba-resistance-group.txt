TEHRAN – Mojtaba Hosseini, the Leader’s representative, has met with al-Nujaba resistance group chief Akram al-Kaabi in Baghdad, lauding the group’s role in the defeat of Daesh (ISIS) in Iraq and Syria. The two sides also discussed regional issues, especially Iraq’s battle against terrorism, AlNujaba.ir reported on Monday. During the meeting, al-Kaabi thanked Iran, Ayatollah Ali Khamenei and the Iranian nation for supporting the Iraqi nation and government in their fight against terrorism. MH/PA
