TEHRAN – Two Iranian warships set sail for Oman and the high seas on Sunday.

 The 47th flotilla of Iranian warships, comprised of Alborz destroyer and Bushehr logistic vessel, embarked on the voyage on Sunday afternoon, Press TV reported. They left southern Iranian port city of Bandar Abbas for Oman and then for the Indian Ocean and the Gulf of Aden. SP/PA
