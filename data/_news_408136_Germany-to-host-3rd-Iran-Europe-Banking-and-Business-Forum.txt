TEHRAN – The third edition of Iran-Europe Banking and Business Forum is due to be held on the sidelines of Euro Finance Week in Frankfort on November 14-18, IRNA reported on Tuesday. Iran’s reconnecting with the international banking system and the country’s business and investment opportunities are among the topics to be discussed in the conference. Specialized workshops on Iran banking system’s current status will be held on the sidelines of the event and representatives from Germany’s Federal Banking Supervision Directory, Deutsche Bank AG as well as officials from Iran will be attending these workshops. Euro Finance Week is one of Europe’s biggest banking and finance events and is held annually in November. The first edition of Iran-Europe Banking and Business Forum was held last year shortly after the implementation of Joint Comprehensive Plan of Actions (JCPOA).
