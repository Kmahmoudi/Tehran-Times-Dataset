TEHRAN- Iran exported €125 million of pistachio to Europe during the first half of 2018, with 19 percent increase compared to the same period of time in the past year, European Union’s statistics agency Eurostat has reported. According to Eurostat, Iran’s export of pistachio to Europe during the first half of 2017 stood at €105 million, Tasnim news agency reported on Friday.
