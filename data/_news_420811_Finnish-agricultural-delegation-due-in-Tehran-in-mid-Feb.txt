TEHRAN – A Finnish agriculture delegation is due to visit Tehran in mid-February to discuss expansion of mutual ties in the industry, IRNA reported. The decision was announced during a meeting between the Finnish Ambassador in Tehran Keijo Norvanto and the Director General of the Office of International Affairs at Agricultural Ministry Houman Fathi. During the meeting the two sides stressed the need for the expansion of ties between the two countries’ private sectors.
