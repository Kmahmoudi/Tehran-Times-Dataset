TEHRAN – “When the civil rights of Palestinians are violated, international organizations do not show any reaction,” a Rouhani aide said on Friday, accusing international bodies of adopting double standards.

 Elham Aminzadeh, talking to IRIB on the sidelines of Quds Day rally in Tehran, said the mercurial behavior comes despite the fact that “most international documents point to the rights of Palestinians to decide their fate.” SP/PA
