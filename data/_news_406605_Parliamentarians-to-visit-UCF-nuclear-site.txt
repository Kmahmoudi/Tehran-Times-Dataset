TEHRAN – The head of the Majlis Nuclear Committee has said his team will visit Isfahan’s uranium conversion facility (UCF) in the near future.

 Mojtaba Zonnuri made the statement while providing a report about their visit to the Arak heavy water reactor a few days earlier, Mehr reported on Wednesday. He added the time for the visit will be announced after the Majlis recess. On the Arak facility, he said the reactor will be used for a multiplicity of purposes once it undergoes redesign. SP/PA
