TEHRAN – Chief of the Navy Force of the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps (IRGC) has underlined developing military cooperation between the IRGC and Oman’s military. Rear Admiral Ali Fadavi made the remarks in a meeting with a visiting Omani delegation on the sidelines of 14th meeting of Iran-Oman Joint Military-Defense Cooperation Committee which was held in Tehran, IRNA reported. Due to religious and historical bonds and the good neighborly relations, ties between the two countries can turn into an example for others, Fadavi noted.
