TEHRAN - The National Iranian Standards Organization and Armenia’s Ministry of Economic Development and Investments signed a memorandum of understanding (MOU) on Halal industries cooperation on Wednesday in Tehran. According to IRNA, The MOU aims for improving bilateral cooperation and boosting the level of trade between the two sides. 
Earlier in April, the Islamic Republic of Iran Customs Administration (IRICA) and Customs Service of Republic of Armenia also signed a memorandum of understanding (MOU) on customs cooperation.
