TEHRAN – MP Ali Motahari has said he has no doubts about the criminality of the Mojahedin-e Khalgh Organization.

 “The Forghan Group who killed my father were a branch of the MKO,” he told the Majlis on Tuesday, the YJC reported. SP/AK
