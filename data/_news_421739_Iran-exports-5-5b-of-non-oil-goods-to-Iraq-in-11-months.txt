TEHRAN- During the 11 months ending on February 19, 2018, Iran exported 12 million tons of non-oil goods to Iraq worth $5.5 billion, the Public Relations Department of the Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO) announced. The figure shows 18 percent increase in comparison with the same period in the preceding year, the report added. According to available data, 66 percent of Iranian goods have been exported to China, Iraq, South Korea, and Afghanistan in the said time, while China and Iraq are the major importers.
