TEHRAN- According to the latest report released by the Central Bank of Iran (CBI), the granted banking facilities to domestic economic sectors witness 18.4 percent growth during the first four month of the present Iranian calendar year (March 21-July 22, 2017) IRNA reported on Sunday. 
 More than 1.53 quadrillion rials (about $39.9 billion) of loans were paid to Iranian economic sectors in the said period, showing 238 trillion rials (about $6.2 billion) increase, i.e. 18.4 percent growth, from the same time span in the preceding year. HJ/MA
