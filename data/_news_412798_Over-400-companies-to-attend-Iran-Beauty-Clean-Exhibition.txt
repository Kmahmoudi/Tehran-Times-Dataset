TEHRAN- Some 427 Iranian and foreign companies will take part in the 24th International Exhibition of Detergent, Cleanser, Hygienic, Cellulose Products & Related Machinery of Iran (Iran Beauty & Clean) from April 24 to 29, IRNA reported. As reported, some 237 Iranian companies and 190 foreign ones will participate in the event which will be held at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds.
The foreign participants reportedly come from China, Italy, Turkey, South Korea, France, Germany, Poland, the United Arab Emirates, Spain, India, Switzerland, Malaysia, Russia, England, the Netherlands, Japan, Lithuania, Greece, Czech Republic, Belarus, Slovenia, Ireland, the United States, and Belgium.
