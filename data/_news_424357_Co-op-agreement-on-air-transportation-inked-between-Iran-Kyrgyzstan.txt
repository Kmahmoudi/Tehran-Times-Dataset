TEHRAN- Iran and Kyrgyzstan signed an agreement to boost cooperation on air transportation, the head of Public Relations Department of Iran Civil Aviation Organization (CAO) Reza Jafarzadeh announced, IRIB reported on Monday. The agreement was signed by deputy head of Civil Aviation Agency of Kyrgyz Republic (CAA KG) and that from CAO, he said. In his meeting with CAO Head Ali Abedzadeh, the Kyrgyz official, who arrived in Tehran leading a delegation, called for expansion of air transportation cooperation with Iran.
