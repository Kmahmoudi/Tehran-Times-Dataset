TEHRAN — Ten new stem cell products were unveiled during the closing ceremony of the 3rd National Festival and the International Congress on Stem cells and Regenerative Medicine (November 28 to December 1) in Tehran.
 The ceremony took place with First Vice-President Es’haq Jahangiri and Vice-President for Science and Technology Sourena Sattari, ISNA reported on Saturday. Laser scanning microscope, confocal microscope, 3D printer, skin cell therapy devices for skin regeneration and wound healing are some of the products.
