TEHRAN - After the implementation of nuclear deal in January 2016, the number of countries which import Iranian goods increased to 15 from the eight destinations before the deal, ILNA reported on Tuesday quoting a trade official. According to Mojtaba Khosrotaj, the head of Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO), Iranian goods were exported to more than 20 countries before the west-imposed sanctions cut the number to eight.
