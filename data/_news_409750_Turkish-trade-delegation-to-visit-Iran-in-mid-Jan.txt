TEHRAN- Antalya Chamber of Commerce and Industry (ACCI) is to dispatch its trade delegation to Iran on January 15, the portal of Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) announced. The Turkish delegates, led by the ACCI’s head, plan to meet their Iranian counterparts in the place of TCCIMA in Tehran to confer on future cooperation.
As reported, the visiting delegates are active in various areas including construction, interior design, industrial machineries, energy, tourism, agriculture, real estate, transportation, petrochemicals, foodstuff, textile, and etc.  HJ/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
