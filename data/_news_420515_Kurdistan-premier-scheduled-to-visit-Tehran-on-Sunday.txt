TEHRAN - The Prime Minister of the Kurdistan Regional Government (KRG) Nechirvan Barzani is scheduled to visit Tehran on Sunday to hold talks with Iranian officials. Barzani will visit Tehran as an extension of his diplomatic endeavors and dialogue with other countries in order to talk about political, economic, security and cultural issues, according to a statement from Iran’s consulate in Erbil that was released on Saturday. The premier will be accompanied by a delegation including Deputy PM Qubad Talabani, according the consulate. The consulate considers the visit very important and positive.
