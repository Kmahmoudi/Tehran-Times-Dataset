TEHRAN – The 35th Fajr International Film Festival announced juries for the Eastern Vista and Interfaith sections of the event on Monday.

 Directors Hany Lasheen from Egypt, Darezhan Omirbaev from Kazakhstan, Zeki Demirkubuz from Turk and Kamal Tabrizi from Iran, and Azerbaijani actress Hamida Omarova are the members of the jury for Eastern Vista, which screens movies from Asian filmmakers. The jury for the Interfaith Competition is composed of Polish theologian and TV producer Michal Legan, Italy’s Religion Today festival artistic director Katia Malatesta and Cinema Organization of Iran former director Hojatollah Ayyubi. The 35th Fajr International Film Festival will be held in Tehran from April 21 to 28.
