TEHRAN- Stock index gained 327 points to 81.509 in Iran’s stock exchange market on Wednesday. Over 1 billion shares worth 3.042 trillion rials (about $80.2 million) were dealt in the stock market.
 Meanwhile, IFX, the main index of Iran's over-the-counter (OTC) market known also as Iran Fara Bourse (IFB), had no change, standing at 920 points on Wednesday.  MA/MG
