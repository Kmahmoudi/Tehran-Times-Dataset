TEHRAN – On June 7, concurrent with the terrorist attacks in Tehran, security forces arrested a terrorist gang in the central city of Qom, the political deputy to Qom governor-general said on Tuesday.

 Ahmad Jamali said security forces confiscated bombing equipment from them. Elsewhere, security forces discovered a remote-control explosive unit in suburban Ardabil, 700 meters away from a village named Golshan. SP/PA
