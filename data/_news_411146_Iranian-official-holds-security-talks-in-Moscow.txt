TEHRAN – Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister Ebrahim Rahimpour visited Moscow on Wednesday for talks on security cooperation.

 Rahimpour and his Russian counterpart Igor Morgulov reviewed cooperation in fight against terrorism and extremism, Fars news agency reported. The sides also talked about the upcoming exchange of meetings between officials of the two countries and reviewed the documents that are to be signed during these visits. SP/PA
