TEHRAN- A German trade delegation from the southeastern state of Bavaria will be in Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) on May 1, to negotiate expansion of ties with Iranian entrepreneurs, the official website of TCCIMA announced. The members of the said Bavarian delegation are active in electronics and electrotechnics, education, construction, health projects, food industry and agriculture, exhibition services, investment consulting and risk management, environment, machineries and industrial equipment, oil and gas, and etc. HJ/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
