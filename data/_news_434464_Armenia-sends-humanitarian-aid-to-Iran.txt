TEHRAN – Armenia on Monday dispatched humanitarian aid for victims of large-scale floods in Iran, ILNA reported. The humanitarian aid included four trucks consisting of necessary goods.  The aid was set to be delivered to Iran’s Red Crescent Society in the city of Tabriz. Also on Monday, a press conference was held in Tehran after the delivery of humanitarian aid by the German Red Cross. The press conference was attended by Rescue and Relief Organization head Morteza Salimi and German Ambassador to Tehran Michael Klor-Berchtold.
