TEHRAN – The secretary of the Guardian Council said on Sunday that Iranians should take care to vote for the right candidate.

 Speaking about the upcoming presidential election, Ayatollah Ahmad Jannati said the right person is one who “knows the enemy” and is “revolutionary”, Fars news agency reported. He also voiced concern over the high number of registered candidates, saying a proper law should be put in place to limit the number of eligible candidates. SP/PA
