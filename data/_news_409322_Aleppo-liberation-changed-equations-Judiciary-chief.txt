TEHRAN – The Iranian Judiciary chief has hailed the liberation of the embattled Syrian city of Aleppo from terrorist groups, saying the victory has radically changed equations in the Middle East and beyond.

 Speaking at a meeting of high-ranking judiciary officials on Monday, Ayatollah Sadeq Amoli Larijani offered his congratulations to the Syrian people and government over the liberation of the city and said, “The West and terrorists were seeking to make Aleppo a base for their terrorist acts to create changes in the entire region.”  SP/PA
