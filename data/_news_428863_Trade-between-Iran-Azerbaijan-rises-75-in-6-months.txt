TEHRAN- The value of trade between Iran and Azerbaijan during the first half of the current Iranian calendar year (March 21-September 22) has risen 75 percent compared to that of the same period of time in the previous year, the chairman of Iran-Azerbaijan Joint Chamber of Commerce told ILNA. Behrouz Pour-Soleyman put the annual trade between the two countries at $55 million in the previous year and said that the figure is anticipated to reach $80 million in this year.
