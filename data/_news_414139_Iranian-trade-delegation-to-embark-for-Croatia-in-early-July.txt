TEHRAN- Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) plans to dispatch a trade delegation headed by ICCIMA Chairman Gholam-Hossein Shafeie to Croatia from July 4 to 7, 2017, the official website of ICCIMA announced. The visit is aimed for the two side’s businessmen to get to know each other and explore grounds of cooperation.  EF/MA
