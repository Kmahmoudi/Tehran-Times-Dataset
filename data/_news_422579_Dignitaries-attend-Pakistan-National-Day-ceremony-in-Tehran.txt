TEHRAN – The ambassador of Pakistan to Iran hosted a reception to commemorate 78th Pakistan’s National Day in a hotel in Tehran on Tuesday evening. Iranian Interior Minister Abdolreza Rahmani Fazli was the chief guest on the occasion, according to a press release. A number of other guests including Alaeddin Borojardi, the chairman of the Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee, and deputy ministers of defense, interior and trade attended the reception. Also, members of the parliament, high ranking civil and military officials, diplomats, scholars, media persons and prominent citizens of both countries attended the event.
