TEHRAN – A new commander has been appointed to the IRGC Ground Force Operation Headquarters, Fars reported on Wednesday.

 Majid Arjomandfar was appointed to the position by IRGC Ground Force chief Mohammad Pakpour. Arjomandfar previously held the position of deputy commander of Hamzah Seyyed ol-Shohada Base. The post was so far held by Ali Akbar Pourjamshidian, who was recently appointed to the commandership of the force’s coordination headquarters. SP/PA
