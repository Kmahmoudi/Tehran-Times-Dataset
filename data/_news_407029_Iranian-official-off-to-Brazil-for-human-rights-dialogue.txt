TEHRAN – The head of Iran’s High Council for Human Rights departed for Brazil on Monday to hold talks with senior Brazilian judicial and human rights officials.

 The visit is made upon the official invitation of the Brazilian side, ISNA reported. While in Brazil, Mohammad Javad Larijani will hold talks with a number of senior Brazilian officials to foster mutual human rights and judicial cooperation.
