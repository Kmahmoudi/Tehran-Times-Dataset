TEHRAN- The 10th International Exhibition of Stone, Marble, Quarry, Machinery & Equipment of Iran (IRSE 2018) will be held at Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds from July 19 to 22, IRIB reported on Saturday. Some 160 Iranian and 55 foreign companies from Germany, Italy, China, Australia and Turkey will showcase their latest products and services in this event. Some foreign trade delegations are also planned to visit the exhibition to discuss future investments and cooperation with Iranian businessmen and producers in this sector.
