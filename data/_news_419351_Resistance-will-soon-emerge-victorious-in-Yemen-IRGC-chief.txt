TEHRAN – Commander of the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps (IRGC) said on Thursday that the Islamic resistance front will soon celebrate a victory in Yemen. Addressing an academic conference in Tehran, Major General Ali Jafari said the Islamic resistance movement is gaining more victories and is expanding its influence in the region and beyond. “Over the past 20 years, we have been witnessing successive victories for the axis of resistance and defeat of the enemies of the Islamic Revolution in all Middle East countries,” Jafari was quoted by Tasnim as saying.
