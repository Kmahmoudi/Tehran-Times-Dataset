TEHRAN – Armenian Prime Minister Nikol Pashinyan will pay an official visit to Iran in February, Armenpress has reported. “On February 27 I am leaving for Iran on an official visit,” Pashinyan said at a meeting with the Armenian community in Cologne, Germany, on the sidelines of his official visit to the European country. Iran and Armenia enjoy good relations. President Hassan Rouhani met the Armenian prime minister on the sidelines of the 73rd session of the United Nations General Assembly in September 2018.
