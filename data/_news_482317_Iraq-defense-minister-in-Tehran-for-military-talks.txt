TEHRAN - Iraqi Defense Minister Thabet Muhammad Saeed Al Abbas landed in Tehran on Saturday afternoon for strengthening military cooperation between Baghdad and Tehran. Thabet Muhammad Saeed Al Abbasi was welcomed by his Iranian counterpart Brigadier General Mohammad Reza Gharaei Ashtiani.
