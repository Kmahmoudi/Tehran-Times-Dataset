TEHRAN — Resolving environmental problems requires national determination and cooperation, a professor and former MP has said, Ana reported on Monday. 

 All organizations, bodies, officials and the public are responsible for the environment and should work towards a solution, Ali Abbaspour said.    Environmental issues are one of the country’s predicaments and to get to grips with it “we need to employ all sciences”, Abbaspour added. Environment has been treated unfairly, resulting in desertification, dust storms, water scarcity, climate change, etc. now haunting us, he regretted.  SP/
