TEHRAN – Alaeddin Boroujerdi, chairman of the Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee, met on Tuesday with Ukrainian Foreign Minister Pavlo Klimkin to discuss bilateral ties.

 Boroujerdi, heading a high-ranking parliamentary delegation, arrived in Kiev on Monday to hold talks with senior Ukrainian officials. He is visiting Ukraine at the invitation of his Ukrainian counterpart, Fars reported. The senior lawmaker also met with Ukrainian Parliament Speaker Andriy Parubiy on Monday as the two sides discussed broadening of parliamentary relations. MH/PA
