TEHRAN -- Director Kamal Tabrizi’s infidelity drama “Mina’s Choice” and Saeid Rustai’s poverty and drug movie “Life+1 Day” each received nine nominations in various categories including best film and best director at the 34th Fajr Film Festival, the organizers announced on Tuesday.
