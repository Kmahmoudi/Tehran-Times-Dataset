TEHRAN- Government Spokesman Mohammad Baqer Nobakht announced on Tuesday that 170 trillion rials (about $4.5 billion) of facilities have been paid to domestic production units by July 3, IRIB news reported. As he further explained, the mentioned figure has been paid to 25,351 production units by the said time and the government plans to provide industrial units with loans as well on the way to increase employment and production in the country.
