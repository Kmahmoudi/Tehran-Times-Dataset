TEHRAN - Following a gas explosion in Iraq’s Sulaymaniyah, which left dozens of people dead and injured, Iranian Foreign Ministry Spokesman Nasser Kanaani extended condolences to the nation and government of Iraq, especially the people and officials of Sulaymaniyah Province. He offered sympathy with the bereaved families and prayed for divine mercy for the deceased and swift recovery for the wounded, according to the Iranian foreign ministry.    All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
