TEHRAN- Export Guarantee Fund of Iran (EGFI), signed a cooperation MOU with Companhia de Seguro de Créditos S.A. (COSEC), the leading insurer in Portugal for credit and bond insurance, Tasnim news agency reported on Wednesday. According to the penned MOU, the two sides will collaborate on transferring economic data, commerce, banking, expansion of insurance coverage, and etc. 
The MOU signing ceremony was reportedly attended by the Portuguese Secretary of State of Internationalization Jorge Costa Oliveira as well as the two companies' managing directors. HJ/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
