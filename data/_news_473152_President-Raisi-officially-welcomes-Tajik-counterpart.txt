TEHRAN— In an official ceremony held on Monday morning, Iran’s President Seyyed Ebrahim Raisi welcomed his Tajik counterpart, Emomali Rahmon, who arrived in Tehran on Sunday. 
 Rahmon was welcomed by Raisi in Saad Abad Palace in northern Tehran.  The two presidents are scheduled to sign various cooperation documents after their meeting.    All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
