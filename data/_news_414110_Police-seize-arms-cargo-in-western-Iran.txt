TEHRAN – The Iranian police announced it has arrested two terrorists in the western Kermanshah province and seized over 92 handguns from them.

 A total number of 92 Colt handguns were accordingly seized in a Peugeot 405 car and the two terrorists were arrested in connection with the Wednesday terrorist attacks in Tehran, Fars reported. The Wednesday attacks on Majlis and Imam Khomeini shrine took 17 lives and wounded more than 50 people. SP/PA
