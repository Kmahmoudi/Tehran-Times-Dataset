TEHRAN – The 22nd Press Exhibition opened at Tehran’s Imam Khomeini Mosalla on Friday. However, an official inauguration ceremony, which will be attended by President Hassan Rouhani, is scheduled to be organized this afternoon.   Afterwards, he will pay a visit to the exhibition, which is Iran’s most significant media showcase. Representatives of over 900 media, including newspapers, magazines, news agencies and press organizations are participating in the event. Various programs, including meetings, seminars and professional workshops will be organized during the exhibition. The organizers have invited some foreign professional journalists to share their experiences with Iranian journalists at the exhibition, which runs through November 11.  Photo: People visit the 22nd Press Exhibition at Tehran’s Imam Khomeini Mosalla on November 3, 2016.
