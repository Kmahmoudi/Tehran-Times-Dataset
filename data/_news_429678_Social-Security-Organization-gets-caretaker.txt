TEHRAN —Labor Minister Mohammad Shariatmadari named Anoushirvan Mohseni-Bandpey as the caretaker for the Social Security Organization, IRNA reported.
 Following a tragic incident on Thursday, the Organization’s director Taqi Noorbakhsh and his deputy for parliamentary affairs Abdorrahman Tajoddin passed away in a car crash in the northern province of Golestan.  Mohseni-Bandpey is currently head of the Welfare Organization and also was the acting labor minister before Shariatmadari took office in early October.
