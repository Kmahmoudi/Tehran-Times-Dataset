TEHRAN—An earthquake measuring 5.9 on Richter scale shook the western city of Gilan-e Gharb, Kermanshah province, on Sunday.  The quake hit the region at around 5 p.m. local time at the depth of 10 kilometeres from earth’s surface.  According to Tasnim news agency 42 were injured but so far no deaths are reported.  MQ/ All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
