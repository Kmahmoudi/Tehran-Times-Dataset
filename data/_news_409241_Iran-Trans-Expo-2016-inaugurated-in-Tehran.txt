TEHRAN- The 1st International Exhibition of Transports and Logistics, Road Construction, Road Maintenance, and Related Industries of Iran known as Iran Trans Expo 2016 kicked off in Tehran on Sunday. The three-day exposition started operation in the presence of the Iranian Transport Minister Abbas Akhoundi and with the participation of 115 Iranian private firms at Imam Khomeini Grand Prayer Campus (Mosalla).
