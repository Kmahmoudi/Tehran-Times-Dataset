TEHRAN- The number of Iran’s exclusive exhibitions in Iraq increased during the finishing Iranian calendar year (ending March 20, 2018), the portal of Trade Promotion Organization of Iran quoted Iranian commercial attaché to Iraq.
 “Iraqis demand for Iranian goods has increased and as expected Iran’s non-oil exports to its western neighbor will go up in future thanks to the held exhibitions and effective marketing,” Naser Behzad said. As he described, 300 Iranian companies, active in different fields including industry, agriculture, mining, trade and engineering services, have showcased their latest products in the said period in Iraq.
