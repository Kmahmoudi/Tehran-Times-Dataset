TEHRAN- Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) plans to dispatch a trade delegation to South Korea on January 5. The delegates active in the foodstuff, information technology (IT) and auto parts fields will stay five days in the East Asian country to find potential South Korean partners through B-2-B meetings, the portal of ICCIMA published on Sunday.
