TEHRAN – The Iranian intelligence minister has expressed support for those who invest in the production sector.

 “Real investors should not be concerned about the security of investment as the country’s intelligence services will support them,” Mahmoud Alavi said on Wednesday, IRNA reported. He further touched upon the concern about the unemployment rate in the country, saying the government cannot resolve the issue in the absence of investment by the private sector. SP/PA
