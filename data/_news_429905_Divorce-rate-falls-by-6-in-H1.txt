During the first six months of the current year, a total number of 48,287 cases of divorce are recorded which shows 6.3 percent decrease year on year in which 51,514 cases of divorce was recorded, IRNA quoted Seyfullah Abu-torabi, spokesman for National Organization for Civil Registration, as saying. The divorce rate varies in different provinces and in order to study divorce rate in each part of the country a 5-year timeframe should be considered, Abu-torabi added. Meanwhile, a total number of 191,267 cases of marriage were recorded in the first six months of the year, the official said.
