TEHRAN – A senior foreign policy advisor to the parliament speaker has hailed the recent pro-Palestinian conference in Tehran, saying all Palestinian groups took part in the conference and managed to reach "great agreements". In an interview with Al-Manar TV channel, Hossein Amir Abdollahian said the Palestinian groups reached agreements among them campaigns to free al-Quds and support Palestinian people. He also underlined that all groups should unite to fight against the Zionist regime’s atrocities.
