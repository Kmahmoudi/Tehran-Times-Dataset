TEHRAN- A top official from Iran Small Industries and Industrial Parks Organization (ISIPO) announced that domestic small and medium-sized enterprises (SME) could export 2.3 billion of goods during the previous Iranian calendar year 1396 (ended March 20, 2018), IRNA reported on Saturday. “In 1396, some 2,575 industrial units were inaugurated employing 39,622 people and using 77.5 trillion rials (above $1.5 billion) of investments,” Farshad Moghimi said.
