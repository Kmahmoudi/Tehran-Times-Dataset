TEHRAN- Seyed Reza Norouz-zadeh was appointed as the new managing director of Iran’s National Petrochemical Company (NPC), Shana reported on Sunday. Oil Minister Bijan Namdar Zanganeh appointed Norouz-zadeh to the post replacing Marziyeh Shahdaie, who was appointed by Zanganeh as the vice oil minister replacing Kazem Vaziri Hamaneh.  Norouz-zadeh has been previously in several positions including deputy industry minister, board chairman of Industrial Development and Renovation Organization of Iran (IDRO), and managing director of Social Security Investment Company (Shasta).
