TEHRAN – The 35th Fajr International Film Festival announced the lineup for the Retrospective of Korean Cinema on Tuesday.

 Seven movies from South Korean filmmakers will be reviewed in the program.
 
“The Table” by Kim Jong-kwan, “The Net” by Kim Ki-duk, “Train to Busan” by Yeon Sang-ho and “The Tunnel” by Kim Seong-hun are among the films. The lineup also includes “The Throne” by Lee Joon-ik, “The Last Princess” by Hur Jin-ho and “Luck-Key” by Lee Gae-byok. The 35th Fajr International Film Festival will be running from April 21 to 28.
