TEHRAN- Bilateral trade between Iran and Iraq exceeded six billion dollars in 2015-16, the Iraqi Ministry of Commerce announced. As IRNA reported, regarding the two countries’ tendency for boosting commerce ties, the volume of bilateral trade is predicted to witness an upward trend in future, too. Iran-Iraq economic transactions have been ever-increasing since 2003.
