TEHRAN – The principlist Popular Front of Revolution Forces is going to hold its second general meeting on Thursday, according to Lotfollah Forouzandeh, a member of the central council of the front.

 During the meeting, the group will choose five candidates for presidential election, Mehr quoted him as saying on Monday. The five will be elected among 10 who had been previously selected, he said, adding during the meeting some 3,000 members of the front will vote for their favorite candidate. SP/PA
