TEHRAN- The 23rd International Exhibition of Detergent, Cleanser, Hygienic, Cellulose Products and Related Machinery, as the largest of its kind in the Middle East, will be held at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds from April 24 to 27.  200 Iranian companies and 200 foreign ones would take part in the exhibition, the ISNA news agency reported. The participants are mainly from 14 countries comprising Turkey, Germany, China, France, Italy, Spain, the United Arab Emirates (UAE), Malaysia, Deutschland, Pakistan, Austria, India, Russia and South Korea. Detergents, cleansers, hygienic and cellulose products as well as home sanitary ware, cosmetics and aromatics will be displayed in the exhibition while related machinery and equipment, counseling and commercial services are also available.
