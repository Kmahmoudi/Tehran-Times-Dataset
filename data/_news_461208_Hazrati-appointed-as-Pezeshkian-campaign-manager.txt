TEHRAN — Reformist presidential candidate Massoud Pezeshkian has appointed Elias Hazrati, the secretary general of the National Trust party, as his campaign chief. Hazrati, a politician and journalist, served as a member of the parliament from 1989 to 2004, and from 2016 to 2020. He is currently the managing director the pro-reform Etemad newspaper.    SA/PA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
