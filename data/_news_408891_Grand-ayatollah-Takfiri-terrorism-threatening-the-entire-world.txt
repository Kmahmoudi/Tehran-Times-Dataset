TEHRAN – A senior Iranian cleric on Tuesday termed Takfiri terrorism as a great danger for the entire world. Ayatollah Nasser Makarem Shirazi made the remarks in a meeting with Chairman of Indonesia People's Consultative Assembly Zulkifli Hasan, IRNA reported.
“All of us should be vigilant and join hands against terrorist groups to foil the danger of Takfiri terrorists,” he stressed.
