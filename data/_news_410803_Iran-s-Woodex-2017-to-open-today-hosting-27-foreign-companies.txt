TEHRAN- The 8th edition of Iran’s International Exhibition of Wood Industries Machineries and Related Equipments (Woodex 2017) will start working today in Tehran, IRIB news reported. According to the official website of Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO), 85 Iranian companies and 27 foreign ones from Germany, Italy, Australia, Luxembourg, China, and Switzerland will take part in the four-day event at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds to showcase their latest achievements and products.
