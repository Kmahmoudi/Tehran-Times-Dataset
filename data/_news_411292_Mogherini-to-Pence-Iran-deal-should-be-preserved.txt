TEHRAN – Vice-President of the United States of America Mike Pence met with High Representative of the European Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy/Vice-President of the European Commission Federica Mogherini to talk about a whole range of different issues, including the Iran nuclear deal, aka the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action. As chairperson of the Joint commission, Mogherini stressed the need to preserve and fully implement the Iran deal, the European External Action Service reported on Monday. SP/PA
