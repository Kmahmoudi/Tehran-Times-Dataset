TEHRAN- Attended by more than 510 foreign exhibitors and 570 domestic participants, the 12th edition of Iran International Auto Part Exhibition kicked off in Tehran on Monday, IRIB news reported. Exhibitors from 23 foreign countries including Turkey, China, Taiwan, South Korea, India, Germany, Italy, Denmark, Belgium, Poland, the United Arab Emirates, Austria, Spain, Japan, Sweden, France, England, Argentina, Romania, Brazil, the Netherlands and Indonesia, are participating in this four-day exhibit which is held at Tehran International Permanent Fairground.
