TEHRAN – Omani Foreign Minister Yusuf bin Alawi bin Abdullah arrived in Tehran on Monday. He held a meeting with Iranian Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif, discussing regional and international developments, according to ISNA. Oman, which is known as the Switzerland of the Middle East, has often acted as an intermediary between Iran and the United States.
