EHRAN- 31st World Islamic Economic Forum (WIFE) started operation in Kuching, Malaysia, on Tuesday with participation of Iranian Minister of Communication and Information Technology Mohammad-Javad Azari Jahromi, IRNA reported. Addressing the forum, Azari Jahromi said that to prepare a roadmap for Organization of Islamic Cooperation (OIC) and Islamic Development Bank (ISDB) studies should be conducted on formation of various hubs regarding available sources and expertise. He voiced Iran’s readiness for conducting such studies. On the sidelines of WIFE, the Iranian minister held meetings with Tatarstan’s president, and some ministers from Indonesia and Philippines.  HJ/MA
