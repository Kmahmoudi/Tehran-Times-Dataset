TEHRAN – Two Iranian local workers have been killed by terrorists based in Pakistan who fired projectiles at Iran’s border region of Saravan, the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps (IRGC) said in a statement on Saturday. The statement said the terrorists were associated with foreign intelligence services. “The IRGC Ground Force at the Quds base reacted to the terrorist move, killing one of the terrorists and wounding two others,” it added.
