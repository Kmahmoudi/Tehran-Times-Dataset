TEHRAN- Production of copper anode and copper cathode in Iran rose 39 percent and 28 percent, respectively, in Iran during the two-month period from March 21 to May 21 in this year from the same time of the past year. As Shata news agency reported, some 45,274 tons of copper anode and 32,464 tons of copper cathode were produced in the two-month time of this year.
