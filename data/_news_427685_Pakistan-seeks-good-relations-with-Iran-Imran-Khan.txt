TEHRAN - Pakistani Prime Minister Imran Khan has said that his country seeks to have good relations with its neighbors, especially Iran.
 “Islamabad seeks to have good relations with its neighbors especially Iran, India and Afghanistan,” Khan said in an interview with Al Arabiya, IRNA reported on Friday. During a meeting with Iranian Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif in August, Imran Khan said that Pakistan and Iran are connected by inseparable bonds of historical, religious and cultural affinities.
