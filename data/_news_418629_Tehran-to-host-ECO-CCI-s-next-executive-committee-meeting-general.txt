TEHRAN – Iran is chosen to host 24th Executive Committee meeting and the 16th General Assembly of ECO Chamber of Commerce and Industry (CCI), the portal of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) announced.
 The decision was made during ECO CCI’s 23rd Executive Committee meeting which was hosted by the Union of Chambers and Commodity Exchanges of Turkey (TOBB) in Ankara on November 17. The event was attended by chamber of commerce officials from Iran, Turkey, Pakistan, Afghanistan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, and Azerbaijan. ICCIMA’s head Gholam-Hossein Shafeie who is also head of Iran’s committee in ECO CCI represented Iran in the organization’s 23rd Executive Committee meeting of CCI. EF/MA
