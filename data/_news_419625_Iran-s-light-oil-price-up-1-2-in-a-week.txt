TEHRAN- Iran’s light crude oil price rose $1.2 in the week ended on December 15 to settle at $63.23 per barrel, IRNA reported on Saturday. The country’s heavy crude oil price also experienced a $1.02 increase in the said week to settle at $60.49 per barrel.
