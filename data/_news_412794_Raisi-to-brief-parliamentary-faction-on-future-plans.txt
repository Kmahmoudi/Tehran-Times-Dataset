TEHRAN – Presidential hopeful Ebrahim Raisi will brief a parliamentary faction of clergies on Sunday on his future plans for the country if he becomes president.  “Hojatoleslam Ebrahim Raisi will participate in a session with members of the Majlis clergy faction,” Tasnim news agency quoted Nasrollah Pejmanfar, member of the parliamentary group, as saying.  In the session, Raisi will share his plans and ideas for the country as well as hear lawmakers’ suggestions and plans, Pejmanfar explained.
