TEHRAN -- Minister of Culture and Islamic Guidance Seyyed Abbas Salehi has appointed two female advisors in social and women’s affairs, the ministry announced on Sunday. 

 Parvin Dadandish has been appointed the minister’s advisor on social affairs, and Marzieh Meratnia was selected as the advisor for women’s affairs.  Photo: Minister of Culture and Islamic Guidance Seyyed Abbas Salehi in an undated photo.
