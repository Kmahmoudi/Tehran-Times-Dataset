TEHRAN – Opening Ceremony of the Fourth Deep Diving Tournament of World Armies was held on Sunday afternoon on Kish Island. The opening ceremony was attended by Navy Commander Rear Admiral Hossein Khanzadi, Army Coordination chief Rear Admiral Habibollah Sayyari, Kish Free Zone CEO Qolamhossein Mozaffari, and a group of Army commanders.
