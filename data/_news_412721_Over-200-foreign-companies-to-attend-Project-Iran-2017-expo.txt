TEHRAN – Over 200 companies will attend the 3rd International Trade Exhibition for Construction Materials, Equipment and Technology of Iran (Project Iran 2017) which will be held at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds from April 24 to 27. According to IRIB, companies from 23 countries are to showcase their latest products and achievements in the construction industry during the event. The exhibition seeks to connect international expertise to the Iranian market, and contribute in shaping the tremendous potential of the country’s promising construction sector. Project Iran 2017 will be solely hosting foreign companies and no domestic exhibitors will participate in the event.
