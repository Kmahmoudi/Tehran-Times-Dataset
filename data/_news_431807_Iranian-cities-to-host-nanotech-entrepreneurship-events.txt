TEHRAN -- Iran Nanotechnology Innovation Council plans to hold Karno, a nanotechnology an entrepreneurship event, at three universities in the cities of Karaj, Tehran and Kerman in February. The events are organized by Amirkabir University of Technology, Shahid Bahonar University of Kerman and Instructor Training Center and Technical and Vocational Researches of Karaj. Iran Nanotechnology Innovation Council aims to introduce nano market potentiality to young experts at the event. The event will be held from February 12 to 15 at Amirkabir University of Technology. The Shahid Bahonar University of Kerman will host the event from February 19 to 21. It will be held from February 27 to March 1 at the Instructor Training Center and Technical and Vocational Researches in Karaj. Karno event will hold workshops on business ecosystem, intellectual property, patent registration, marketing, teamwork, problem solution and meetings with successful entrepreneurs.
