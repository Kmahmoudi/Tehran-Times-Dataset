TEHRAN – Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) in collaboration with Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) is planning on sending a trade delegation to Belarus from October 10 to 13. According to an announcement on the official website of TCCIMA, the delegation will be headed by TCCIMA’s Chairman Masoud Khansari.
