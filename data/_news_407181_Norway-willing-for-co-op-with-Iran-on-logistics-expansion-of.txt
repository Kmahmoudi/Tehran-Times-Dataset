TEHRAN- The State Secretary at the Norwegian Ministry of Trade, Industry and Fisheries Dilek Ayhan in a meeting with the Deputy Head of Ports and Maritime Organization of Iran Jalil Eslami, announced her country’s willingness for improvement of logistics and marine cooperation as well as collaboration on expansion of ports with Iran. According to Shana news agency’s Saturday report, the two sides discussed making policies and setting regulations for the marine cooperation including securing safety, offering offshore services, encouraging private investors to invest in this sector and etc., and eventually signed a joint statement on future cooperation in this regard.  HJ/MA
