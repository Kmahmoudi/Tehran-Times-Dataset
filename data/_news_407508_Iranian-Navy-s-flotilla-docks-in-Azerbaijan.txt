TEHRAN – A flotilla of the Iranian Navy’s warships arrived in the Republic of Azerbaijan’s territorial waters and berthed at the port of Baku on Wednesday.

 A welcoming ceremony was held in the Azeri capital upon the arrival of the Iranian Navy’s fleet with some senior commanders of the Azerbaijani navy and some officials, Tasnim reported. The commander of the flotilla said his forces have arrived in Baku to convey Iran’s message of peace and friendship to all Caspian Sea littoral states.
