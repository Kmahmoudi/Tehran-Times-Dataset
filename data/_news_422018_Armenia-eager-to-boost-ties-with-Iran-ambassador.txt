TEHRAN – The Armenian ambassador to Tehran has hailed the growing relations between Tehran and Yerevan, saying his country views its ties with the Islamic Republic as a foreign policy priority. For nearly 27 years, Armenia, as an independent state, has had bilateral ties with Iran, Ambassador Artashes Tumanyan said, Tasnim reported on Monday.
