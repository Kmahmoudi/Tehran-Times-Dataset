TEHRAN- Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) will host a trade delegation from Vienna on May 8, the official website of TCCIMA announced. The delegation, active in oil and gas, environment, water management, tourism, food industry, construction, legal advisory services, renewable energies, and plastics, will be led by the head of Vienna Chamber of Commerce and Industry and plans to explore avenues of developing mutual ties with their Iranian counterparts.
