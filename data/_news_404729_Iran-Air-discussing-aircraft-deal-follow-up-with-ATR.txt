TEHRAN - A team of ATR aircraft manufacturer representatives are visiting Tehran to hold technical talks with Iran’s flag carrier, Iran Air, Fars news agency quoted Farhad Parvaresh, the managing director of Iran Air, as saying on Tuesday. According to Fars, Parvaresh noted that negotiations about financing, technical follow-up on the contracts and selection of the aircraft parts are among the subjects of ongoing negotiations with Airbus and ATR.  “None of the projects are stopped,” he added.
