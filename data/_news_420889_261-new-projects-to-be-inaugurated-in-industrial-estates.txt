TEHRAN – Some 261 new projects are planned to be inaugurated in industrial estates across the country on the occasion of Ten-Day Dawn (February 1-10, marking the victory anniversary of the Islamic Revolution), IRNA reported on Wednesday. According to Sadeq Najafi, the head of Iran Small Industries and Industrial Parks Organization, the mentioned projects will create job opportunities for over 9,000 people and the total investment put in to these projects is estimated at near $600 million.
