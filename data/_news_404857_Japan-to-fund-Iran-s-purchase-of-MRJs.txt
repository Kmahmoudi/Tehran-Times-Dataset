TEHRAN – The Japanese government will fund the purchase of jets by Iran from Mitsubishi Heavy Industries – known as Mitsubishi Regional Jets (MRJs), the Iran newspaper reported on Monday.  

 Asghar Fakhrieh-Kashan, the deputy transport minister, said according to the agreement Japan would fund the purchase of Mitsubishi planes based on a scheme known as build-operate-transfer (BOT). The official added Japan has agreed to provide the fund to make the purchase without any guarantees from the Iranian government. SP/PA
