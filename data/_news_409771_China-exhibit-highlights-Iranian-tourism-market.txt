TEHRAN – A select of Iranian tourism potentials, crafts, and traditions have been put on show at an international tourism fair in Haikou, the capital of Hainan Province in China. The Touring & Automobile Club of the Islamic Republic of Iran under the auspices of Cultural Heritage, Tourism, and Handcrafts Organization is tasked to run the country’s pavilion that covers 55 square meters. Representatives and tourism associates from over 20 countries are attending the tree-day festival which comes to an end today, IRNA reported on Tuesday.
