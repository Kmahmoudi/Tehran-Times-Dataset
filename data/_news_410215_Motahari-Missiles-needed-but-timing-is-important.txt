TEHRAN – Improving the country’s missile power is a must, but timing is a principle in how Iran interacts with the world, MP Ali Motahari wrote on Instagram on Wednesday.

 “Some misinterpreted a recent interview of mine as a rejection of the need for missiles… but what is important is a timely show of power,” he said, adding, “Now that we are in the first months of the JCPOA implementation, we postpone the show to some later time.” SP/PA
