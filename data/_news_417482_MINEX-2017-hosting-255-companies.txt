TEHRAN- The 6th Annual Exhibition and Conference on Investment Opportunities in Iran’s Mines and Mining Industries (MINEX 2017) kicked off in Tehran on Tuesday with participation of 255 Iranian and foreign companies, IRIB news reported. As reported the attending companies include 200 Iranian and 55 foreign ones from 15 countries such as Germany, France, China, Australia, Denmark, Sweden, South Africa, Turkey, Russia, Poland, the Netherlands, and Italy.  The event, which will wrap up on Thursday, presents the latest technologies and equipment, services and machineries in mining industry.
