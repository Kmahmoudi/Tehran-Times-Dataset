TEHRAN – President Hassan Rouhani on Sunday submitted the membership act of Iran in the Association of Tax Authorities of Islamic Countries to the Ministry of Finance and Economic Affairs. According to the order, the membership is in accordance with the implementation of Article 123 of the Constitution of the Islamic Republic, Mehr news agency reported. The single article of the same article allows the government to join the Association of Tax Authorities of Islamic Countries according to the attached statute and acts to pay the membership fee.
