TEHRAN- In a meeting with the senior officials on Saturday, Iranian President Hassan Rouhani called on Oil Minister Bijan Namdar Zanganeh to expedite the trend of gas supplying to the country’s power plants.  Following the president’s remarks, Saeid Mo’meni, the director for gas supplying in the National Iranian Gas Company (NIGC), said there are 84 power plants throughout the country, of which 81 power plants have received gas and supplying gas to the remaining three power plants will be expedited, Shana news agency reported.
