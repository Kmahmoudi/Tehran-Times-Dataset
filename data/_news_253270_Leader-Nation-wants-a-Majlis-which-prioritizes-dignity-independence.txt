TEHRAN – Leader of the Islamic Revolution said on Wednesday that the Iranian people want parliamentarians who will be “religious”, “committed”, “brave”, “resistant against the hegemonic system’s excessive demands”, defenders of the country’s “dignity and national independence”, “interested in the country’s progress”, determined to solve the people’s problems”, and finally in favor of a “generative economy.”
