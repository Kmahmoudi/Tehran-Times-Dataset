TEHRAN – Iran exported $41.69 billion of non-oil goods in the first 11 months of the current Iranian calendar year ending to February 19. The exports rose by 4.96 percent year on year, according to data released by the Islamic Republic of Iran Customs Administration. Exports to China, South Korea, and Turkey rose by 11.29 percent, 41.08 percent, and 30.71 percent respectively, while exports to the United Arab Emirates fell by 14.09 percent, the report added.
