TEHRAN- Companies from 12 countries are attending the fifth edition of Iran intl. aluminum conference and exhibition (IAC 2018) which kicked off in Tehran on Tuesday, IRIB news reported. Companies from countries including India, China, Turkey, Greece, Germany, Italy and Switzerland besides 60 Iranian companies are showcasing their products and achievements in the two-day exhibit which is held on the sidelines of a three-day conference.
