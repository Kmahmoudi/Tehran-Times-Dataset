TEHRAN- The value of exported goods from Iran’s southern port of Shahid Rajaie grew 44 percent in the past Iranian calendar year 1395 (ended March 20, 2017), compared to the preceding year, IRNA reported. As Alireza Dashtani, an official in Hormozgan Province Customs Administration, told reporters on Saturday, during the said time, 44 million tons of various goods, at the value of $11.141 billion, were exported from Shahid Rajaie.
