TEHRAN – The Iranian Judiciary chief has criticized the misuse of Islam, saying, “The Takfiri and Salafi interpretations of Islam have disgusted the world.”

 Speaking to high-ranking Judiciary officials on Monday, Ayatollah Sadeq Amoli Larijani said, “That interpretation of Islam which nurtures terrorists will find fratricide to be its only outcome.”  “The Islamic world at this era needs a real and documented interpretation and explanation of Islam which is founded on divine sources,” Amoli Larijani stated, according to Mehr.
