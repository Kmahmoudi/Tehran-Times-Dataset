TEHRAN- IFX, the main index of Iran's over-the-counter (OTC) market known also as Iran Fara Bourse (IFB), rose 1.2 percent to stand at 1,070 points in the week ended on Friday, Tasnim news agency reported. As reported, 1.283 billion securities worth 9.274 trillion rials (about $220.8 million) were traded in 150,000 deals during the mentioned week. Base market and first market experienced the highest growth in the number of deals and first market and second market had the highest increase in terms of the value of deals.
