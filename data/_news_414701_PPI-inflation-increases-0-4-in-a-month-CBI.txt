TEHRAN- The average Producer Price Index (PPI) in the third month of the current Iranian calendar year, Khordad (May 22-June 21, 2017), witnessed a 0.4 percent rise and reached 237.2 compared to its preceding month, IRNA reported on Saturday. According to the latest report of the Central Bank of Iran (CBI), year-on-year PPI inflation stood at 8.7 percent in Khordad.
The figure, in the 12-month period ending June 21, grew 6.5 percent, the same report confirmed. HJ/MA
