In northern Mazandaran Province, neighbors and relatives of bride and groom hold Surtan ritual the night before wedding ceremony.
 They provide some fruits, confectionaries as well as some groceries and dairies and put them on big decorated trays. They place a volume of the Holy Quran, some candles and flowers in one of the trays, wishing a fortunate life for the couple.
