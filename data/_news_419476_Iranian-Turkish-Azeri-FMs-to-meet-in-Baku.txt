TEHRAN – Foreign ministers of Iran, Turkey and Azerbaijan will discuss regional issues during a trilateral meeting which will be held in Baku on December 20, the Turkish Foreign Ministry said on Monday. The ministry told Trend news agency that the meeting will make a great contribution to the process of ensuring peace and stability in the region. “During the trilateral meeting, the sides will discuss the decisions made at previous meetings,” it said. The ministry added that Turkish Foreign Minister Mevlut Cavusoglu will have bilateral meetings with his Azeri and Iranian counterparts.
