TEHRAN – Loyalists to presidential candidate Ebrahim Raisi will hold their first gathering on Saturday in Tehran.

 To be held in the afternoon, the gathering will include a speech by Raisi himself, Fars reported on Wednesday. The gathering will be held at Seven Tir Martyrs Hall in Fayyazbakhsh St. and will start at 17:00. This week Raisi visited South Khorasan, Sistan-Baluchestan, and Yazd provinces. SP/PA
