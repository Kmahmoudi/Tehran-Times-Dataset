TEHRAN – International Atomic Energy Agency Director General Yukiya Amano has launched a strong defense of the Iran nuclear deal, saying it provided a peaceful mechanism to settle the issue.  “Agreement on the JCPOA (acronym for the deal) changed the course on Iran from confrontation to cooperation,” he said at a New Year reception speech on Jan. 26.  Ever Since January 2016, when the deal was implemented, the Agency has been verifying and monitoring Iran’s implementation of its nuclear-related commitments under the deal, he further explained.  AK/PA  All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
