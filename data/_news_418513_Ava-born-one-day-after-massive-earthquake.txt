A mother is taking care of her baby who was born just one day after a massive earthquake struck the city of Sarpol-e Zahab in the western province of Kermanshah. The girl, named Ava, was born in a field hospital established in the city by the Army. Physicians and nurses rushed to the help of the mother after she went into labor.  The birth of the baby created a feeling of joy amid a sense of tragedy and grief that has gripped the city after the devastating earthquake. Tehran Times/ Mohammad Khodabakhsh
