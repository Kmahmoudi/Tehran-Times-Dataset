TERHAN- The 8th edition of International Exhibition of Architecture, Interior Design, and Modern House (Midex 2018) will start operation on Thursday with participation of 210 Iranian and foreign companies, IRIB news reported.  Some 193 Iranian exhibitors besides companies from Italy, Germany, China, France, and Turkey will showcase their latest industry, equipment, products, and services in this event. The four-day event will be held at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds. HJ/MA
