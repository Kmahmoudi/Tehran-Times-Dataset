TEHRAN- According to the latest report of the Statistical Center of Iran (SCI), unemployment rate in spring which corresponds to the first quarter of current Iranian calendar year (March 21-June 21) stood at 12.1 percent, increasing 0.5 percent in comparison with the spring of last year. The center put the number of the unemployed at 3.322 million persons in spring of this year, IRIB reported on Tuesday.
