TEHRAN – The 22nd Exhibition of Press and News Agencies, Iran’s most significant media showcase, will be held at Tehran’s Imam Khomeni Mosalla from November 4 to 11, the organizers announced on Tuesday.
 
Over 800 Iranian media, including newspapers, magazines, news agencies and press organizations, will set up stands at the exhibition, which will also organized about 200 meetings on the sidelines. In addition, representatives of several international media are scheduled to attend the exhibit to introduce their national media.
