TEHRAN- Iran will hold its exclusive trade and industrial exhibition from February 12 to 15 in Baghdad, Iran’s commercial attaché to Iraq announced, IRNA reported on Friday. According to Naser Behzad, the exhibition will be held by cooperation among Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO), the country’s embassy in Iraq, and Iranian entrepreneurs at the place of Baghdad International Fairgrounds. 
 
Some 100 Iranian companies are expected to introduce their latest products and services at this exhibition, he added. HJ/MA
