TEHRAN – The European Union will make sure that the Iran nuclear deal “will continue to be fully implemented by all, in all its parts”, the EU’s foreign policy chief Federica Mogherini said on Friday. Speaking at a conference in Samarkand, Uzbekistan, Mogherini said the 2015 nuclear agreement was “a major achievement of European and international multilateral diplomacy that is delivering”. The nuclear deal, also known as the JCPOA, was signed between Iran and six world powers on July 14, 2015. The JCPOA went into effect on January 16, 2016.
