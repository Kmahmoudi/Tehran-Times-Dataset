A funeral procession was held on Monday afternoon for Milad Heidrai, an IRGC military advisor who was martyred in an Israeli air strike on Syria on Friday.  Heidari was laid to rest in the cemetery of the Khordad 15 Martyrs in Qarchak county about 30 km southeast of Tehran. A large number of people, military and local officials attended the ceremony.
