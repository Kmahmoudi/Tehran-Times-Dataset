TEHRAN- Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mine and Agriculture (ICCIMA) delegation and Iranian Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif are scheduled to pay visits to Senegal, Brazil, and Uruguay from April 9 to 12, the portal of ICCIMA reported. The delegation is planned to attend some business forums and will hold talks and mutual meetings with their counterparts in the destination countries. Discussion subjects, as announced, are agriculture and foodstuff, technical engineering services, auto parts industry, mining, oil and gas, petrochemicals, medicine, and medical equipment.
