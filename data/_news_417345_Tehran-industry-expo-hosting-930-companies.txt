TEHRAN – The 17th edition of Tehran International Industry Exhibition kicked off at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds on Friday, IRIB news reported.
 Some 930 domestic and foreign exhibitors from different countries including Germany, Italy, Czech Republic, Sweden, South Korea, Romania, Bulgaria, the Netherlands, France, Spain, China, Japan and Britain are participating in this year’s exhibition. The four-day exhibition features various areas including industrial tools and equipment, large and small industries production lines, industrial automation and technical and engineering services.
