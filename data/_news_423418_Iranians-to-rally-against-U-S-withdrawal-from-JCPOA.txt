TEHRAN – Iranians will take to the streets across the country this week to condemn U.S. President Donald Trump’s decision to withdraw from the 2015 nuclear agreement, Fars reported. The protests will be staged after this week’s Friday prayers. Demonstrators will shout anti-American slogans. Trump’s decision prompted widespread condemnation from world leaders.
