TEHRAN — In a helicopter crash in Persian Gulf waters in southern Iran on Tuesday night two were killed and two went missing, IRNA news agency reported. A search operation is underway to find the two missing people. The rescue helicopter, operated by Iranian Offshore Oil Company (IOOC), crashed while transferring an oil worker who had suffered a heart attack.  According to the report, the patient and the paramedic are dead while the pilot has survived.  MQ/PA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
