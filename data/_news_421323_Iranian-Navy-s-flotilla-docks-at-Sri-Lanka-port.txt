TEHRAN – The Iranian Navy’s 50th flotilla of warships berthed at Sri Lanka’s port of Colombo on Friday morning, the Navy’s public relations office said, Tasnim reported. The operational-training naval fleet, comprising Shahid Naghdi and Bayandor destroyers and Tonb logistic-combat warship, has travelled to Sri Lanka to convey the message of peace and friendship between the two countries. During their stay in Colombo, the commanders of the flotilla are planned to meet with senior Sri Lankan military officials. There will also be visits to a number of military centers as well as cultural and historical sites. MH/PA
