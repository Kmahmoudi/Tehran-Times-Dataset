TEHRAN – Iran’s Army has praised progress in defense industry as a potent symbol of self-sufficiency and self-reliance. In a statement issued on the occasion of National Day of Defense Industry on Monday, the Army said the country’s advances in various military fields have turned the defense sector into a symbol of self-sufficiency and materialization of the purposes of the resistance economy. It also emphasized that the Army is prepared to combat any potential or practical threat posed by the enemies. SP/PA
