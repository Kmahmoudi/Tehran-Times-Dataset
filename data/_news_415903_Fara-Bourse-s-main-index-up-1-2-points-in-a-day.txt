TEHRAN- IFX, the main index of Iran's over-the-counter (OTC) market known also as Iran Fara Bourse (IFB), rose 1.2 points to 920 on Sunday, Tasnim news agency reported.
 As reported, 210 million securities worth 940 billion rials (about $24.8 million) were traded in 28,000 deals on Sunday. Also, Tehran Stock Exchange (TSE)’s main index (TEDPIX) fell 101 points to 81,661 in the same day. Some 465 million shares worth 1.14 trillion rials (about $206.9 million) were traded through 42,000 deals in TSE.
