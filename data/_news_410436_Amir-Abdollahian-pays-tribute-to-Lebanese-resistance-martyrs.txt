TEHRAN – Hossein Amir Abdollahian, a senior foreign policy advisor to the parliament speaker, paid tribute to Imad Mughniyah and other resistance martyrs at their graves in Lebanon’s Dahieh, southern Beirut. Heading a delegation, Amir-Abdollahian arrived in Beirut on Monday to hold talks with the Lebanese officials on key regional and international issues. He also met with the wife of Samir Kuntar – another martyr who was killed in Syria – at the Iranian embassy in Beirut. Amir Abdollahian commemorated Kuntar as a martyr of resistance who fought the Zionist regime and defended the holy shrines in Syria. MH/PA
