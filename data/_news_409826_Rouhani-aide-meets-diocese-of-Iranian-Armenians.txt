TEHRAN – Elham Aminzadeh, the presidential aide for citizenship rights, met on Thursday with the diocese of Iranian Armenians.

 Aminzadeh’s meeting with Bishop Sebo-Serkisian 
was held in Saint Sarkis Cathedral in Tehran on the occasion of the New Year and the birthday of Jesus Christ, ISNA reported. Congratulating Sebo-Serkisian on the Christian occasions, Aminzadeh presented a copy of the recently drafted Citizenship Rights Charter to Sarkisian. She also laid wreaths on the graves of Armenians who died while defending the country against the invading Saddam army in the 1980s. SP/PA
