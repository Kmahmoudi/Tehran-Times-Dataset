TEHRAN – Iran is in talks with Belgium on using the European country’s Antwerp port for exporting petrochemical products, IRNA reported. Iran’s National Petrochemical Company (NPC) Managing Director Marziyeh Shahdaie met with Frank Geerkens, the port ambassador at Antwerp Port authority in Belgium, to discuss the subject. Cooperation on HSE and firefighting areas were also discussed in the meeting. EF/MA
