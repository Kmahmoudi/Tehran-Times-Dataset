TEHRAN – The Iranian Air Force has successfully overhauled Mirage fighter’s on-board oxygen generation system (OBOGS) which supplies oxygen to the flight crew.

 The repair was carried out by experts of the overhaul center of the Islamic Republic of Iran Air Force (IRIAF) for the first time, the official website of the IRIAF reported on Saturday. Mirage is a French air-superiority fighter and attack aircraft designed and built by Dassault Aviation. Iran reportedly has at least 12 operational Mirage fighters. SP/PA
