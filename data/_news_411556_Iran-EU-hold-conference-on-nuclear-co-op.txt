TEHRAN – The EU and Iran started a high-level seminar on “international Nuclear Cooperation: Expectations and Responsibilities” in Brussels on Tuesday. The conference will end on Wednesday.

 The seminar is the result of a joint initiative of Commissioner Miguel Arias Cañete and Iranian Vice President and Head of the Atomic Energy Organization of Iran Ali Akbar Salehi, the AEOI reported. The seminar represents an important element of the growing cooperation between Iran and the EU, following implementation of the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA), the official name for the nuclear deal.
