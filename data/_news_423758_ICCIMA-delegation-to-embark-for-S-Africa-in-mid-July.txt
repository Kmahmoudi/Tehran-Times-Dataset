TEHRAN- Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture’s (ICCIMA) delegation, led by the chamber’s head Gholam-Hossein Shafe’i, will leave Tehran for South Africa on July 15, the portal of ICCIMA reported. The delegation, comprised of Iranian entrepreneurs active in various sectors including mining, foodstuff, technical and engineering services, plastics, petrochemicals, food industry, medicine, construction materials, medical tourism, auto industry, carpet and etc., will end its trip on July 18.
