TEHRAN - Qatari referee Abdulrahman Al Jassim has been chosen to officiate the match between Lebanon and Iran in the 2022 World Cup qualification. Al Jassem will be assisted by his countrymen Taleb Al Marri and Saoud Ahmed Al Maqaleh. Saoud Al Adba is also fourth official.  The ‘Persian Leopards’ will play Lebanon at the Saida Municipal Stadium in Sidon, Lebanon on Thursday. Iran sit top of Group A, five points ahead of third-placed Lebanon.
