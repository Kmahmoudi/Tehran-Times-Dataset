TEHRAN- Iran exported $190 million worth of home appliances during the 10-month period ended on January 20, according to an official in Trade Promotion Organization (TPO).
 Masoud Kamali, director general of the exports development office of TPO, said while the figure shows three-percent fall from the same time span of the previous year, we had also exports to the European markets in this year”, ISNA reported on Saturday.
