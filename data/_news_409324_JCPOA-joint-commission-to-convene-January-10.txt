TEHRAN – Iran’s foreign minister has told the National Security and Foreign Policy Committee of the Majlis that the JCPOA joint commission meeting will be held on January 10.

 Committee spokesman Hossein Naqavi Hosseini said on Monday evening that Zarif had briefed the MPs on the recent developments regarding the nuclear deal between Tehran and world powers, IRNA reported. Zarif had also said the call for the JCPOA meeting comes along with the presidential orders to take action against the extension of the Iran Sanctions Act for another ten years. SP/PA
