TEHRAN- Leader of the Islamic Revolution, Ayatollah Seyed Ali Khamenei, attended for the first night of the grieving ceremonies for Hazrat Zahra (SA). A myriad of high-profile officials and citizens joined the event marking the anniversary of the martyrdom of Hazrat Zahra, the daughter of the Prophet Mohammad (PBUH).
