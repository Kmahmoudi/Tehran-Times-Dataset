TEHRAN – Tehran is hosting the second edition of Iran’s international exhibition of Printing, Processing and Packaging (IPAP 2018), IRIB reported. Running from October 11 to 14 at Tehran’s Shahre Aftab International Exhibition Center, the event is hosting 130 domestic and foreign exhibitors from different countries including Italy, Germany, India, Turkey and Pakistan. Visitors and investors from Georgia, Qatar, Libya, the UAE, Iraq, Russia, Afghanistan, India and Pakistan are also present in the expo. The exhibition is aiming to bring together the people active in this industry and to present the country’s capabilities in this sector in order to facilitate business growth and to establish high quality packaging, and increasing non-oil exports.
