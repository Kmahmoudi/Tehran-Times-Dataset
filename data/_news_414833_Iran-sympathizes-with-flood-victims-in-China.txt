TEHRAN – Bahram Qassemi, the spokesman for Iran’s Foreign Ministry, extended condolences on Wednesday to the Chinese government and families who lost loved ones in a flood in the country.

 Hail, landfalls and flooding have led to killing of 56 people and missing of 22 in 11 provinces over the past week. SP/PA
