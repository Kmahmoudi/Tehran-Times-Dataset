TEHRAN- Iran’s Industrial Development and Renovation Organization (IDRO) plans to implement some 54 projects using 660 trillion rials (about $14.73 billion) of investments by March 20, 2022, the organization’s Head Mansour Moazzami announced on Saturday. As IRNA quoted him as saying, implementation of these projects, which are aimed at supporting production and job creation in the country’s deprived areas, will create jobs for about 29,000 people within the next four years.  HJ/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
