TEHRAN – President Hassan Rouhani said on Tuesday that full implementation of the nuclear deal, reached between Iran and world powers in 2015, is an important test for the development of cooperation in other areas. President Rouhani made the remarks during a phone conversation with French President Emmanuel Macron, IRNA reported. He also said Iran is not seeking to “dominate” the Middle East, stressing that the Islamic Republic, instead, is trying to help restore peace and security to the region.
