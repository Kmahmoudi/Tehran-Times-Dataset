TEHRAN —The 11th exhibition on women and national production opened in Tehran’s Goftegoo Park on Wednesday with Tehran’s Mayor Mohammd Ali Najafi in attendance. Showcasing the domestically produced clothing items and accessories for men, women, and children all manufactured by Iranian female breadwinners the exhibition is coordinated by Tehran Municipality.    The event aims at supporting domestic producers as well as supplying products at competitive prices and better qualities produced by more than 3,000 breadwinner women.   Over the one-week exhibition has prepared a suitable ground for women who are responsible for their families either because they lost their spouses or their spouses are not capable of working due to illness.
