TEHRAN- Production of copper cathode and anode in Iran rose 50 percent and 56 percent respectively in spring (March 21- June 22, 2018), in comparison with the same season in the preceding year, IRNA reported.  Giant copper cathode production units in Iran could produce 53,233 tons of the product in the said time this year while their production of anode stood at 70,871 tons, the report said.
