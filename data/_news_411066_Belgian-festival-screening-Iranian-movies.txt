TEHRAN – Two Iranian films are on screen at the 33rd Mons International Love Film Festival, which is underway in the Belgian city.

 “Daughter” by Reza Mirkarimi is competing in the official section while “Apricot Groves”, an Iranian-Armenian film production by Iranian director Purya Heidari-Ureh, is scheduled to go on screen in the European Competition section of the festival, which will run until February 17.
