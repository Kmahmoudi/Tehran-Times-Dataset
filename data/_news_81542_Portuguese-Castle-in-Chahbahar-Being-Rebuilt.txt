The director of the Chahbahr Cultural Heritage Office, Mohsen Nazari, said that Tis Castle was surveyed last year to determine its boundaries and it is being renovated this year. Some ancient artifacts such as golden coins and women's jewelry from the Sassanid era were discovered in the area of the castle and are now being kept in the Zahedan Museum.
