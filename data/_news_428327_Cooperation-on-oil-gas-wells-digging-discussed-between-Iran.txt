TEHRAN- Iran and Oman explored the ways to cooperate on the digging operation of oil and gas wells, Shana reported on Monday. In a meeting between Iran’s North Drilling Company’s Managing Director Hamidreza Golpayegani and   Ali Abdullah Al-Riyami, the director general of oil and gas marketing at Oman’s Ministry of Oil and Gas, on Tehran on Sunday, the two sides stressed the exports of technical-engineering services and also Omani companies’ investment making in the upstream projects in Iran.
