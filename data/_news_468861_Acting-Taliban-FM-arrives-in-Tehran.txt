TEHRAN — Acting Taliban Foreign Minister Mulavi Amir Khan Muttaqi arrived in Tehran late on Saturday. 
 A Taliban Foreign Ministry spokesman said during the visit, Amir Khan Muttaqi will discuss political, economic, transit, and Afghan refugees in Iran with officials in Tehran.
