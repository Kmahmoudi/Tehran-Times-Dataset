TEHRAN- A delegation from Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) will visit the Netherlands from January 28 to February 1, 2018, the portal of ICCIMA reported. The delegation, to be headed by ICCIMA Chairman Gholam-Hossein Shafeie, includes Iranian entrepreneurs active in agriculture, green energies, irrigation and related industries, healthcare, logistics, transportation, oil, gas, and petrochemicals. The visit will be paid with cooperation of Iran-Netherlands Joint Chamber of Commerce, the report said.
