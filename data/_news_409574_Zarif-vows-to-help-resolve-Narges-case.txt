TEHRAN – Iranian Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif has said he will make efforts to help relieve Narges Kalbasi from her predicaments.

 “I assure you that our objective is her freedom from her current problems and a fair treatment,” Mohammad Javad Zarif wrote on Wednesday in reply to a letter by some celebrities, professors, and lawyers, Mehr reported. In his trip to India a few weeks ago, Zarif met with Narges to discuss her case. Narges, a philanthropist, has been sentenced to a year in prison for unintentional murder.
