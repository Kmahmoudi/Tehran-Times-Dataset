TEHRAN- Three major aluminum ingot producers in Iran produced 244,577 tons of ingots during the first ten months of the current Iranian calendar year (March 20, 2016- January 19, 2017), IRNA reported.  The figure shows a one-percent decrease compared to the same period last year, according to a report released by Iranian Mines and Mining Industries Development and Renovation Organization (IMIDRO).
