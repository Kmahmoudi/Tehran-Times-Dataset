According to Iran's deputy foreign minister Ali Baqeri Kani, Iran will return to the JCPOA revival negotiations in Vienna on November 29. In a phone call with @enriquemora_ , we agreed to start the negotiations aiming at removal of unlawful & inhumane sanctions on 29 November in Vienna. Baqeri tweeted on late Wednesday, "In a phone call with @enriquemora_, we agreed to start the negotiations aiming at removal of unlawful & inhumane sanctions on 29 November in Vienna."
