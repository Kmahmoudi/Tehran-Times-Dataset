TEHRAN – the 10th edition of the exhibition of women and national production opened at Tehran’s Goftegoo Park on Friday. The exhibition space includes 74 booths, special for women, men, and kids. Handicraft products are being sold on the sidelines of the exhibition. The event will come to an end on March 13.
