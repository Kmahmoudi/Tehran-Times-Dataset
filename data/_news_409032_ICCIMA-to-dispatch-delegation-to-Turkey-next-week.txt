TEHRAN- Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) intends to dispatch a team of its members and high ranking officials to Istanbul next week, the chamber’s website announced on Sunday. The visit will take place on December 20 and the Iranian Vice President Es’haq Jahangiri will lead the delegation to Turkey.
