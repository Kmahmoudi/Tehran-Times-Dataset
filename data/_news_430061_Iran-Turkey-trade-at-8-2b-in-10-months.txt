TEHRAN- According to the latest data released by Turkish Statistical Institute, the trade between Iran and Turkey in the first 10 months of 2018 stood at $8.252 billion, Tasnim news agency reported. The figure shows a 10-percent drop in comparison with the same time in the year before when the volume stood at $9.1 billion, the report added. From January to October 2017, Turkey exported $2.6 billion of goods to Iran, the figure registering 22 percent drop reached $2.02 billion in the same period in 2018.
