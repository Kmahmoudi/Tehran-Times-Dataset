TEHRAN – A sabotage attack on one of the buildings owned by the Atomic Energy Organization of Iran (AEOI) near Karaj was foiled on Wednesday morning, security forces announced. Karaj is 40 kilometers west of the capital Tehran.   The security forces said the sabotage attack left no damage, the IRIB news agency reported.
