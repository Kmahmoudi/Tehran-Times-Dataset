TEHRAN – President Hassan Rouhani is scheduled to visit Istanbul on Tuesday to attend an emergency summit of the Organization of the Islamic Cooperation on al-Quds (Jerusalem). Parviz Esmaeili, the presidential office’s director for communications and information, made the announcement on Sunday, Mehr reported. The OIC will hold meetings at level of heads of state and ministers in Istanbul on Wednesday to discuss the repercussions of the recognition of al-Quds as the capital of the Israeli regime by the United States.
