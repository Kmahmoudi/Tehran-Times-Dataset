TEHRAN – The eighth edition of Iran’s International Exhibition of Accessories & Furniture Machinery, Equipment & Related Industries (Woodex 2017) is scheduled for February 6-9, 2017, the official website of Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) announced. According to the event’s organizer, European and Asian companies from 13 countries including Germany, Italy, Spain, France, Finland, China, Turkey, Russia, Switzerland, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Hong Kong and Taiwan will showcase their latest products and services in the four-day event.
Woodex 2017, the most specialized exhibition of wood industry in Iran, will kick off at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds. EF/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
