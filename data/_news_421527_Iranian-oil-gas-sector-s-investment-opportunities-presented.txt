TEHRAN- Investment potentials in Iran’s oil and gas sector was presented in the International Petroleum (IP) Week 2018, which was held in London from February 20 to 22. Mohammad Ali Emadi, senior advisor to the deputy for development and engineering in National Iranian Oil Company (NIOC), was Iran’s representative who overviewed his country’s oil and gas sector in the conference. IP Week hosted by the Energy Institute (EI) is a renowned and highly respected event where leaders from the global oil and gas industry share their wealth of knowledge and experience with fellow experts over three days of conferences, seminars, roundtables and social events.
