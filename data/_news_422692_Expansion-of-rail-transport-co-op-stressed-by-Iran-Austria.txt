TEHRAN – A 10-member Austrian trade delegation visited Tran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) on Sunday to discuss expansion of cooperation between the two countries in rail transport, TCCIMA portal reported. In a meeting with Mohammad Reza Bakhtiari, TCCIMA’s deputy for international affairs, the two sides called for mutual cooperation in different areas of rail industry services including innovative prefabricated concrete components used in platforms and buildings, insulation systems and electronic components, production and maintenance of rolling machines and disaster risk management.
