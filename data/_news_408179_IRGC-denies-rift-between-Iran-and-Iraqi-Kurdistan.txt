TEHRAN – A spokesman for the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps (IRGC) on Wednesday dismissed reports of disagreement between Iran and the semi-autonomous Kurdistan region of Iraq, saying the Kurdish friends will never allow any hostile action against Iran’s national security.

 Speaking to the Tasnim on Wednesday, IRGC Public Relations chief General Ramezan Sharif quashed media reports about tense ties between Tehran and Erbil following the recent comments by a senior Iranian military official. SP/PA
