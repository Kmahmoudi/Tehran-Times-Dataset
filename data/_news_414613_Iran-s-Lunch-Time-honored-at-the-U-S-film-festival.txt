TEHRAN – Iranian director Alireza Qasemi’s “Lunch Time” won the best student short film award at the 6th edition of the Snake Alley Festival of Film, an international festival of short movies, which was held in Burlington, Iowa, in the U.S. from June 21 to 24. “Lunch Time” tells the story of a 16-year-old girl, who deals with the harsh bureaucracy to fulfill her responsibility of having to identify the body of her mother.
