TEHRAN – The chairwoman of the Islamic Republic of Iran's Documents and National Library Organization has called for expanding cultural cooperation between Iran and Bulgaria, IRNA reported.

 "Cultural cooperation will lead to exchange of opinions among scientists and researchers," Ashraf Boroujerdi said in a meeting with cultural and foreign policy advisors of Bulgarian president on Wednesday. "Collaboration can present the true image of both countries' cultures to their people,” she added. SP/PA
