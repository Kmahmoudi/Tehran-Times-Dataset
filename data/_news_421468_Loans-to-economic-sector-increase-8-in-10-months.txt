TEHRAN- Iranian banks gave 6.42 quadrillion rials (about $102.3 billion) of facilities to domestic economic sector during the 10 months ending to January 20, 2018, which shows eight percent growth in comparison with the same time span in the previous year, ILNA reported on Wednesday. In the said period, Iranian banks could manage to offer about 340 trillion rials (about $7.5 billion) of facilities more than that of the preceding year to domestic economic sector, according to the latest report of the Central Bank of Iran (CBI).
