TEHRAN- In a meeting with Kazakhstan’s deputy prime minister, head of Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO)underscored the vitality of developing all-out ties with Eurasian Economic Commission (EEC) as his organization's top priority. Mojtaba Khosrotaj, who led a delegation comprised of officials from Iran’s ministries of transportation and agriculture, and TPO to Kazakhstan to attend EXPO 2017, also visited EEC trade minister and the Kazakh deputy agriculture minister. During these meetings the officials discussed trade of agricultural products as well as easing their transit and swap.
