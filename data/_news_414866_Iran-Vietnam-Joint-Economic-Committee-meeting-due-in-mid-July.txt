TEHRAN – The ninth round of Iran-Vietnam Joint Economic Committee meeting is due to be held in Tehran on July 15 and 16, the official website of Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO) reported. In this regard TPO officials and representatives of concerned organizations and institutes held a meeting this week to discuss preparations for the upcoming committee meeting. EF/MA
