TEHRAN- The 10th International Flour and Bakery Industry Exhibition of Iran (ibex 2016) will be inaugurated at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds form December 6 to 9, the portal of Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO) reported. As reported, 115 Iranian companies as well as 59 foreign ones from Italy, Germany, France, Turkey, Belgium, Austria, Spain, Switzerland, the Netherlands, Russia, Czech Republic, South Korea, Lebanon, and Poland will participate in the event.
