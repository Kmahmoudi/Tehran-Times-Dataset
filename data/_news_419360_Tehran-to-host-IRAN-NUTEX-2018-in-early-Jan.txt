TEHRAN- The 5th edition of International Exhibition on Nuts, Dried Fruit & related industries (IRAN NUTEX 2018) is to be held at Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds from January 3 to 6, 2018, IRIB news reported. The three-day event, which is to be held with cooperation of Iran Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) and some other Iranian private and state-run entities, seeks increase in Iran’s production of nuts and dried fruits as well as their exports to the world. Tens of Iranian production and processing units are reportedly to show case their latest products and modern machineries in this exhibition.  As reported, 110 Iranian companies took part in IRAN NUTEX 2017, while business delegations from 28 countries visited the event.
