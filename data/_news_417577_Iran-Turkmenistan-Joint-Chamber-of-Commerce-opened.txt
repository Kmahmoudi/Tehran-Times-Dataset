TEHRAN- Iran-Turkmenistan Joint Chamber of Commerce was officially opened in Tehran on October 11, the portal of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) reported on Saturday.  According to the report, Mohammad-Reza Karbasi, the ICCIMA deputy head for international affairs, and Behrouz Olfat, director of Europe and America Department at the Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) of Iran, attended the opening ceremony.
