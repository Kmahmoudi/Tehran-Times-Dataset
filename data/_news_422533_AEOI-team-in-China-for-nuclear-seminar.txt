TEHRAN – An expert delegation from the Atomic Energy Organization of Iran (AEOI) was scheduled to set off to China on Tuesday night to attend a nuclear seminar on Wednesday, the AEOI spokesman said. Behrooz Kamalvandi said on Tuesday that the seminar focuses on Iran-China’s international cooperation on peaceful uses of nuclear energy, Mehr reported. The seminar follow previous gatherings at Brussels, Moscow and Isfahan. Kamalvandi added delegates from the 5+1 countries will participate in the seminar to discuss different issues, including the outlook of nuclear international cooperation within the framework of the JCPOA and the related Annex 3 of the deal.  MH/PA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
