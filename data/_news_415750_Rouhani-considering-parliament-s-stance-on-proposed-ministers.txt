TEHRAN – President Hassan Rouhani is considering the “taste of the Majlis” in proposing his ministers, Rouhani’s deputy for parliamentary affairs said on Monday.

 “Mr. President will also consider the interests of the system and his own plans to lead the country on the path to progress as he introduces the 12th cabinet to the Majlis,” Hossein Ali Amiri said, ILNA reported. Sources have said Rouhani plans to propose his future cabinet to the Majlis on Tuesday.
