TEHRAN – An MP has said Iran should call on the 5+1 group to convene an emergency meeting to raise its concerns over the violation of the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA), ILNA reported on Saturday.

 Heshmatollah Falahatpishe, who sits on the Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee, said there is no powerful apparatus in the JCPOA to guarantee the behavior of Western countries, the U.S. in particular, regarding their commitments based on the agreement.
