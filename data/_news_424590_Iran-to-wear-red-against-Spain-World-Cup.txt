KAZAN, Russia – Iran national football team, known as Team Melli, will wear red kit in the match against Spain in the 2018 World Cup in Russia. Iran will face Fernando Hierro’s team on Wednesday at the Kazan Arena in Kazan. Spain will wear white kit. Iran defeated Morocco 1-0 in their Group B opener with their white kits.
