TEHRAN – The head of Iran’s Judiciary has said fighting terrorism would be possible only through the cooperation of all regional countries.

 Sadeq Amoli Larijani made the remarks on Saturday before departing for Iraq for talks on regional terrorism, Fars reported. He said he will meet with his Iraqi counterpart, prime minister, and parliament speaker. Amoli Larijani is accompanied in his trip by Deputy Foreign Minister for African and Arab Affairs Hossein Jaberi Ansari and other officials. SP/PA
