TEHRAN- The 5th edition of International Exhibition on Nuts, Dried Fruit & related industries (IRAN NUTEX 2018) was inaugurated at Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds on Wednesday, IRIB news reported. The four-day event, which is held with cooperation of Iran Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) and some other Iranian private and state-run entities, seeks increase in Iran’s production of nuts and dried fruits as well as their exports to the world. Tens of Iranian production and processing units are showcasing their latest products and modern machineries in this exhibition.  As previously reported, 110 Iranian companies are taking part in IRAN NUTEX 2017, while business delegations from 28 countries will visit the event.
