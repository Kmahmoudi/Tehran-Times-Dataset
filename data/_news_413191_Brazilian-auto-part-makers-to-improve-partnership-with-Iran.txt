TEHRAN- The organizer of 4th Iran Automotive Industry International Conference (IAIIC) Aram Razzaqi informed of a new round of cooperation between Iranian and Brazilian auto part manufacturers, IRIB news reported on Saturday. He announced that a Brazilian delegation comprised of representatives from 10 major auto part manufacturers of the country will be present at the Industrial Development and Renovation Organization of Iran (IDRO) place on Wednesday and will take part in a seminar on Thursday to explore avenues of further trade, industrial, technology, and investment cooperation with their Iranian counterparts.
