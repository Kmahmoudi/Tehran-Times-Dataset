TEHRAN – The Islamic Revolution Guards Corps Ground Force is set to stage a massive war game in eastern, central and northeastern Iran on Monday, which will include the launch of a series of modern and high-accuracy rockets.

 The military maneuver, codenamed Payambar-e Azam-11 (The Great Prophet-11), will last for three days, Tasnim reported on Sunday. The first stage of the drills on Monday will see the launch of modern rockets in central parts of Iran.  SP/PA
