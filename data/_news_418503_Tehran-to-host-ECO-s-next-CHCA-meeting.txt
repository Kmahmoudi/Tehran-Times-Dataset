TEHRAN – Iran has been chosen to host the next meeting of Economic Cooperation Organization (ECO) Council of Heads of Customs Administration (CHCA), IRIB news reported on Tuesday. The decision was made during the 8th round of the event which was held in Pakistan. In the meeting, Iran presented a technical documentation of Iranian customs’ model of electronic information exchange which was welcomed by other ECO members. According to Iranian customs administration, CHCA is eager to know more about Iran’s achievements in electronic customs and electronic information exchange. EF/MA
