TEHRAN – The Cultural Heritage, Tourism, and Handicrafts Organization (CHTHO) as the organizer of the 10th Tehran International Tourism Exhibit has announced that the fair will bring the country under the spotlight in terms of its rich history and culture. The organization is also set to keep up with the highest regional standards for setting up the exhibit and its upcoming editions, ILNA reported on Sunday quoting Ali-Bager Namatizargaran who is in charge of the CHTHO marketing and advertising tourism. “See Iran Differently” has been selected as the motto of the exhibition, which will be held at the Tehran International Permanent Fairground from February 6 to 9, 2017.
