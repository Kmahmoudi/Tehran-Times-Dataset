TEHRAN – The Majlis speaker has said there is no problem to launch an investigation into the performance of Tehran Municipality.

 Ali Larijani made the statement on Tuesday in a meeting with three lawmakers who had requested investigation into the municipality, Fars reported. The meeting came after the Urban Development Committee, in a session with Mayor Mohammad Baqer Qalibaf, turned down a motion to investigate the performance of municipality. Reportedly, the session had been convened following a judicial case about the performance of municipality. SP/PA
