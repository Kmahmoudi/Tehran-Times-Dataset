TEHRAN- Tehran International Exhibition of Automobile and Related Industries (Tehran Auto Show) became operational at Shahr-e-Aftab International Exhibition Center on Tuesday hosting giant Iranian and foreign automakers, IRIB news reported. As reported, 200 Iranian and foreign automakers from South Korea, Japan, China, Turkey, France, Germany, England, Spain, Italy and Taiwan are taking part in the five-day event.
Among the well-known car manufacturing companies it can be referred to Iranian carmakers Iran Khodro, Saipa and Pars Khodro as well as foreign companies including BMW, Scania, Volvo, Renault, Mitsubishi, MG, Hyundai, Toyota, KIA Motors, Nissan, MVM, Chery and etc. 
Famous automotive service companies such as Caspian, Liqui Moly and Profi-Car are also present in the show. 
The participating companies plan to unveil their latest products in Tehran Auto Show which includes introduction of 25 new car models to the market.
The show will wrap up on February 18. HJ/MA/MG
