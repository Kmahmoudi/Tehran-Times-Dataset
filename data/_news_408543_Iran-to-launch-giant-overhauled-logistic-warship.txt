TEHRAN – The commander of the Iranian Navy’s First Zone announced on Wednesday that the country will soon launch a giant overhauled warship.

 “The Khark logistic warship, which is the biggest warship in West Asia has been overhauled after 35 years by Navy experts in Bandar Abbas and will be launched soon,” Rear Admiral Hossein Azad told reporters in Bandar Abbas, IRNA reported. SP/PA
