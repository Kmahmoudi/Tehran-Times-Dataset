TEHRAN – A Tehran Friday prayer leader has said that the enemies oppose the Islamic Republic because it’s under the umbrella of Wilayat al-Faqih.  Addressing worshippers, Ayatollah Mohammad Emami-Kashani said the enemies are exploiting Iran’s economic issues to create insecurity in the country. Thanks to the Leader of the Islamic Revolution Ayatollah Ali Khamenei's wise advices, the enemies' anti-Iran plots have been thwarted, Emami-Kashani said. He also lauded the Parliament and Judiciary for standing against the bullying powers and issuing prompt statements against enemies' measures.
