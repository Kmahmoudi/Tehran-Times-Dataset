TEHRAN – Iranian director Zaniar Lotfi’s “Cradle” will compete in the 37th Image of Cabestany Short Film Festival, which will be held in the French city from March 16 to 19.

 “Cradle” is the story of a child who tries to take care of a newborn baby sleeping in a cradle. ABU/YAW All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
