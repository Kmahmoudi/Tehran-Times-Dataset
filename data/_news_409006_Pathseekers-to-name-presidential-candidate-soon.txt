TEHRAN – The Society of Pathseekers of the Islamic Revolution will introduce its candidate for the 2017 presidential election within 10 days, said the society’s secretary general.

 Speaking to Tasnim news agency on Saturday, Parviz Sarvari said his fellow members of the society are discussing their candidates. He said by the end of the Iranian month of Azar, December 20, they will disclose their candidate to the principlist coalition. Society of Devotees of the Islamic Revolution and the Islamic Coalition Party also recently said they are about to introduce their candidates. SP/PA
