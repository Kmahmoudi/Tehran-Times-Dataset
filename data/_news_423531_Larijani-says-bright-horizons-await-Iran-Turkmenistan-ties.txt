TEHRAN – Iranian Majlis Speaker Ali Larijani said on Sunday that there are bright horizons for relations between Tehran and Ashgabat, ISNA reported. “Fortunately, there’s a good approach toward the expansion of relations with Turkmenistan,” Larijani said in a meeting with Turkmen Foreign Minister Rasit Meredow in Tehran. Also, he said, the parliamentary ties between the two countries are very good and both sides’ parliaments are willing to boost ties. Larijani also said the parliaments should make use of existing potentials to expand relations.
