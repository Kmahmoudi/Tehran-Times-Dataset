TEHRAN – Heads of the three branches of government met at the Parliament building on Monday evening to discuss various issues, including plans to facilitate the process of foreign investment in the country. “Good decisions have been made to better attract investment, particularly by the Iranian entrepreneurs abroad,” President Rouhani said after the trilateral meeting with Parliament Speaker Ali Larijani and Judiciary Chief Ayatollah Sadeq Amoli Larijani. All investors and entrepreneurs should be able to enjoy the economic opportunities in Iran, the president added.
