TEHRAN – Iranian President Hassan Rouhani in a message to his Romanian counterpart Klaus Iohannis on Thursday stressed the need for a further enhancement of relations between Tehran and Bucharest.

 “I extend my heartfelt congratulations to His Excellency, the Romanian government and people on Romanian Republic's national day,” Rouhani said in his message to Iohannis, ILNA reported. “I hope for growing expansion of bilateral relations in all political, economic and cultural levels,” he wrote.
