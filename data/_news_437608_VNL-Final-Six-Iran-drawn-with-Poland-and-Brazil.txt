TEHRAN – Iran have been drawn with Brazil and Poland in Pool B of the 2019 Volleyball Nations League Final Six. The Iranian team, who are looking for the VNL first medal, will start the competition with a match against powerhouse Poland on July 11. Igor Kolakovic’s men will meet Brazil on July 12. Hosts the U.S. are pitted against Russia and France. The VNL final round will be held in the Credit Union 1 Arena, Chicago, United States From July 10 to 14.  This will be the first edition of the World League or the Nations League to have the Final Round hosted in North America.
