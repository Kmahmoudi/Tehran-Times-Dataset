TEHRAN – Iran’s Construction and Development of Transportation Infrastructures Company announced that currently 3,500 kilometers of railway is under construction across the country, IRNA reported on Saturday. According to Kheirollah Khademi, managing director of the company, five major railway projects are going to be operational during the current Iranian calendar year (March 21, 2017- March 20, 2018).
