TEHRAN – Iranian automaker Iran Khodro Company (IKCO) launched its first representative office in Armenian capital Yerevan, IRNA reported on Saturday. Iranian ambassador to Armenia Seyed Kazem Sajjadi and a handful of Armenian officials attended the opening ceremony.
The company which has been actively present in Armenia’s auto market in recent years is now seeking to increase its market share and also set a base for future cooperation with Armenia in auto industry.  EF/MA
