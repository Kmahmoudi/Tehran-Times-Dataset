TEHRAN- Serbia is to receive a trade delegation from Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO) in mid-February, the portal of TPO reported. Concurrent with Iran-Serbia Joint Economic Committee meeting, the delegation will make a visit to the southeast European country.
