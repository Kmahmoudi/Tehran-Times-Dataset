TEHRAN – The head of the Iranian Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee has said U.S. President-elect Donald Trump is duty bound to implement the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action, the official name for the nuclear deal.

 “The JCPOA is an international agreement,” Alaeddin Boroujerdi said on Saturday, ICANA reported. However, he added, “As the Supreme Leader said, if they tear the deal, we will burn it.” SP/PA
