The 17th International Police, Safety, and Security Equipment Exhibition (IPAS 2018), opened in Tehran on Monday. With the theme of “security and safety support domestic production”, the event will run until October 4 at Imam Khomeini Mosalla with companies from 20 countries in attendance, showcasing their products. Increasing the quality and upgrading police equipment in order to reduce accidents and minimize the loss they incur and supporting inventors active in field of designing and devising police equipment are of the objectives of the exhibition.
