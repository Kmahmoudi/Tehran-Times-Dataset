The Leader granted the clemency on the occasion of Eid al-Adha (the Feast of Sacrifice) and Eid al-Ghadeer upon a request by Judiciary Chief Ayatollah Sadeq Amoli Larijani, Press TV reported on Thursday. The 10th day of the lunar Islamic month of Dhu al-Hijjah marks the Eid al-Adha, which is celebrated at the end of the annual Hajj pilgrimage. Article 110 of the constitution grants the Leader the right to pardon or commute the sentences of convicts upon a recommendation from the Judiciary chief.
