TEHRAN – Chief of the Islamic Revolution Guard Corps (IRGC) Mohammad-Ali Jafari met on Monday with Army Commander Abdolrahim Mousavi, rejoicing over the enemy’s failure in creating discord among Iranian armed forces. The two top generals highlighted the importance of brotherly relations among the armed forces, saying cooperative interaction between the IRGC and Army would frustrate the enemy, Mehr reported. It came two days after Mousavi attended an IRGC meeting and said the enemies have repeatedly failed to harm Iran through creating division between the IRGC and the Army.
