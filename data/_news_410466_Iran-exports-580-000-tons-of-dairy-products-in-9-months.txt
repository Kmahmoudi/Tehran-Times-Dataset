TEHRAN- Iran could manage to export 580,000 tons of dairy products during the first nine months of the present Iranian year (March 20- December 20, 2016), Mehr news agency quoted Iranian Deputy Agriculture Minister.
 As Hassan Rokni said, Iran exports dairy product to 30 countries.The country produces seven million tons of milk domestically, he added.  HJ/MA  All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
