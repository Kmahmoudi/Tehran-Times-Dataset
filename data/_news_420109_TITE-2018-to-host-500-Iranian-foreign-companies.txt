TEHRAN- The 11th edition of Tehran International Tourism Exhibition (TITE 2018) is to host 500 Iranian and foreign companies from February 23 to 26, IRIB news reported on Sunday. 
 “Some 430 Iranian and 70 foreign companies from Italy, Germany, Brazil, England, Switzerland, Spain, Russia, and Cyprus will take part in this event at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds,” Mohammad Moheb Khodaiee, the deputy head of Cultural Heritage, Handicrafts and Tourism Organization of Iran announced in a news conference.
