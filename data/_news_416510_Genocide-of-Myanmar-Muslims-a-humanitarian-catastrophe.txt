TEHRAN – A special aide to Iran’s parliament speaker has expressed deep concern over systematic violence against Muslims in Myanmar, describing it as a “humanitarian catastrophe”. “Genocide of the Muslim minority in Myanmar is a humanitarian catastrophe, which requires urgent humanitarian aid, [and] political and international support,” Hossein Amir-Abdollahian tweeted on Sunday. Amir-Abdollahian also denounced the recent wave of brutal crackdown on Muslims. He also deplored Muslim countries’ silence on the genocide of Rohingya Muslims.
