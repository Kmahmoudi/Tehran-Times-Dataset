TEHRAN- The Iranian capital is simultaneously hosting four international exhibitions from October 25 to 28 at the Tehran Permanente International Fairground, IRIB news reported on Wednesday. The three international exhibitions include the 4th International Exhibition of Footwear, Bag, Leather and Related Industry, the 16th International Exhibition of Heating, Cooling, Ventilating, Air Conditioning and Refrigerating, and the 2nd International Fisheries Industry Exhibition (IFEX 2017). As reported, 160 Iranian and 80 foreign companies are taking part in the 4th International Exhibition of Footwear, Bag, Leather and Related Industry. Also, foreign delegations from Russia, China, Italy, and Turkey plan to visit the exposition to discuss cooperation with Iranian counterparts. The 16th International Exhibition of Heating, Cooling, Ventilating, Air Conditioning and Refrigerating is hosting 400 Iranian companies and 200 foreign ones from Italy, China, Serbia, South Korea, Germany, Taiwan, Turkey, Thailand, Sweden, Norway, Finland, Denmark, Ireland, Switzerland, Kuwait, Japan, England, the U.S. India, Russia, the E.U. Spain, Greece, Belgium, and Poland.  These two exhibitions were inaugurated on Wednesday with participation of officials from Iranian Industry Ministry, Parliament, and Chamber of Commerce. Some 105 Iranian and foreign companies from Italy, Turkey, Greece, Germany, the U.S., Norway, Denmark, the Netherlands, Spain, France, and India are present in IFEX 2017, which started operation in the presence of the Iranian Agriculture Minister Mahmoud Hojjati.  HJ/MA
