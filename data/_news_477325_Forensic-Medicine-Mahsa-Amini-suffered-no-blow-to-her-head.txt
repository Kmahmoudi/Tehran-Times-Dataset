TEHRAN – The Forensic Medicine on Friday released the impatiently awaited report about the death of Mahsa Amini in which it said she suffered no damage to her head and other vital organs. Based on the medical documents, a CT scan of her brain and lungs, examination of the corpse, pathology and necropsy, it was revealed that Amini’s death was not related to a blow to head or other vital organs, it said. Amini, a 22-year-old woman, fainted at police custody in Tehran on September 13, where she was about to receive education about Islamic dress code.
