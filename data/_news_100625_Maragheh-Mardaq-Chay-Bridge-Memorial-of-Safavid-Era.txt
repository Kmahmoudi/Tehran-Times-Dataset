The 26m-long bridge which is made of brick, used to be a transparent junction for Maragheh, Hashtroud and Mianeh. The bridge and its Roman style of identical spans and arches is a must-see for all those who journey the region. Maragheh is a city in north-western Iran. It lies on the southern slopes of Mount Sahand, just 22km east of Lake Orumiyeh, and 130km south of Tabriz by road.
