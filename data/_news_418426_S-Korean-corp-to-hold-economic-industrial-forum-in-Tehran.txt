TEHRAN - South Korea’s Small & Medium Business Corporation (SBC) is to hold an economic-industrial forum in Tehran to boost ties with Iranian businesspeople, the portal of Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) reported on Sunday. The forum will be held on November 26 with the participation of 12 South Korean companies active in various fields including industry, agriculture, foodstuff, chemicals, etc.   The South Korean companies are willing to expand ties with their Iranian counterparts through making business-to-business (B2B) transactions, the report added.  HJ/MG All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
