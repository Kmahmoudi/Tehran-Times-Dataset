TEHRAN – The Iranian Foreign Ministry on Thursday denounced a terrorist attack on Germany’s consulate in Afghanistan, saying the international community must make comprehensive efforts to seriously fight terrorism.

 “Such a vicious conduct emphasizes the necessity of an international consensus and all-out efforts by all governments, nations and vigilant consciences in the human society to rigorously and relentlessly fight this ghastly tragedy,” ministry spokesman Bahram Qassemi said on Friday, Press TV reported.
