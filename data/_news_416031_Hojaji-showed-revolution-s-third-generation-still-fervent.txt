TEHRAN – The bravery of Mohsen Hojaji, who was brutally beheaded by Daesh recently in Syria, has proven that the third generation of the Islamic Revolution is still fervent, the Friday prayer leader of Tehran told worshippers.

 According to Fars, Ayatollah Ahmad Khatami also said the fact that the Islamic Revolution Guard Corps has promised it will take revenge against Daesh is praiseworthy, predicting the expected revenge will be harsh.
