TEHRAN – Iranian director Ali Zare-Qanatnoi’s animation “Dad’s Fragile Doll” will go on screen at the Millennial Film Festival, which will be held in Austin, in the U.S. state of Texas from August 16 to 18. Based on a story with the same title by prominent Iranian writer Hushang Golshiri, the film tells the story of a girl, who reconstructs the events leading to her father’s imprisonment and execution using dolls her mother made.
