TEHRAN – Interior Minister Abdolreza Rahmani-Fazli said on Sunday that the country’s Social Council is set to hold a meeting in a month, attended by the Leader of the Revolution Ayatollah Ali Khamenei. Rahmani-Fazli said that during the meeting the full report of the measures taken by the country’s governmental bodies regarding social harms will be presented to the Leader, Fars reported. Due to the efforts of the Leader and President Rouhani, the Social Council has been put in the right direction and has managed to attain a true understanding regarding the country’s social harms, he added.
