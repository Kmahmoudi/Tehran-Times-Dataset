TEHRAN – Trade promotion Organization (TPO) of Iran plans to establish two permanent trade centers in Iraq, TPO portal reported on Tuesday quoting the country’s commercial attaché in Iraq as saying. In this regard, Nasser Behzad noted that these trade centers will be established following TPO’s policies in order to create an appropriate basis for the activity of Iranian businessmen in the neighboring country.
