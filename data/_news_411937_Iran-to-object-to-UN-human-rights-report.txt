TEHRAN – Tehran will object to a recent report by the United Nations special rapporteur on Iran in an upcoming meeting of the UN’s Human Rights Council in Geneva, ISNA reported. 

 Kazem Gharibabadi, Iran’s Human Rights Council director for international affairs, said on Monday that that an Iranian team, headed by Deputy Foreign Minister Abbas Araqchi who is now in Geneva participating in the four-week-long sessions of the UN body, will bring up the issue in the coming days.
