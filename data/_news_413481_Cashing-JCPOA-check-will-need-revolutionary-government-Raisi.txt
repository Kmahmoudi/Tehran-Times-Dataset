TEHRAN – Speaking to his supports in Yasuj on Monday, presidential hopeful Ebrahim Raisi criticized the incumbent government for having adopted voluntary actions in implementing the nuclear deal before having the other side “cash the check,” IRIB reported.
  
“Cashing the JCPOA check will need a revolutionary government,” Raisi said.  He also said despite what the government says it is in fact possible to create jobs in the millions and his potential government will do that.
