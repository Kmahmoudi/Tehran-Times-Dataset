Around 320,000 Iranians visited the neighboring country in January-October this year in comparison with 204,000 the year before, IRNA reported on Tuesday. The Republic of Azerbaijan hosted a total of 2,276,604 foreign nationals during the 10-month period, showing 20.1 growth year on year. Russian, Georgian, Iranian and Turkish tourists accounted for the highest number of visits to the country.
