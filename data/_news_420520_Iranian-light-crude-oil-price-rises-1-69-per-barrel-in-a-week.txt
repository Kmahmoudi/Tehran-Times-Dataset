TEHRAN- Iran’s light crude oil price rose $1.69 in the week ended on January 12 to settle at $69.26 per barrel, Shana news agency reported on Saturday. The country’s light oil price was reported $68.51 on average since the beginning of 2018. Iran’s heavy crude oil price also experienced a $1.24 growth in the said week to settle at $65.62 per barrel.  The country’s heavy oil price was reported $65.07 on average since the beginning of 2018. In the mentioned week, Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries (OPEC)’s basket price stood at $66.81 per barrel with $1.32 increase compared to the preceding week. MA
