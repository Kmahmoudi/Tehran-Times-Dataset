TEHRAN – The principlist Combatant Clergy Association on Thursday selected two of its key figures, Mizan news agency reported.

 Hojjatoleslam Mohammad Hassan Torabi Fard was chosen as the body’s vice-chancellor and Qolamreza Mesbahi Moqaddam as the spokesperson. Now that the body knows its key figures, it will start meetings with other principlist groups and parties to discuss their policies for the next year’s presidential elections, sources have said.
