TEHRAN – Iran’s conglomerate production increased by 26 percent in the first eight months of the current Iranian calendar year (March 21-November 21) compared to last year’s same time span, IRNA reported on Saturday. Five major Iranian pelletizing plants produced over 20.808 million tons of conglomerate in the said period. Iranian First Vice-President Es’haq Jahangiri inaugurated Sangan Pelletizing Plant in Khaf region on Thursday, with the capacity of producing five million tons of conglomerates per annum.
