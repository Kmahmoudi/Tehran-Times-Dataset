TEHRAN- A Belgian trade delegation is scheduled to visit Iran on October 30-31 to explore avenues of enhancing mutual economic cooperation with their Iranian counterparts, the portal of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines, and Agriculture (ICCIMA) announced.  The delegates are active in various fields such as oil and gas equipment and services, commercial vehicles and auto parts, construction, interior decoration and architecture, flooring and textiles, forwarding and transport, new energies, food industry, information technology and etc. HJ/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
