TEHRAN – Abbas Araqchi, Iran’s deputy foreign minister for legal and international affairs, left Tehran for Jakarta on Sunday to attend the ministerial meeting of Indian Ocean Rim Association (IORA), IRNA reported.

 Araqchi departs for Jakarta to attend IORA meetingCommittee of Senior Officials Meeting of IORA started work in Jakarta, Indonesia, on March 5, with participation of Iranian representatives. Majid Bizmark, director general of the Iranian Foreign Ministry for international affairs, environment and sustainable development, and Iranian Ambassador to Jakarta Valiollah Mohammadi-Nasrabadi are present in the IORA meetings.
