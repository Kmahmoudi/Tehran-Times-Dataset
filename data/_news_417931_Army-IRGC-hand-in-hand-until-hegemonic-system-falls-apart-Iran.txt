TEHRAN – The Army and the Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps (IRGC) will work hand in hand until the “foundations of the hegemonic system collapse and the life of the Zionist regime comes to an end,” the commander of the Islamic Republic of Iran’s Army said on Wednesday.  Major General Abdolrahim Mousavi made the remarks at a graduation ceremony for Army cadets in Tehran.  The Army chief also welcomed the Leader of the Islamic Revolution Ayatollah Ali Khamenei for addressing the event.  Top commanders, university instructors, and clergymen also attended the meeting.
