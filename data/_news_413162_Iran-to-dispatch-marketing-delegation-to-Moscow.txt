TEHRAN- The Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO) is to send a trade, marketing and investing delegation to Russia from September 15 to 18 to explore avenues of exporting Iranian products to the country, the official website of TPO announced. The delegation will travel to Moscow simultaneous with the International Food Exhibition in the Russian capital (WolrdFood Moscow), which is going to be held on September 11-14.
