The unfortunate incident took place at about 11 a.m. around Vali-Asr square of Qom subway construction site and following the collapse a group of construction crews got trapped under the rubble. Rescue and relief forces along with emergency medical services teams soon attended the scene and started searching through the rubble for survivors.
