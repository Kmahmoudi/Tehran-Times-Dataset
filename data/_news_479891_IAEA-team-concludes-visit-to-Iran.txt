The delegation from the International Atomic Energy Agency was headed by Massimo Aparo, chief of Department of Safeguards and the IAEA deputy director. The IAEA officials held talks with the chief of the Iranian Foreign Ministry Department for International, Legal and Parliamentary Affairs and the director for the Foreign Ministry Department for Arms Control and International Security. The IAEA team also held talks with AEOI chief Mohammad Eslami. During the negotiations the sides exchanged views on safeguards issues, areas for cooperation and future joint plans, the AEOI said in a press release.
