TEHRAN- Tehran Stock Exchange (TSE)’s main index (TEDPIX) gained 313 points to 81,579 in the week ended on Friday, Tasnim news agency reported. Some 2.584 billion securities worth 7.834 trillion rials (about $206.9 million) were traded through 182,000 deals in TSE. The first market’s index rose 236 points to 57,173 and second market’s index added 596 points to 177,753, showing 0.414 percent growth and 0.34 percent fall, respectively, in the mentioned week.
