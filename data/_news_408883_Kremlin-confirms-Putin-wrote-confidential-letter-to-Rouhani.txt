TEHRAN – The Kremlin says Russian President Vladimir Putin’s point man on Syria has delivered a “confidential” letter penned by the Russian head of state to Iranian President Hassan Rouhani.

 “I can confirm the submission of the Russian president’s letter to his Iranian counterpart but cannot reveal its details,” Kremlin spokesman Dmitry Peskov said on Monday. Russia’s ITAR-TASS news agency reported that Peskov had confirmed the delivery of the message in the form of a letter. SP/PA
