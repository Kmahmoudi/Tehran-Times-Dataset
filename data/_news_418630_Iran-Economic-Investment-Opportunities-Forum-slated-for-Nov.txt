TEHRAN – An international forum on Iran’s economic and investment opportunities is due to be held on November 25-28 at IRIB International Conference Center in Tehran. According to the portal of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA), 100 foreign companies, interested in investment in Iran, along with about 300 Iranian companies will be participating in this event. Active in a variety of areas including tourism, renewable energies, agriculture, oil, gas and petrochemicals, auto industry, mining and etc., the foreign participants will have the chance to hold meetings with Iranian counterparts during this four-day forum. China, Germany, Denmark, Finland, France, Russia, Japan, Serbia, South Korea, Spain, Switzerland, Sweden, UAE, Hong Kong and the United Kingdom are among the countries which have representatives to attend the event.
