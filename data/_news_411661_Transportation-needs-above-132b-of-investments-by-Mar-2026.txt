TEHRAN- Some 5 quadrillion rials (about $132.8 billion) of investments is required to fully implement transportation projects in Iran by the end of the country’s 1404 Outlook Plan (March 2026), according to a transport official. Iranian Deputy Transport Minister Amir Amini made the statement addressing the media on Sunday, IRNA reported.  HJ/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
