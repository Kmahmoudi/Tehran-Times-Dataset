TEHRAN – Iranian President Hassan Rouhani said on Saturday that the major powers, especially the U.S., seek to disappoint the Iranian people, stressing that “all should be vigilant not to play in enemy’s court”.
 “Current situation in the region [the Middle East region] shows that all the plots have been foiled. So, they seek to hatch other plots in the region. People should be cautious about this officials should adopt due plans,” he told reporters after a meeting of heads of the three branches. Rouhani attached great importance to boosting people’s hope under current political, cultural and economic situations of the country. He also said that many achievements have been made in fighting terrorists in Iraq, Syria and Lebanon, infuriating the U.S. and the Zionist regime of Israel. The meeting between Rouhani, Majlis Speaker Ali Larijani, and Judiciary Chief Ayatollah Sadeq Amoli Larijani was hosted by the Judicial Branch. NA/SP
