TEHRAN- Finland's Ambassador to Tehran Harri Kämäräinen told IRNA that a task  force, comprised of high banking officials of the two countries, has been formed to overcome the present obstacles on the way of expanding bilateral banking ties. The ambassador noted that the formed task force has been successful in easing foreign exchange operations between the two sides’ banks. He also expressed content about the ever-increasing economic ties between Iran and Finland since the implementation of Iran’s nuclear deal in January 2016.
