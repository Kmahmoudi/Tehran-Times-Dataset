TEHRAN – The Islamic Revolution Guards Corps’ intelligence forces managed to capture a big haul of contraband spying devices in northern Tehran, an informed source announced on Monday.

 The unnamed source said spying devices include two-way radios and eavesdropping equipment, Fars reported. The devices were captured from a criminal band that sold them in an organized way, according to the source. The source said four members of the band were identified and arrested as well. SP/PA
