TEHRAN – The Iranian police has announced it has found nothing to indicate that a fire that burned down the Plasco building in downtown Tehran had been set off deliberately.

 “The forces have not encountered any suspicious case indicating some intention behind the incident,” Police Chief Hossein Ashtari said on Wednesday, IRNA reported. The data obtained from the location of the incident correspond to what city officials have said, he stressed. SP/PA
