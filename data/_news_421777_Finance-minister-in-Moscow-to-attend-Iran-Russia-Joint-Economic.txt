TEHRAN- Iranian Finance and Economic Affairs Minister Masoud Karbasian arrived in Moscow on Sunday to attend the 14th meeting of Iran-Russia Joint Economic Committee, IRIB news reported.
 The meeting will be held in the Russian capital on Monday and Tuesday and a number of cooperation MOUs are planned to be signed between the two countries on the sidelines of it. During the two-day meeting, Karbasian, who is the Iranian chairman of the committee, will discuss mutual cooperation in different areas including energy, transportation, commerce and costumes, health and agriculture with the Russian Energy Minister Alexander Novak, who is the Russian chairman of the committee.  A business forum will be also held on the sidelines of the meeting to be participated by the Iranian and Russian businessmen and traders.
