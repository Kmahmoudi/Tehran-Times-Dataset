TEHRAN- Seven development projects worth 700 billion rials (about $16.6 million) were launched at Tehran’s Mehrabad International Airport, according to the director general of the airport. Mir-Saeed Safinia said the projects have been handed over to the contractors and are scheduled to be completed within 18 months, IRIB reported on Tuesday. He mentioned conducting some improvements in Terminal 1 and Terminal 2 of the airport as one of these projects.
