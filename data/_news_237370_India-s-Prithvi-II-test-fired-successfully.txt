BALASORE, Orissa -- Indigenously developed, nuclear-capable ballistic missile Prithvi-II was today test-fired from the Integrated Test Range (ITR) at Chandipur, about 15 km from here off Orissa coast, in less than an hour of flight testing of “Dhanush” from a naval ship.
