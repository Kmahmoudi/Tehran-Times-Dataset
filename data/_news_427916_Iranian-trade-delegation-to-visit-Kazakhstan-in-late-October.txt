TEHRAN- Iran-Kazakhstan Joint Chamber of Commerce plans to dispatch an Iranian trade delegation to Kazakhstan in late October, the portal of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) published. The delegates’ visit will be concurrent with holding the first permanent exhibition of Iranian premium products in Almaty, the capital city of Kazakhstan, and a meeting is also scheduled to be held between the traders and businessmen of the two countries.
