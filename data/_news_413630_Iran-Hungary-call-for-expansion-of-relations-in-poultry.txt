TEHRAN – Iranian Deputy Agriculture Minister Hassan Rokni has called for expansion of cooperation between Iran and Hungary’s poultry industries, IRNA reported on Sunday. The official, who is visiting the European country heading a 50-member delegation comprised of representatives of the mentioned industry, met with his Hungarian counterpart Lajos Bognár.
Bognár, for his part, said there is currently bilateral cooperation in training and Hungary is ready to expand it to other areas as well.
