TEHRAN- The 1st Iran Consulting & Engineering Services Exhibition (ICES-Expo 2017) will be held at the Permanent Fairground of Tehran Municipality (Boostan-e Goftegoo) from February 1 to 4, 2017, ISNA reported on Tuesday. Some 500 foreign guests from the Middle East, Europe and Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS) will attend the event, organized by the Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO).
