TEHRAN- Tehran Stock Exchange (TSE)’s main index (TEDPIX) gained 6,017 points to stand at 180,635 points on Wednesday, registering a new record again, up 3.45 percent from Tuesday’s record, Tasnim news agency reported. Also, IFX, the main index of Iran's over-the-counter (OTC) market known also as Iran Fara Bourse (IFB), rose 58 points to 2,071 on Wednesday, as 1.276 billion securities worth 6.86 trillion rials (about $163.3 million) were traded through 117,000 deals.
