TEHRAN – The Iranian team of nuclear negotiators are going to attend a meeting of the Parliament National Security and Foreign Policy Committee to brief the lawmakers on the results of the latest session of the JCPOA joint commission, Hossein Naqavi Hosseini, committee spokesman, told Tasnim on Saturday.

 Representatives from Iran, the 5+1 group and the European Union gathered in Austria on Tuesday to address Iran’s complaint about a U.S. congressional bill extending the Iran Sanctions Act for ten years. SP/PA
