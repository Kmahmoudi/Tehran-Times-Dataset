TEHRAN – Ayatollah Ali Khamenei, Leader of the Islamic Revolution, issued a message on Sunday offering his “sincere” condolences to the Iranian families who lost loved ones in the oil tanker inferno in the East China Sea. 
Calling the incident “a bitter and heartbreaking incident”, the Leader said, “I sincerely express condolences” to the families of the deceased crew.
