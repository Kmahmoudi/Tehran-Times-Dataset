TEHRAN – Three Iranian films will go on screen at the 34th BUFF International Film Festival, which will be held in Malmo, Sweden from March 20 to 25.

 Iranian director Behruz Nuranipur’s “A157” will be screened in the documentary section while “Just This Once” by Mohammad-Hassan Shahmohammadi and “Class” by Reza Golchin will be shown in the short films section of the festival.
