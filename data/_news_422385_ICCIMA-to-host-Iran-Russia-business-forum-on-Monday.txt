TEHRAN- Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) will host Iran-Russia business forum in Tehran on Monday, the portal of ICCIMA announced. A delegation of Russian traders and businessmen active in different areas including agriculture, transportation, oil and gas, solar energy, logistics, imports and exports, IT and mining industries will negotiate possible cooperation with the Iranian businessmen during the mentioned forum.
