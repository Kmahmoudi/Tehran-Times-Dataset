TEHRAN – Iran’s first vice president said on Friday that the recent terrorist attacks in Tehran led to forging unity and solidarity among Iranian nation in their struggle against terrorism.

 Es’haq Jahangiri made the remarks on the sidelines of the funeral processions held for victims of the Wednesday terrorist attacks, IRNA reported. “When Takfiri terrorist groups disrupted order and attacked various countries and Muslims in the region, the Islamic Republic of Iran was the only country to stand up against them,” he stated.
