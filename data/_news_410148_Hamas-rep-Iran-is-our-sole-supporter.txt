TEHRAN – The representative of the Palestinian Hamas resistance movement in Tehran has said Iran is the only supporter of the organization in the Middle East.

 “Unfortunately, we are seeing a growing trend of Arab countries normalizing their ties with the Zionist regime,” Khalid Qadumi said in a meeting on Monday, ISNA reported. “Such moves used to be carried out covertly, but now they are becoming overt….
