TEHRAN- A 20-member German trade delegation will pay a visit to the Iranian capital to hold talks with their Iranian counterparts on expansion of cooperation, the portal of Trade Promotion Organization of Iran reported.  “This is the delegation’s second visit to Iran and Germans will meet their Iranian counterparts at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds on Saturday during an official meeting which is to be led by TPO Head Mojtaba Khosrotaj,” Behrouz Hosnolfat, an official from TPO was quoted as saying.  “The delegation has previously held talks on non-oil exports and attraction of investments and this time intends to discuss cooperation on various fields including steel, banking and finance, renewable energies, metallurgy equipment, medical equipment, auto and auto parts, and development of business environment,” he added.
