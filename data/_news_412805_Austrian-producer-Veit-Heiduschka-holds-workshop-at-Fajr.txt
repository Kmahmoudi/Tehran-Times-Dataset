TEHRAN – Austrian producer Veit Heiduschka, mostly known for collaborations with his fellow director Michael Haneke, held a workshop on filmmaking during the 35th Fajr International Film Festival in Tehran on Thursday. 

 He is the producer of Haneke’s movies including “Funny Games”, “Cache”, “The White Ribbon” and “Amour”. Iranian actor and producer Alireza Shojanuri accompanied Heiduschka during the workshop, which was attended by students from Iran and several other countries. The workshop was held at the Talent Campus, a venue established to discover young gifted students in various fields of cinema. The campus’ workshop program began on April 19, two days ahead of the opening of the festival and will come to an end on April 26. Over 100 students from 33 countries are attending the campus’s various workshops conducted by a large number cineastes from Iran and around the world.
