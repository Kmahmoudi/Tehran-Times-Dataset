TEHRAN – Heads of the three branches of government are scheduled to meet on Sunday in Majlis to examine the country’s economic condition, ISNA reported. In addition to the president, parliament speaker and judiciary chief, ministers and officials from the judiciary will attend the meeting. Meanwhile Mehrdad Lahouti, the spokesman of the Majlis Independent Velayee Faction, said on Wednesday that members of the faction will soon meet with President Rouhani to talk about economic problems. “The president must be held accountable for current economic issues,” said Lahouti.
