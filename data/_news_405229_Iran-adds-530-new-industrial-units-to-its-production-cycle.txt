TEHRAN- The managing director of Iran Small Industries and Industrial Parks Organization (ISIPO) announced that 530 new industrial units have come online during the first four months of the current Iranian year 1395 (March 20-July 21, 2016), IRNA reported on Friday. “In the same period, 430 inactive units have also been revived,” Ali Yazdani said. “The administration plans to make 12,000 of inactive industrial units as well as projects with less than 60 percent of physical development operational by the current yearend (March 20, 2017),” he added.
