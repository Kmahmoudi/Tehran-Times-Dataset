TEHRAN- Iran will set up a pavilion in the 8th Oman Health Exhibition which will take place in New Oman Convention & Exhibition Center in Muscat from September 24 to 26, Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) of Iran announced. The event targets all aspects of the health and wellness industry, opening doors for trade and investment. It was launched in 2009 and is now on its eight edition.
