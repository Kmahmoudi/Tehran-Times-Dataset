TEHRAN – The chief of staff of the Iranian armed forces is scheduled to visit Turkey on Tuesday for talks on fight against terrorism, IRIB reported on Sunday.

 The three-day tour by Major General Mohammad Baqeri will take place at the official invitation by his Turkish counterpart General Hulusi Akar. According to the report, Baqeri and his high-ranking company are expected to discuss the latest regional developments, bilateral cooperation and border issues with Turkish officials. SP/PA
