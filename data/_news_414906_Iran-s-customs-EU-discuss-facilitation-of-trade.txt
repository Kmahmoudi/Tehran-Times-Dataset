TEHRAN – Islamic Republic of Iran Custom Administration (IRICA)’s officials and the European Union’s representatives gathered in Brussels to discuss expansion of customs cooperation and facilitating trade relations, IRIB reported on Saturday. According to IRICA’s official website, in this regard director general of Taxation and Customs Union of the European Commission and IRICA’s president met and explored different areas of mutual cooperation.  EF/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
