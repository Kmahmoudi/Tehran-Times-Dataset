TEHRAN – Hosting 768 foreign and domestic companies, the 17th Iran International Electricity Exhibition (IEE) kicked off on Saturday at Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds, IRNA reported.
 Some 458 Iranian companies alongside 310 foreign exhibitors from 30 different countries including Germany, China, Turkey, Italy, Spain, Finland, Poland, United Kingdom, Sweden, UAE and Russia are showcasing their latest achievements and products in this four-day event.  The exhibitors are active in various sectors of power industry including wire, cable, electrical equipment, automation, instrumentation systems and etc.  EF/MA
