TEHRAN – Thousands of mourners attended a funeral service for Seyed Rohullah Ajamian in Karaj, a Basij member, who was martyred during the recent riots in Karaj.  The service was also attended by local officials and IRGC members. Ajamian was a longtime member of Basij who was stabbed to death by rioters on Thursday. Rioters implicated in the martyrdom were arrested by security forces before the funeral was held.  All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
