TEHRAN – Mohsen Rezaee, secretary of the Expediency Council, said on Tuesday that Iran-China relations are improving, predicting a “bright future” for bilateral relations. Meeting with a high-ranking Chinese delegation in Tehran, Rezaee urged the Chinese government to share its experience in the fields of economy and investment with the Islamic Republic, IRIB news reported. Hoping that the Chinese side would try to overcome obstacles in the path of expanding bilateral ties, the official said the hegemonic powers oppose friendly relations between Iran and China. MH/PA
