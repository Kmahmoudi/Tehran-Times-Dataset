TEHRAN – An Iran-Thailand business forum is scheduled to be held in Tehran on July 24, Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) portal reported. A handful of Thailand’s Halal industry entrepreneurs and businessmen mostly active in areas like food industry, agriculture and cosmetics, are going to attend this forum. Skin and hair products, oils, rice, , ginger, chocolate, pasta, fruits, vegetables, sugar, sweets, biscuits, canned products, corn and coffee are some of the products to be promoted by the Thai delegation.
