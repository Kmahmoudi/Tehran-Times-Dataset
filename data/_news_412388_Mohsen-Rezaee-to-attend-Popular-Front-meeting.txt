TEHRAN – Mohsen Rezaee, secretary of the Expediency Council, will attend the Thursday meeting of the Popular Front of Revolution Forces, Tabnak news website reported on Wednesday.

 According to the source, Rezaee will appear as a keynote speaker to “answer possible rumors about presidential election”. The popular front is to meet to reduce the number of its potential candidates from the current ten to five and later on to just one for the May 19 presidential election. Rezaee is currently one of the possible candidates of the front. SP/PA
