TEHRAN- Kazakhstan’s Almaty is to host an Islamic Republic of Iran’s exclusive exhibition on October 5-8, according to the official website of Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO). The exhibition is supposed to be held focusing on exposition of Iranian products in various sectors such as constructional materials, oil, gas and petrochemicals, mines and mining machinery, food products and horticulture. Almaty, Kazakhstan's largest metropolis, served as the country's capital until 1997 and remains Kazakhstan's trading and cultural hub.
