TEHRAN – The final batch of a 149-ton yellow cake shipment from Russia will arrive in Tehran on Tuesday, the spokesman of the Atomic Energy Organization of Iran announced on Monday, ISNA reported.
 “During the past week, three batches of the shipment were imported from Russia and the fourth and final batch is expected to arrive in Tehran on Tuesday,” Behrouz Kamalvandi said. “The 149-ton yellow cake shipment is more than our production of the past 10 years,” he said. SP/PA
