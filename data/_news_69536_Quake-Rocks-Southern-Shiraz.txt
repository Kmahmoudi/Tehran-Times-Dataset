According to the Seismological Base of Shiraz, affiliated to the Geophysics Institute of Tehran University, the earthquake hit the city at 23:24 local time (1854 GMT) with its epicenter 20 kilometers south of Shiraz. The quake was accompanied by a tremendous sound, sparking panic among residents who spilled over to the streets.
