TEHRAN –Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) is planning to dispatch a trade delegation to Luxembourg and Belgium on July 11, ICCIMA portal reported. According to the report, ICCIMA head Gholam-Hossein Shafeie will be heading the delegation visiting the European countries. The visit is aimed for the two side’s businessmen to get to know each other and explore grounds of cooperation.
