On Saturday, Ayatollah Khamenei appointed General Ata'llah Salehi as the new deputy head of the Islamic Republic Guard Corps (IRGC) recruitment headquarters. He said that the appointment was made on the proposal of the IRGC commander and due to Salehi's competence, experience and knowledge.
