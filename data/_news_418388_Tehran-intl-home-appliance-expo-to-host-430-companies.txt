TEHRAN- The 17th International Exhibition of Household Appliances is due to be held from 23 to 26 November at Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds, IRIB reported. Some 294 Iranian and 136 foreign companies from 20 countries including China, Turkey, Georgia, Italy, Germany, South Korea, Thailand, Taiwan, Japan, Malaysia, Indonesia, Slovenia, Slovakia, Poland, Czech Republic, the Netherlands, Spain, France and Switzerland will be participating in this year’s event. According to the event organizers, an industry conference, a small bazaar exhibition and several workshops and specialized meetings are also planned to be held on the sidelines of the exhibition. Several trade delegations from different countries will also visit the exhibition to discuss business with Iranian counterparts. EF/MG
