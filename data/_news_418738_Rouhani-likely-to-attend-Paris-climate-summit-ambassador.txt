TEHRAN – Iran’s ambassador to Paris, Abolghassem Delfi, said on Wednesday that President Hassan Rouhani is likely to attend a summit on climate mobilization which will be held in France on December 12. In an interview with France 24, Delfi said Tehran is considering the invitation to attend the summit. “A presidential visit cannot be organized overnight, so we are verifying the dates and the availability of our president.”  Pointing to Macron’s upcoming visit to Tehran, he said when the moment is right, the French president will visit Tehran.
