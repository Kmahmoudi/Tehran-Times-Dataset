TEHRAN- Mohammad Meshkinfam has been appointed as the new managing director of Pars Oil and Gas Company replacing Ali-Akbar Sha’banpour, Shana reported on Saturday. National Iranian Oil Company (NIOC) Managing Director Ali Kardor appointed Meshkinfam to the post.
Pars Oil and Gas Company is in charge of implementing development phases of Iran’s South Pars gas field in the Persian Gulf. MA/MG
