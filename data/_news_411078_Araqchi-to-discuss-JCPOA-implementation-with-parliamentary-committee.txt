TEHRAN – On Wednesday, the Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee is going to put on agenda the Foreign Ministry’s fourth report on the implementation of the JCPOA.

 The session will be attended by Deputy Foreign Minister Abbas Araqchi for legal and international affairs, the committee spokesman Hossein Naqavi Hosseini said, IRIB reported. The Foreign Ministry is required to make such a report to the committee every three months. The committee itself will present a report to the Majlis floor every six months.
