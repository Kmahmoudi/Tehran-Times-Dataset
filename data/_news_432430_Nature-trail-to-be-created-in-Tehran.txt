TEHRAN — Tehran’s longest walking trail, dubbed “nature trail”, will be developed in district 6, central Tehran, the district mayor has said.
 The trail will start from Tehran international permanent fairground and will pass from Mallet Park to end in Pardisan Park, Tasnim news agency quoted Mohammad Reza Dehdaripour as saying on Monday. Research works and the project designs will be completed and the project will be implemented in the district 6, he concluded.
