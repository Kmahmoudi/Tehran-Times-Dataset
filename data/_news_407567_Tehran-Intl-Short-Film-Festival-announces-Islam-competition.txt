TEHRAN – Eleven films from Iranian and foreign filmmakers will be screened at the 33rd Tehran International Short Film Festival in a section dedicated to movies about Islam. “The Past” by Wathab Alsagar and “The Photo” by Baqer Alrabi, both from Iraq, “That’s Mine” by Maryam Kashkulinia from Iran and “The Safe Territory” by Luca Innocenti from Italy are among the movies selected for the section entitled “Films about Islam”. The Iranian Youth Cinema Society (IYCS) is the main organizer of the festival, which will be held at Tehran’s Charsu Cineplex from November 8 to 14.
