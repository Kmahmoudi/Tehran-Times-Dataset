TEHRAN – Commander of the Islamic Revolutionary Guards Corps (IRGC) Major General Mohammad Ali Jafari has said Iran is not wasting a moment to improve its defense capabilities as in the missile and drone industries. “Because of the morale for defense and for countering tyranny, we have no problem in the defense arena, and are growing and developing every day in the field of defense weapons like missiles and drones,” the IRGC commander said, Tasnim reported on Wednesday.
