TEHRAN- Iranian Energy Minister Reza Ardakanian, heading a delegation, left Tehran for Brasilia on Sunday to attend the 8th edition of the World Water Forum which is running from March 18 to 23, IRNA reported. The World Water Forum is the world’s biggest water-related event and is organized by the World Water Council (WWC), an international organization that brings together all those interested in the theme of water.  Its mission is “to promote awareness, build political commitment and trigger action on critical water issues at all levels, to facilitate the efficient conservation, protection, development, planning, management and use of water in all its dimensions on an environmentally sustainable basis for the benefit of all life on Earth”.
