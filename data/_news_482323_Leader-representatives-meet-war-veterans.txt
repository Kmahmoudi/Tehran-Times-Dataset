TEHRAN- Representatives of the Leader of Islamic Revolution met on Saturday with veterans of Iraq’s war against Iran in the 1980s. Senior clerics, including Hujjat-ul-Islam Ali Akbari, visited with a few disabled war veterans in Tehran. Ayatollah Ali Khamenei’s representatives paid tribute to the veterans for their priceless sacrifices during the imposed war on Iran.
