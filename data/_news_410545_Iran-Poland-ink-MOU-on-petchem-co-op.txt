TEHRAN- Petrochemical Special Economic Zone Organization of Iran and Pulawy Company of Poland signed a memorandum of understanding (MOU) on cooperation in petrochemical sector, Shana reported. The MOU, signed in Iranian southwestern Khuzestan province on January 26, covers cooperation on investment making and also transfer of technology. Pulawy that specializes in the production of mineral fertilizers and chemical products for various sectors and industries is a part of Grupa Azoty, one of the largest chemical companies in Europe and in the world.
