TEHRAN – The Iranian intelligence ministry has disbanded a 4-member terrorist group in one of the country’s eastern regions, a security official said on Tuesday.

 Hossein Zolfagari, the deputy interior minister for security affairs, said the intelligence ministry forces confronted the terrorist team on Monday during which one of the terrorists was killed and the three other were arrested. Some explosive was also taken from them, he added. SP/PA
