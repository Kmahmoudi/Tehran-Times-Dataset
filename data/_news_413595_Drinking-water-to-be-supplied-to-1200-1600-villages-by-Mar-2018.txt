TEHRAN- In line with completing water supplying projects in the country, Iran’s Energy Ministry plans to supply drinking water to 1200-1600 new villages by the end of current Iranian calendar year (March 20, 2018), Tasnim news agency reported on Saturday. Iran has allocated over $639 million to building modern water distribution networks and implementing water projects in the country’s rural areas by the end of this calendar year. MA/MG
