TEHRAN – The 14th edition of Iran’s International Exhibition of Transportation and Urban Services is due to kick off in Tehran on October 27, IRIB news reported. According to the report, 20 renowned companies from nine countries will gather with 145 domestic firms to showcase their latest products and services at the Tehran International Permanent Fairground. The exhibitors will present their products in various spheres including commodity groups, public transport, commercial vehicles, machinery, utilities, construction and etc. The event aims to introduce achievements, capacities and capabilities of institutions, companies and municipalities across the country and also familiarize foreign companies with Iranian counterparts and vice versa. Japan, China, Germany, Sweden, Turkey, South Korea, Taiwan, Spain and the Netherlands will be participating in the four-day event.
