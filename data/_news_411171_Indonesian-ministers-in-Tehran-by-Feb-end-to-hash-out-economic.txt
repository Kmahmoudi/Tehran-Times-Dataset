TEHRAN- Indonesian Coordinating Minister for Economic Affairs Darmin Nasution and Minister of Energy and Mineral Resources Ignasius Jonan will pay a visit to the Iranian capital by the end of the current month to discuss development of economic ties with Iran, IRNA reported. As Nasution was quoted, the two Indonesian ministers plan to confer on expansion of energy ties, in particular, those in gas and oil sector, with the Iranian high-ranking officials. HJ/MA
