TEHRAN – President Seyyed Ebrahim Raisi has appointed Masoud Mir-Kazemi as his vice president and the new head of the country’s Planning and Budget Organization (PBO), ILNA reported on Wednesday. Mir-Kazemi, who was a member of the Parliament from 2012 until 2016 and also served ministerial post in the cabinet of President Mahmoud Ahmadinejad, replaced Mohammad-Baqer Nobakht who was the PBO head for the past eight years.
