TEHRAN- Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) will send an economic-trade delegation to Serbia, Poland and Germany on June 16, the official website of TCCIMA reported. The delegates active in the fields of foodstuff, technology, medicine, and constructional materials will discuss the possible areas for bilateral cooperation with their European counterparts during their eight-day stay in the mentioned countries.
