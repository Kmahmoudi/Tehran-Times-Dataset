TEHRAN- Russian Company Regiontransneft is in charge of a €2.8 billion-project on construction of an airport complex in Iranian central city of Qom.  According to Tasnim news agency’s Sunday report, the Russian company has commenced implementation of the project, which originally belonged to an Iranian holding company and plans to build passenger and container terminals in the airport’s location as well. According to the Russianconstruction news website, Regiontransneft has also embarked on the implementation of an integrated project for construction and launching of several real estate projects on Kish Island.
