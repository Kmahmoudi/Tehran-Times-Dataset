TEHRAN — In the afternoon meeting of the Tehran City Council, Alireza Zakani was elected as the mayor of Tehran, winning the highest number of votes in the 21-member council.
 In the open session of the City Council on Sunday, Zakani gathered 18 votes.  Zakani was a candidate for the presidential post but he withdrew in favor of Ebrahim Raisi.
