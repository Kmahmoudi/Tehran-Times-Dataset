TEHRAN – Iran’s deputy foreign minister for legal and international affairs has warned that mass destruction arsenals of Israel are a serious threat to the region.
 Abbas Araqchi told the 14th conference of the Asian member states of the Chemical Weapons Convention (CWC) that Iran condemns any use of chemical weapons, ILNA reported on Tuesday. SP/PA
