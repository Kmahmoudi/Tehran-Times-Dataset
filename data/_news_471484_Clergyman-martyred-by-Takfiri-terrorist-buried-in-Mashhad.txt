A funeral procession was held on Thursday for Aslani, a clergyman who was stabbed in the holy shrine of Imam Reza (AS) on Tuesday. He succumbed to his injuries on Thursday. People from all walks of life, officials of Khorasan Razavi province, as well as local officials of the shrine city of Mashhad, participated in the funeral procession.
