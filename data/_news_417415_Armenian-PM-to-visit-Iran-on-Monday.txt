TEHRAN – Armenian Prime Minister Karen Karapetyan will pay a two-day visit to Iran on Monday at the invitation of Iran’s Vice President Es’haq Jahangiri.
 Karapetyan will meet with Iranian President Hassan Rouhani, Jahangiri and the country’s Parliament Speaker Ali Larijani, according to the official website of Armenia’s Public Radio. The head of the Armenian government will also visit Ararat Sports Club in Tehran where he is scheduled to meet with representatives of the local Armenian community, including the business community.
