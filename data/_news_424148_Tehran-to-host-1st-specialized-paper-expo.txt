TEHRAN- The 1st international specialized exhibition of paper, cardboard, cellulose and related machineries will be held on July 28-31 at Tehran Permanent International Fairground, IRIB reported. The exhibition, which start operation under the Iranian Ministry of Industry, is aimed at protecting Iranian products and easing technological transfer in this sector into the country, the report confirmed.
