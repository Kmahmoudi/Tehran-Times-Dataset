TEHRAN – Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) plans to dispatch a trade delegation to Spain 24-28 September, ICCIMA portal reported. Headed by ICCIMA chairman Gholam-Hossein Shafeie, the delegation will be comprised of entrepreneurs and businessmen active in a variety of areas including railways and rail industries, food and agriculture, renewable energy, petrochemicals, pharmaceutical and medical industries, banking and insurance, automotive and manufacturing as well as industrial machinery.
