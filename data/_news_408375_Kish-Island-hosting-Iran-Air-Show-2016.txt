TEHRAN- Iran Air Show 2016, the 8th edition of the international event, which will be held until November 19 on Kish Island, south of Iran, kicked off on Wednesday in the presence of high-ranking Iranian officials, Tasnim news agency reported. As the news agency previously quoted Reza Ja’farzadeh, the head of Public Relations Department of the Civil Aviation Organization of Iran (CAO), the air show is to being held with 30 European and Asian companies from Germany, Italy, Russia, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Poland and Ukraine, participating.  On the way to provide a suitable venue for the participants and visitors to meet their potential counterparts and discuss their interests to explore business opportunities, the show is being held by Pars Kish Aero Exhibition Company, with cooperation of Kish Free Zone Organization and CAO.  HJ/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
