TEHRAN – On December 29, 2009, the Iranian nation “brandished its sword at falsity and injustice” to the revolution, Majlis Speaker Ali Larijani said on Wednesday.

 The nation on this day once again put the revolution on its right path and illuminated the country’s future, he said, IRIB reported. Elsewhere, Expediency Council’s Strategic Research Center Director Ali Akbar Velayati said the nation on this day heroically defeated the sedition. SP/PA
