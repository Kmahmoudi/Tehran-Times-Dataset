TEHRAN – “I Am a Victim”, a new novel by filmmaker Dariush Mehrjui, has recently been released.

 Published by Beh-Negar Publications, this is the third novel by the filmmaker after “That Bloody Receipt” and “Two Memories” published within the past year. The novel “I Am a Victim” is about Sima and Kasra, an educated couple that face a dilemma following their decision to immigrate to another country. In this story, Mehrjui wants to show how some people lose their lives in dream of migration.  RM/MMS All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
