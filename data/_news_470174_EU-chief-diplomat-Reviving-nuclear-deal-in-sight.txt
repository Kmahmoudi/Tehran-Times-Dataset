TEHRAN - The European Union’s top diplomat said on Monday a revived nuclear deal between Iran and world powers was “in sight” and urged negotiators to compromise. Josep Borrell’s comments came after a phone call with Iranian Foreign Minister Hossein Amir Abdollahian on the Vienna talks.  “Another important call with Iranian Foreign Minister @Amirabdolahian.  As Coordinator of the #JCPOA, I strongly believe an agreement is in sight. The moment has come to make an ultimate effort and reach a compromise,” Borrell tweeted. All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
