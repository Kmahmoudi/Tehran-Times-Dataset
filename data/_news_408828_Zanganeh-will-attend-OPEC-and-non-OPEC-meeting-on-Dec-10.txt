TEHRAN –  Iranian Oil Ministry's SHANA news agency reported on Monday that Oil Minister Bijan Namdar Zanganeh will attend the upcoming OPEC meeting with the non-OPEC producers which is due to be held in Vienna on December 10.  According to Reuters OPEC will meet non-OPEC countries to finalize the limit pact approved on November 30.
