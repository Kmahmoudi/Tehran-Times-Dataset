TEHRAN – A number of Iranian scientists issued a statement on Wednesday expressing support for guidelines issued by Leader of the Islamic Revolution Ayatollah Ali Khamenei for the second stage of the revolution.
 The statement was signed by 37 scientists in medical sciences. In a statement after the 40th anniversary of the Islamic Revolution, the Leader asked the youth to take “the second great step” for the progress of the revolution."  The Leader also insisted on “justice and campaign against corruption” in the next stage. The Leader also pushed for promotion of science and research; economy; spiritually and ethics; independence, freedom, and national dignity; foreign relations; and lifestyle.
