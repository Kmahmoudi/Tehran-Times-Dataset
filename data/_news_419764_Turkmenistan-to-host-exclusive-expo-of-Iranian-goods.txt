TEHRAN – The 14th exclusive exhibition of Iranian goods and services is due to be held in Turkmenistan’s capital Ashgabat on March 1-3, 2018, the portal of Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO) reported. Iranian companies active in various areas including electricity, oil and gas, petrochemicals, auto and spare parts, wood industries, as well as food industry, agricultural products, minerals and mining industries, construction materials, banking services, transportation and tourism will participate in this exhibition. Turkmenistan’s last year most imported products have been mechanical equipment, steel, electrical equipment, construction materials and metal products.
