TEHRAN- Islamic Republic of Iran Customs Administration (IRICA) and Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) signed a memorandum of understanding (MOU) on the joint holding of a training course for Afghanistan’s customs personnel, IRINA reported on Sunday. The MOU was signed by IRICA Head Foroud Asgari and Yukiharu Kobayashi, the chief representative of JICA Iran Office.    This MOU is a clear sign of IRICA’s successful cooperation with other countries, according to Asgari.
