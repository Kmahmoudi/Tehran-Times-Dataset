TEHRAN – Iran plans to stage five big war games in the coming months, which would see new missile and radar systems unveiled, a top military official has said.

 Khatam al-Anbia Air Defense Base Commander Farzad Esmaili told Tasnim on Sunday that the military exercises are going to be held from mid-summer until end of the current Iranian year (March 2017). SP/PA
