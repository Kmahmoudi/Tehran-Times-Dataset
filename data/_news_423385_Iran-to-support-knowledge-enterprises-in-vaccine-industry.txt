TEHRAN – Iran will support knowledge enterprises that are active in the vaccine industry, the secretary of Biotech Development Center (BioDC) at Science and Technology Vice-Presidency said on Saturday. Mostafa Qanei said that BioDC plans to increase the number of knowledge enterprises in the field of vaccine production, Mehr reported. “We plan to establish vaccine production lines both inside and outside the country through attracting Iranian and foreign investors,” he explained. He said that Iran will share vaccine production technology with other countries to develop the industry in the country.
