TEHRAN – The Australian ambassador to Tehran said on Saturday that his country never forgets the dark record of the Mojahedin Khalq Organization (MKO) terrorist group.

 In a meeting on Saturday with Mohammad Javad Hasheminejad, the secretary general of the Habilian Foundation, Ian Biggs highlighted Iran’s role in maintaining regional stability and fighting terrorism, IRNA reported. Australia supports international fight against Daesh and other terrorist groups in the region, Ambassador Biggs said, adding that such services are now underway in Iraq. SP/PA
