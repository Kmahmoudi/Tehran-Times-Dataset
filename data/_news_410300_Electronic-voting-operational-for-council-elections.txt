TEHRAN – The e-voting for the upcoming council elections has become final and is completely operational, said the head of IT committee at the election headquarters.

 “We are also in talks with the Guardian Council over if and how to hold the upcoming presidential election entirely electronically,” Amir Shoja’an told IRNA on Saturday. Shoja’an underlined that the Interior Ministry is fully prepared to offer electronic voting facilities at 10,000 polling stations around the country. SP/PA
