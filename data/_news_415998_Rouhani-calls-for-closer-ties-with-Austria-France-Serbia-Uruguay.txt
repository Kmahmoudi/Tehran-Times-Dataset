TEHRAN – In separate meetings on Wednesday with newly-appointed ambassadors of Austria, Serbia, Uruguay to Tehran, President Hassan Rouhani called for expansion of cooperation with their respective countries.  

 Austrian Ambassador Stefan Scholz, Serbian Ambassador Dragan Todorovic, and Uruguayan Ambassador Dragan Todorovic separately delivered their credentials to Rouhani. During the meeting, the Serbian ambassador delivered an invitation by his country’s president Aleksandar Vucic to Rouhani for an official visit to Belgrade. Tehran’s Ambassador of Paris, Abolghassem Delfi, also met with Rouhani before starting his mission. SP/PA
