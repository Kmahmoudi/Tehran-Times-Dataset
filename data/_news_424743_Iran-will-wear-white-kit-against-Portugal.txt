SARANSK, Russia – Iran national football team, known as Team Melli, will wear white kit in the match against Portugal in the 2018 World Cup in Russia. Iran will face Ronaldo-spirited Portugal on Monday at the Mordovia Arena. Carlos Queiroz’s side will advance through the group stage if they earn a win. Portugal will wear red. Iran defeated Morocco 1-0 in their Group B opener with their white kits. Team Melli lost to Spain 1-0 in their second match.
