He is carrying a written message from UAE President Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan al-Nahyan for President Mohammad Khatami on ways of developing bilateral relations in the interests of the two countries, IRNA reported. Sheikh Hamdan, who is Sheikh Zayed's son, is scheduled to meet with Iranian Foreign Minister Kamal Kharrazi. The two will discuss joint measures for the establishment of security and peace in the region as well as the world. Sheikh Hamdan visited Tehran in July, 2001 at the head of a 20-strong delegation to deliver Sheikh Zayed's congratulatory message on President Khatami's reelection.
