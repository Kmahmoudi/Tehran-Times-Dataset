TEHRAN- Head of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) Gholam-Hossein Shafeie will lead a delegation of Iranian traders and businessmen to New Delhi this week, the portal of ICCIMA reported. The delegation will make its four-day trip on February 14 to 18 to attend a business forum and joint meetings with Indian counterparts. The Iranian delegation is comprised of businessmen from various sectors including petrochemicals, chemical materials, bitumen, steel, and agriculture.  HJ/MA
