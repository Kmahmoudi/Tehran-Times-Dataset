TEHRAN – The chairman of Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee said on Monday that the rumors on the presence of one woman among terrorists who raided Majlis on June 7 are unfounded.

 Speaking to the Iran Consultative Assembly News Agency, Alaeddin Boroujerdi said security experts have not found any evidence on the presence of the fourth terrorist and strongly rule out that one woman was among the terrorists. SP/PA
