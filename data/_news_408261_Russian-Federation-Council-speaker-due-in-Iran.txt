TEHRAN – The Russian Federation Council speaker is slated to pay an official visit to Iran on Sunday to review possible avenues for fostering mutual relations.

 While in Tehran, Valentina Ivanovna will hold talks with senior Iranian officials including President Hassan Rouhani, Parliament Speaker Ali Larijani, as well as Foreign Minister Mohammad-Javad Zarif, ISNA reported on Saturday.  She is slated to discuss possible ways for fostering regional, international and parliamentary cooperation during her two-day visit.
