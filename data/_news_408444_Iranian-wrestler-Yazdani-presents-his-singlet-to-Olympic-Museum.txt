Iranian freestyle wrestler Hassan Yazdani Cherati presented his singlet who wore in the 2016 Olympic Games to the Olympic Museum. The Iranian wrestler defeated Russia's Aniuar Geduev in men's 74kg freestyle wrestling. Geduev had already defeated Jordan Burroughs, the reigning Olympic champion, in the quarterfinals. The Olympic Museum Collection mainly includes artefacts from the Olympic Games, items illustrating the links between the IOC and the Olympic family, artworks related to sport or the Olympic Movement, and Olympic stamps and coins.
