TEHRAN- Since the beginning of the current Iranian calendar year (March 20, 2016), some 153 trillion rials of facilities (about $4.04 billion) has been paid to domestic industrial sector, Iranian deputy industry minister announced. As IRNA quoted Hossein Ayoubi-Mehrizi addressing a conference in Kashan, a city in the center of Iran, the provided loans have been granted to 21,000 medium and small-sized production units of the Iranian industrial and agricultural sectors.
