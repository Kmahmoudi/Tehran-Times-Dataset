TEHRAN — President Hassan Rouhani will visit Zahedan in the southeastern province of Sistan-Baluchestan. According to provincial officials, Rouhani plans to inaugurate Zahedan International Airport’s second runway, an airline Co., Chehel Koreh copper mine and factory, and Iranshahr-Chabahar gas pipeline through a video conference, Mehr reported on Monday. Additionally, the president is scheduled to attend the opening ceremony of an urban gas pipeline and visit with the citizens.
