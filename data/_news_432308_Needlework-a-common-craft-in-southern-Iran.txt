A woman completes patterns of needlework on a traditional dress in Jask, a port city in southern Iran, January 26, 2019. Needlework (Souzan-douzi in Persian) is a very common occupation among females in southern regions of the country. It is the art of drawing images on plain fabrics by sewing delicate stitches using needle and colorful yarns.
