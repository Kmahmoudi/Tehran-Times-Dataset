TEHRAN- IFX, the main index of Iran's over-the-counter (OTC) market known also as Iran Fara Bourse (IFB), rose 1.1 percent to stand at 909 points in the week ended on Friday, Tasnim news agency reported. As reported, some 1.487 billion securities worth 9.165 trillion rials (about $242.460 million) were dealt in Fara Bourse market during the mentioned week.
