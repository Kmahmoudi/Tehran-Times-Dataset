TEHRAN – Iran’s Interior Ministry formally announced on Tuesday the start of the preliminary stage of city and village council elections. 
 Iran will stage presidential and city and council elections in May.  Accordingly, overseeing bodies will be formed and executive processes scheduled.  Registration will start on March 20 and will run on for six days.  AK/PA  All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
