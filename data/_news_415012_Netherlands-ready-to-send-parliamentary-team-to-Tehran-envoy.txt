TEHRAN – Susan Trstal, the Dutch ambassador in Iran, said on Tuesday that her country is ready to send a parliamentary delegation to Tehran in the near future.

 In a meeting with Hossein Amir Abdollahian, the Majlis Speaker’s special aide for international affairs, the ambassador highlighted the need for bolstering parliamentary relations, IRNA reported. Amir Abdollahian, for his part, congratulated the new parliamentary election in Holland and appreciated the Netherlands for condemning the June 7 terror attacks in Tehran. SP/PA
