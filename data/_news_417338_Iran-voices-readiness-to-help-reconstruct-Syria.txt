TEHRAN – Alaeddin Boroujerdi, chairman of the Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee, on Thursday voiced Iran’s readiness to assist the Syrian government in the reconstruction process in the post-war era. In a meeting with Syrian President Bashar al-Assad in Damascus, Boroujerdi said the Islamic Republic is ready to use its industrial and economic capabilities to help Syria, Tasnim reported. He also hailed Syria’s resolve in fighting against the plots hatched by the U.S., Israel and their regional allies in more than six years of militancy in the country.
