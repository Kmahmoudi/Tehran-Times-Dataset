TEHRAN – The speaker of the People’s Consultative Assembly of Indonesia is scheduled to pay an official visit to Tehran on December 4, IRNA reported on Tuesday.

 Zulkifli Hasan’s visit is to take place upon an official invitation extended to him by his Iranian counterpart Ali Larijani. Hasan and the high-ranking parliamentary delegation accompanying him are slated to confer with Larijani, President Hassan Rouhani, and prominent religious figure Ayatollah Nasser Makkarem Shirazi on issues of mutual interest.
