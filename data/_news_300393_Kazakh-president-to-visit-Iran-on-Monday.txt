TEHRAN – Kazakh President Nursultan Nazarbayev is scheduled to visit Iran on April 11.
 The two-day visit will take place at the invitation of Iranian President Hassan Rouhani. The Kazakh president plans to meet with senior Iranian officials to discuss bilateral relations and international developments. Nazarbayev will visit Uzbekistan and Turkey on April 13 and 14 after his trip to Iran.
