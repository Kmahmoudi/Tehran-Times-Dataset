TEHRAN - Mojtaba Khosrotaj, head of Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO), said the country plans to increase the number of its commercial attachés to 50 in near future. As ISNA reported, Khosrotaj noted that Iran currently has 20 commercial attaches in countries which are export destinations for Iranian goods.
