TEHRAN – The 16th edition of Iran’s International Paint, Resin, Coating and Compsites Fair (IPCC 2016) kicked off Tuesday at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds. According to IRIB news, during this 4-day event the latest products and services of more than 270 domestic companies and over 150 foreign participants active in the field of paint and related industries are being presented.
Switzerland, Germany, Austria, Italy, Indonesia, Taiwan, Ukraine, the United Arab Emirates, Turkey, China, Spain, India, France, the Netherlands, Sweden, Malaysia, Britain, South Korea, the United States, India, Russia, Lebanon, Thailand, Australia and Slovenia are among the countries participating in this year’s event.
