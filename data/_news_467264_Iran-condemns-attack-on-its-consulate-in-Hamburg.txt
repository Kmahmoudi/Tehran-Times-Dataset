TEHRAN – The Iranian foreign ministry spokesman has condemned an attack on the building of the Islamic Republic’s Consulate General in Hamburg.  Saeed Khatibzadeh demanded the German government fulfill its obligation to take action seriously and responsibly to provide security for the diplomatic missions of Iran in Germany in line with the Vienna Convention.
