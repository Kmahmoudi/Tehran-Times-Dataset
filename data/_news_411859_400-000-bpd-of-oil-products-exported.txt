TEHRAN- Iran has exported 400,000 barrels per day (bpd), equal to 64 million liters per day, of oil products since the start of the current Iranian calendar year (March 20, 2016), IRNA quoted an official in National Iranian Oil Products Distribution Company (NIOPDC). Making the remarks in a press conference on Saturday, Alireza Rajabpoor said that Iran targets exporting its oil products to Far East countries as well as regional ones while Pakistan, Armenia, Iraqi Kurdistan, Nakhchivan Autonomous Republic, Afghanistan, China, India and Sri Lanka stand among its major customers.
