TEHRAN- According to the acting head of Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO) more than $500 million of engineering and technical services has been exported in the first eight months of the current Iranian calendar year (March 21- November 21, 2018), IRIB reported on Wednesday. “About four trillion rials (about $95.2 million) of incentives has been paid by the government to spur exports of engineering and technical services,” Mohammadreza Modoudi announced addressing the first consensus meeting on the development of exports of technical and engineering services.
