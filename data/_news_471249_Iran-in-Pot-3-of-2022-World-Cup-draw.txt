TEHRAN – Iran national football team have secured Pot 3 in the 2022 World Cup draw. As Asia’s highest-ranked team, Iran are drawn in Pot 3. The 2022 World Cup will be hosted by Qatar from Nov. 21 to Dec. 18.  Japan and South Korea are also in Pot 3, while Saudi Arabia have secured the Pot 4. The draw ceremony will be held in Doha, Qatar, on Friday.
