TEHRAN- The Central Bank of Iran (CBI) announced that the inflation rate in the twelve-month period ended on the last day of the second Iranian calendar month Ordibehesht (May 21) stood at 9.1 percent, IRNA reported on Saturday. It is while the Statistical Center of Iran has put the inflation rate in the mentioned period of time at 8 percent.
