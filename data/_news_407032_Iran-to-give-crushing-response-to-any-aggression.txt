TEHRAN – Navy Commander Habibollah Sayyari said on Monday that the Iranian armed forces will confront any threat or aggression with a crushing response. “If they want to threaten us or create a challenge for us, we will give them a crushing and firm response," Sayyari told al-Alam. He also stressed his forces’ capability to safeguard the territorial waters.
