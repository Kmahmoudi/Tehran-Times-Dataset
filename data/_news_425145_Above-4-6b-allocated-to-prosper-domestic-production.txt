TEHRAN- According to Iranian deputy finance minister, the government has granted 200 trillion rials (above $4.68 billion) to protect domestic production projects in the present Iranian calendar year (ending on March 20, 2019). “Despite its budget deficiency, the government has allocated the announced figure to supply the working capital of the Iranian production units and support production projects with more than 60 percent of physical progress,” IRNA quoted Amir Bagheri as saying in a news conference.
