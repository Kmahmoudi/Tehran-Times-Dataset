TEHRAN- An envoy of the Leader of the Islamic Revolution visited the earthquake-stricken villages in Khoy county to monitor the ongoing measures. Hojatoleslam Abdolhossein Moezzi, the Leader’s representative, visited Khoy in the wake of the 5.9-magnitude earthquake that struck the city on January 28. He toured the earthquake-ravaged city to discover about the difficulties and challenges that exist.
