TEHRAN-Iran-Greece Joint Chamber of Commerce was established, Tasnim news agency reported citing Public Relations Department of Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA). Mehdi Jahangiri, the chairman of Iran-Greece Joint Chamber of Commerce, mentioned Greece one of the significant trade partners of Iran and stressed the need to lure Iranian entrepreneurs into cooperation for the expansion of trade ties with the European country.
