TEHRAN – Iranian police force have crushed three drug bands in Sistan-Baluchestan province, southeastern Iran.

 During the operations police also discovered 4,362 kilograms of illegal drugs, ILNA reported on Sunday. Police found one of the gangs in possession of 753 kilograms of hashish, 630 kilograms of opium, as well as some ammunition.
2,420 kilograms of opium was also found in the cars left by another gang who took to flight after the police chased them. SP/PA
