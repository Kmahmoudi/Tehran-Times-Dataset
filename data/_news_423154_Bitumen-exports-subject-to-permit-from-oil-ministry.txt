Tehran – Iran’s cabinet has passed a directive to subject bitumen exports to obtaining permit from the oil ministry. The decision was made during a cabinet meeting chaired by President Rouhani on Sunday in Tehran, Shana reported. Iran is currently exporting bitumen to numerous countries, including South Africa, India, Qatar, Malaysia, China, Afghanistan, and Iraq.
