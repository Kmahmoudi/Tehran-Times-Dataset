TEHRAN- Some 59 new wagons, made in Iran, joined Tehran subway network on Wednesday, IRNA reported.
According to Maziyar Hosseini, an official in Tehran Municipality, two of the new wagons are double-decker. Hosseini said that the new domestically made wagons will add to the capacity of Tehran subway in transporting passengers.
