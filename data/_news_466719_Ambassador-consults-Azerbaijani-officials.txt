TEHRAN— Seyyed Abbas Mousavi, the Iranian ambassador to Baku, consulted with Azeri officials on Friday.
 In separate meetings, Mousavi met with the Chairman of the State Oil Fund of the Republic of Azerbaijan and the country’s Deputy Foreign Minister for International Affairs.
