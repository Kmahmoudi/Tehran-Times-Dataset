TEHRAN — Iran’s Welfare Organization will construct some 113,000 housing units for the underprivileged under the coverage of the organization within the next three years, the organization’s director has said.
 Currently, some 53,000 houses are under construction both in rural and urban areas, Farid Qobadi-Dana explained, adding that, the organization is planning on constructing 113,000 more housing units in three years. Moreover, since the year 1394 (March 2015-March 2016) some 1,000 families with two or more members with disabilities have been provided with housing and some 6,800 more houses are under construction, Tasnim news agency quoted Qobadi-Dana as saying on Wednesday.
