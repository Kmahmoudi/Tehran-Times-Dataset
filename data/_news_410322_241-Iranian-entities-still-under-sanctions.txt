TEHRAN – The secretary general of the Islamic Coalition Party on Sunday said 241 Iranian people and organizations are still under sanctions.

 “In a report by the Foreign Ministry it is said that sanctions on 241 Iranian people and organizations will remain in place at most seven years and then they will be excluded from the lists of sanctions by Europe, America, and the United Nations,” Mohammad Nabi Habibi said. Habibi also said that banking sanctions are a clear sign of the violation of the JCPOA, the official name for the nuclear deal. SP/
