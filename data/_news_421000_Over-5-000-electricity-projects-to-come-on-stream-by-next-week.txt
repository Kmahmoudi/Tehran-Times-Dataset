TEHRAN- Some 5,317 electricity projects are scheduled to be inaugurated in the country on the occasion of Ten-Day Dawn (February 1-10, marking the victory anniversary of the Islamic Revolution), Energy Minister Reza Ardakanian announced. The minister said that 278.4 trillion rials (about $5.8 billion) investment has been made for implementation of these projects, Mehr news agency reported on Sunday.
