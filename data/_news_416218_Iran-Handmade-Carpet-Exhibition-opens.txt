TEHRAN_ The 26th Iran Handmade Carpet Exhibition opened its doors to the public at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds on Wednesday. A total of 730 carpet producers are participating in the exhibit which will run till August 29. The showcase will also include nomadic rugs and carpets. Iran exported $89 million of hand-woven carpets during the first four months of the current Iranian calendar year (March 21- July 22, 2017), showing a year-on-year increase of 3.7 percent in terms of value.
