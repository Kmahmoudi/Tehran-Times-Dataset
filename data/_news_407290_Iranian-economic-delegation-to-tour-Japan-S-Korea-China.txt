TEHRAN- Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) plans to dispatch an economic delegation to Japan, South Korea, and China to hash out fortifying Iranian businessmen’s economic ties with their Asian counterparts, portal of ICCIMA announced. The Iranian delegation will tour the named countries from October 30 to November 3.
The delegation’s visits to these three countries are paid following Iranian Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif’s trip to the said destinations. HJ/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
