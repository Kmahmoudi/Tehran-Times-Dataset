TEHRAN- Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) will dispatch a high ranking economic and trade delegation to China from December 24 to 29, 2017, the official website of TCCIMA announced. The delegation will be led by TCCIMA Head Masoud Khansari and will pay visits to two Chinese cities of Beijing and Shanghai, the reported said.
