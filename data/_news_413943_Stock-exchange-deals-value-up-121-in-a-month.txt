TEHRAN- The value of deals in Iranian stock exchange market rose 121 percent in the second Iranian calendar month, Ordibehesht (April 21-May 21), compared to its previous month, Farvardin, Tasnim news agency reported on Wednesday. As reported, the value of deals in Ordibehesht stood at 83.012 trillion rials (about $2.225 billion) rising from 37.538 trillion rials (about $1.006 billion), in Farvardin.
