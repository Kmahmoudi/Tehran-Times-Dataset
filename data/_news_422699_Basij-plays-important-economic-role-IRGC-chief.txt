TEHRAN - Major General Mohammad Ali Jafari, the commander of the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps, said on Monday that Basij plays a very necessary role in economic and social areas. “Basij has always made efforts in line with prosperity of the country and plays a very important role in various areas,” Jafari said in a ceremony. He added Basij played important role in defense area and it is essential to provide opportunities for it to be more active in social and economic spheres.
