TEHRAN – Iran and Belarus signed a memorandum of understanding (MOU) on expansion of medical and pharmaceutical cooperation, the portal of Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) of Iran reported on Saturday. According to Alireza Seyhoun, the commercial attaché of the Iranian embassy in Belarus, signed by the two sides’ deputy ministers, the MOU aims for facilitating medicine and medical equipment registration processes, joint production of medicine and medical equipment, transferring medical knowledge, establishing common rules for quality control as well as expanding mutual cooperation.
