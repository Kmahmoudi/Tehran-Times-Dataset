TEHRAN – The second edition of Iran’s international metal working exhibition (AMBIran 2017) kicked off on Tuesday in Tehran. Being held in collaboration with Germany’s Messe Stuttgart, the four-day event is hosting over 200 exhibitors from 20 different countries. AMB exhibition is considered to be a platform for promoting Iranian industries and will be an opportunity for facilitation of joint investments and knowledge transfer into the country. EF/MA
