TEHRAN- An Iran- Uzbekistan business forum was held at the Trade Promotion Organization (TPO)’s Morvarid Conference Hall in Tehran on Thursday, TPO portal reported. The so called forum was attended by high ranking officials from both sides including Iranian Deputy Industry Minister and Head of TPO Mojtaba Khosrotaj, Uzbekistan’s Foreign Minister Abdulaziz Kamilov and Foreign Trade Minister Elyor Ganiev and a handful of managers, company representatives and entrepreneurs from various areas.
