TEHRAN- Investment making in Iran rose 4.1 percent during the past Iranian calendar year (ended on March 20, 2018), Central Bank of Iran (CBI) Governor Valiollah Sief announced. The official also said that all economic sectors of the country have experienced value added growth in the previous year, IRNA reported on Friday. Iran's growth of non-oil gross domestic product (GDP) hit 4.3 percent in the past year, Statistical Center of Iran (SCI) has reported.
