TEHRAN- Iran-Brazil Joint Chamber of Commerce was officially opened in Tehran on July 9, IRIB reported. According to the report, Pedram Soltani, the vice president of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA), Brazilian ambassador to Tehran Rodrigo de Azeredo Santos, and Iranian Foreign Ministry's director general for the Americas Mohammad Keshavarz-Zadeh attended the opening ceremony.
