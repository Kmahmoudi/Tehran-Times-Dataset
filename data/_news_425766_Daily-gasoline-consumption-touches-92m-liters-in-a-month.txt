TEHRAN- Average daily gasoline consumption in Iran reached 91.7 million liters during the Iranian calendar month of Tir (June 22-July 22), 8.1 million liters more than the preceding month. The figure for average daily consumption in the mentioned period increased by 8.5 percent compared to the previous month, IRIB reported. As reported, the figure for daily consumption jumped to a record high of 102.2 million liters in the last day of the mentioned month (June 22).
