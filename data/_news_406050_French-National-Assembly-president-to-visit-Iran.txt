TEHRAN – The Iranian ambassador to Paris said on Saturday that President of the National Assembly Claude Bartolone plans to visit Iran soon.
 Ali Ahani also said a plan is underway to arrange a trip by French Foreign Minister Jean-Marc Ayrault to Iran. Bartolone was scheduled to take a trip to Iran on July 16. However, the trip was canceled because of a bloody terrorist attack in the French city of Nice. NA/PA
