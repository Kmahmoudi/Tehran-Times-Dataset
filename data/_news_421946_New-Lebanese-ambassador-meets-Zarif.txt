TEHRAN – The new Lebanese ambassador to Iran submitted a copy of his credentials to Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif on Saturday. Also on Saturday, Zarif met new heads of United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime (UNODC) and United Nations Industrial Development Organization (UNIDO) offices in Tehran. Also on the same day, the outgoing ambassadors of Zimbabwe and Vietnam to Tehran met with Zarif in separate meetings. During the meetings, which marked the end of the two ambassadors’ terms in Iran, they made their farewells to the foreign minister.
