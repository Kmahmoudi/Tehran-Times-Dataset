TEHRAN- Iran exported $183 million worth of home appliances during the nine-month period ended on December 21, 2017, according to an official in Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO). Masoud Kamali Ardakani, the director for Industrial and Mineral Products Export Development Office of TPO, mentioned Iraq, Afghanistan, Sudan, Tajikistan, Turkey and Italy as some export destinations of the products, IRNA reported on Wednesday.
