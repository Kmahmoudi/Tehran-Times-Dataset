TEHRAN – The commander of Iran’s Army has said Daesh will be wiped out in the near future.

 Daesh and other Takfiri terrorist groups will die out in the near future, Major General Ataollah Salehi said on Monday, according to Tasnim news agency. Highlighting Iran’s foresight in realizing threats, the general said the country’s top military officials had anticipated four years ago that enemies had plans to pose a threat to Iran from the western borders.
