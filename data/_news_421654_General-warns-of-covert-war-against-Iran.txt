TEHRAN - Ahmad Vahidi, president of the Supreme National Defense University of Iran, has warned against a Western-led “covert war” against Iran through infiltration.
 “Enemies seek to infiltrate into decision-making structure and impede the country’s progress and development,” he told the Mehr news agency in an interview published on Wednesday. He added, “Enemies seek to influence those who want to help the country progress and tend to fulfill the West’s demands.” NA/PA   All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
