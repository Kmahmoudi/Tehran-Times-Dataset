TEHRAN – Pakistan’s Army Chief General Qamar Javed Bajwa will visit Iran in coming days, said Director General of Inter-Services Public Relations (ISPR) Major General Asif Ghafoor on Thursday. Addressing a press conference, Ghafoor said, “We have contacts with Iran at civil and military level for better coordination on security issues.” The visit will further enhance coordination between the two countries, he added.
