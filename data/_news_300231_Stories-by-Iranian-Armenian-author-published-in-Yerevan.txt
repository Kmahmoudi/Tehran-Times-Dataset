 TEHRAN - Iranian-Armenian writer and translator Khachik Khacher’s “Three Stories in Three Languages” has recently been released in the Armenian capital of Yerevan.    The book, which is in Persian, German and Armenian, is composed of three stories: “The First Day of the New Year”, “The Lone Apple Tree” and “A man In a Basque Hat”.   AFM/YAW END All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
