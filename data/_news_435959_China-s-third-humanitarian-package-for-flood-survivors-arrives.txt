China sent essential supplies, including diesel generator, sewage pumps, microphone and amplifier, and tents to help thousands of people who have been displaced and suffered great loss as a result of the flooding in many provinces in the country. The plane carrying the equipment landed at the Imam Khomeini International Airport on Sunday, the report added.
