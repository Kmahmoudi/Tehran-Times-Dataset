TEHRAN- Iran and Greece signed an MOU on transportation cooperation, Reza Nafisi, director general of Transit and International Transportation Department in Iran Road Maintenance & Transportation Organization, informed, IRNA reported on Sunday. “The MOU was inked during the two-day Iran-Greece road transportation joint committee in Tehran,” he said. The 18-section-MOU was penned by Reza Nafisi and an official from Greek Ministry of Infrastructure, Transport and Networks in Tehran, the report confirmed. “The MOU seeks easing mutual transportation ties and removal of any barriers in this regard,” the Iranian official added.
