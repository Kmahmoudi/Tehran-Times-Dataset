TEHRAN- Iran-Czech business forum will be held on April 24 at the place of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA), the portal of ICCIMA reported on Monday. Both countries entrepreneurs and businessmen will take part in the forum, the report added. Iran and Czech Republic, in December, 2017, signed an agreement to encourage and support mutual investment. According to Shada news agency, the document was signed by Czech Deputy Finance Minister Ondrej Landa and Deputy Finance Minister of Iran Mohammad Khazaie.
