TEHRAN – Ayatollah Ali Khamenei, Leader of the Islamic Revolution, reiterated on Thursday that Iran will continue to strengthen its defense power regardless of the pressure by the West.
 “We will continue to strengthen defense infrastructure and will not give to pressure by others,” the Leader told a large gathering of people in Mashhad on the first day of the Persian New Year.
