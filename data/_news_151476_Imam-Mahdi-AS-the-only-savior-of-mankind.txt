TEHRAN -- Shaban 15, 1428 (Aug. 29, 2007) is the birth anniversary of the promised one and the only real savior of all mankind, the 12th infallible Imam of the Shia, Hazrat Mahdi (AS), whose followers look forward to his reappearance. Best wishes and felicitations to all Shia Muslims, especially you readers, on this happy occasion.
