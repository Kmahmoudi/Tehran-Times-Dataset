TEHRAN- Tehran Stock Exchange (TSE)’s main index (TEDPIX) gained 42 points to end Monday’s trade at 81,533, Tasnim news agency reported. As reported, 525 million shares worth 1.41 trillion rials (about $37.2 million) were traded through 48,000 deals in the stock market. Also, IFX, the main index of Iran's over-the-counter (OTC) market known also as Iran Fara Bourse (IFB), gained 8.8 points to 929 on Monday, as 168 million securities worth 1.46 trillion rials (about $38.5 million) were dealt.
