TEHRAN – The sitting government sidelined people in resolving problems, presidential candidate Mohammad Baqer Qalibaf said on Monday. Speaking on Jame Jam TV, Qalibaf said the government is not active enough, adding, “Society does not like the temperament of the incumbent government.” “One of people’s concerns today is the economic problem which has caused serious challenges in the social sphere,” the Tehran mayor reiterated.
