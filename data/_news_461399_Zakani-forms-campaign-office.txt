TEHRAN — Ruhollah Motefaker Azad has been appointed as the campaign manager of presidential candidate Alireza Zakani. In a statement issued on Wednesday, Zakani also has named Malek Shariati Niasar as his campaign spokesperson.  Informing the people on his Twitter account, he used #RevolutionaryYouthGovernment and #1980s_campaign.
