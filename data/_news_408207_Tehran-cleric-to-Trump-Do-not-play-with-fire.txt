TEHRAN – “I should tell the upstart president of America that if you go on the same path as your predecessors, you will end up like them,” the interim Friday preacher of Tehran said.

 “Do not repeat their mistakes. They were so arrogant…. Playing with Iran means playing with fire,” Ayatollah Ahmad Khatami said. Blasting Donald Trump for calling Iranians terrorist, the ayatollah called on him to apologize and said the Iranian nation will stand to the last drop of their blood. SP/PA
