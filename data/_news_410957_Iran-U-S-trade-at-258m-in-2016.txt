TEHRAN- Registering an 11-percent drop in comparison with the preceding year, Iran-U.S. trade in 2016 stood at $258 million. According to the latest report of American Statistical Association (ASA), the trade between Iran and the U.S. was above $292.3 million in 2015, while the figure currently stands at $258.1 million.  The value of exports from the U.S. to Iran in 2016 was $172.1 million, decreasing 38 percent from $281.5 million in 2015. The country’s imports from Iran in 2016 stood at $86 million showing an intense rise from $10.8 million in 2015.
