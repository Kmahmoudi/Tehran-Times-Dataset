TEHRAN – Armenian President Serzh Sargsyan has said that the Iran nuclear deal is of exceptional constructive nature, supporting the full implementation of the agreement, the Armenpress reported on Wednesday. “I am happy that France supports this deal which has been clearly stated by President Macron,” Sargsyan said in an interview with the French L’Express weekly. He also said Armenia’s relations with Iran with the history of 2,000 years are “vital for our country.” “Our political dialogue is stable, the mutual visits are quite regular,” he added. MH/PA
