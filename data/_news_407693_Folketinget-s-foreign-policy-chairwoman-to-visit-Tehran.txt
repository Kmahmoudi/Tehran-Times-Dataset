TEHRAN – The chairwoman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Danish Parliament (Folketinget) will visit Tehran on Sunday.

 Ane Halsboe-Jorgensen and her accompanying team will make the trip upon invitation from Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee Chairman Alaeddin Boroujerdi, ISNA reported on Tuesday. The Majlis has increased exchange of visits abroad over the period since sanctions lift. SP/PA
