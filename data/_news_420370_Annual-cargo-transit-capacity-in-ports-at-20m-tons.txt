TEHRAN- According to one of the deputy heads of Iran’s Ports and Maritime Organization (POM) transit capacity of Iranian ports have reached 20 million tons per annum, IRNA reported on Monday.
 “Thanks to the improved infrastructure, 15 million tons of freight are transported via ports of Iran,” Mohamamd-Ali Hassanzadeh told IRNA on the sidelines of a conference.  HJ/MA
