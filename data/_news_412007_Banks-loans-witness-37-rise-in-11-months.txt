TEHRAN- Iranian banking system offered 4.77 trillion rials (about $126.4 million) of facilities to domestic economic sectors during the first 11 months of the current Iranian year (March 20, 2016- February 18, 2017), showing 37.1 percent increase compared to the same period in the previous year, IRNA reported. According to a report by Central Bank of Iran (CBI), the figure stood at 1.29 trillion rials (about $34.1 million) in the preceding year. HJ/MA
