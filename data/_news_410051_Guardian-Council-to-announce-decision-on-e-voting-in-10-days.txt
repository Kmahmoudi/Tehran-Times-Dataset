TEHRAN – The Guardian Council will probably announce its decision about if and how to hold e-voting in the May 19 elections, ISNA reported on Saturday.

 The council’s spokesman, Abbasali Kadkhodaei, said experts are investigating the possibility of e-voting in the upcoming elections, which will include the presidential and council elections. Kadkhodaei also said women can run for presidency in the upcoming election. SP/PA
