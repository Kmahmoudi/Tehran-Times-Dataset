TEHRAN - The chiefs of the executive, legislative, and judicial branches held talks on Saturday night at the President's office. Furthermore, the chiefs of the three branches underscored that rioters' cases should be handled in accordance with legal and Sharia guidelines. President Ebrahim Raisi, Speaker Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf, and Judiciary Chief Gholamhossein Mohseni Eje'i further urged all authorities and others involved in the affairs of the three government branches to continue their efforts to serve the people and develop public confidence. It was concluded at the meeting that the 7th development plan and the budget bill for the calendar year 1402 (March 2023-March 2024) would be presented to the parliament at the agreed-upon time.
