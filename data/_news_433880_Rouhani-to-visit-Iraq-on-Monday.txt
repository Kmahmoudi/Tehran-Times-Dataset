TEHRAN – President Hassan Rouhani is set to start a three-day official visit to Iraq on Monday. Boosting ties between the two neighbors amid U.S. sanctions against the Islamic Republic is the main agenda of the trip, Mehr reported. Heading a high-ranking delegation, Rouhani will discuss bilateral ties and regional cooperation with Iraq’s president and prime minister, director of the presidential media office said on Saturday.
