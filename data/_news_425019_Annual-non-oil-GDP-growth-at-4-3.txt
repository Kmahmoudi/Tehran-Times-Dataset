TEHRAN- Iran's growth of non-oil gross domestic product (GDP) hit 4.3 percent in the previous Iranian calendar year 1396 (ended on March 20, 2018), Statistical Center of Iran (SCI) reported. The figure including oil sector reached 3.7 percent, the report added. Iran’s GDP grew by 6.3 percent during the Iranian calendar year 1395, according to the previous SCI report.
