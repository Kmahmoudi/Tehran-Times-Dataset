TEHRAN – Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister Abbas Araqchi said on Wednesday that the European Union’s political will to salvage the 2015 nuclear pact, officially known as the JCPOA, is still strong. “Yesterday and today, I held good meetings with political leaders and nuclear negotiators of England and France. The political will of the European Union and the three European countries to keep Barjam (JCPOA) is still strong,” Araqchi tweeted.
