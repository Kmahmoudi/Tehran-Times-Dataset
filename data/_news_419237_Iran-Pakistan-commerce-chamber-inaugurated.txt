TEHRAN - Iran-Pakistan Joint Chamber of Commerce was officially opened in Tehran on Sunday, the portal of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture reported. The value of trade between Iran and Pakistan hit $1.2 billion in the past Iranian calendar year (ended on March 20, 2017) increasing from $861 million in its previous year and the figure is planned to increase to $5 billion by 2021.
