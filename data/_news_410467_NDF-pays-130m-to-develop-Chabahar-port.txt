TEHRAN- The National Development Fund of Iran (NDF) paid $130 million to Iran’s Ports and Maritime Organization (PMO) for development of the southeastern port of Chabahar, the PMO’s CEO Mohammad Saied-Nejad announced. As Mehr news agency quoted him, by the present time, 85 percent of the phase one of Chabahar port’s development project has been completed and this phase will be inaugurated within the next two or three months.
