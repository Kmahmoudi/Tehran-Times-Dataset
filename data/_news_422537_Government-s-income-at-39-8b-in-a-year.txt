TEHRAN- Iranian government spokesman Mohammad Baqer Nobakht put the government’s income at 1.675 quadrillion rials (about $39.88 billion) in the past Iranian calendar year. Addressing a press conference, the official said the last year’s budget had envisaged the government’s income of 1.74 quadrillion rials(about $41.42 billion), Tasnim news agency reported on Tuesday. He said 1.19 quadrillion rials (about $28.33 billion) of oil income has been envisaged in the budget, of which 77.4 percent was gained.
