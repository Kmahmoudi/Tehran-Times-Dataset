TEHRAN- Some seven trillion rials (about $159.817 million) has been expended for development of transportation terminals at border locations in Iran during the past Iranian calendar year (ended on March 20, 2017), IRNA reported on Monday quoting Reza Nafisi, a director in Iran Road Maintenance & Transportation Organization. Iran has 23 terminals it its borders and development of these terminals is a top agenda for the Road Maintenance & Transportation Organization. MA
