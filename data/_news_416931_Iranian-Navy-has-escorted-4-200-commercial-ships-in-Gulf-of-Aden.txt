TEHRAN – The Iranian Navy has escorted 4,200 commercial ships in the Gulf of Aden since 2008 A.D., Navy chief Habibollah Sayyari said on Tuesday  Speaking to reporters, Sayyari said 25 of the ships were from other countries which had asked for help. The commander also said the country’s navy has been actively present in international waters since 2008. Iran’s flag has been raised across the world, showing the country’s power, he added.
