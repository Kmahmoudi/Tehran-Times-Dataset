TEHRAN - The 13th edition of Civil Engineering and Building Exhibition of Iran (CEBEX 2017) kicked off on Tuesday in Kish Island, south of the country. According to IRNA, the event is hosting over 90 domestic companies and 30 foreign participants from different countries including Italy, Germany, China, Turkey, UAE, South Korea, the Netherlands, Finland, Brazil, Switzerland, the U.S., Spain, Britain, Saudi Arabia and France. Basic construction products, architecture, interior decoration, outdoor equipment, mechanical installations in buildings as well as electric equipment and facilities, electronics and security, etc. are among products and services that are presented in this exhibition.
