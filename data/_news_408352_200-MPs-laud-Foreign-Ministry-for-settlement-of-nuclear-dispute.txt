TEHRAN – Some 200 Iranian lawmakers on Tuesday appreciated a tactful handling of the nuclear dispute with the West.

 Ali-Akbar Ranjbarzadeh, a member of the Majlis presiding board, read out a statement to that effect to the formal session of the parliament, IRNA reported. The statement was released upon a report provided by Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee about the implementation of the nuclear deal over the past six months. SP/PA
