TEHRAN – The Iran-Switzerland joint chamber of commerce is to hold an event titled “Iran Challenge” with the goal of promoting creation of new startups in Iran.
 The event is scheduled to be held in Tehran today aiming to help investors and businessmen interested in establishing new startups get acquainted with this area of activity, the portal of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture reported. In this line, the Iran-Switzerland joint chamber of commerce is going to hold similar events covering different fields at 2-3 month intervals, the report added. EF/MG
