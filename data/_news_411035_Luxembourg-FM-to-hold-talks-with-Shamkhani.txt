TEHRAN – Luxembourg’s Foreign Minister Jean Asselborn is scheduled to hold talks with Secretary of the Supreme National Security Council Ali Shamkhani Monday with a focus on closer ties. The two sides will discuss economic cooperation and will exchange views on the main issues in the Middle East and the larger world, IRNA reported. Asselborn last visited Iran in November 2015, during which he met top Iranian officials including President Hassan Rouhani and Foreign Ministry Mohammad Javad Zarif. MH/PA
