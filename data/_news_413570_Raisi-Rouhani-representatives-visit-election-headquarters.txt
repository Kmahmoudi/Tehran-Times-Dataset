TEHRAN – Representatives of presidential candidates Hassan Rouhani and Ebrahim Raisi on Friday visited election headquarters in the premises of the Guardian Council.

 Mohammad Reza Sadq represented Rouhani while Hossein Tala did so for Raisi, Fars reported. In the meantime, Interior Minister Abdolreza Rahmani Fazli said there have been no reports of any security concerns about the elections. He also said after polling is finished, ballot boxes will be opened and counting will start. Presidential votes will be counted first, he said. SP/PA
