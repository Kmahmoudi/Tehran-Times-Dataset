TEHRAN- According to an official from Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO), Iranian companies signed $465 million of contracts on exporting technical and engineering services during the first half of the present Iranian year (March 21– September 22, 2017), IRNA reported on Monday.  “The figure shows 1,000 percent growth, in comparison with the same period in the preceding year,” Mahmoud Eskandari said. Iran could earn $460 million from exports of technical and engineering service in the last Iranian calendar year 1395 (ended on March 20, 2107), he continued.
