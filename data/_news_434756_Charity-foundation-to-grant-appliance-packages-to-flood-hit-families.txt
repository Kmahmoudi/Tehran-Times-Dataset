TEHRAN – Headquarters for Executing the Order of the Imam also known as Setad-e Ejraiy-e Farman-e Hazrat-e Emam, provides some 18,000 packages consisting of household appliances to the families affected by flood, Mohammad Mokhber, the Foundation’s director has announced. “Some 4,000 packages worth of 200 billion rials (nearly $5 million) has so far been dispatched to Golestan province, which will be distributed in the next two weeks,” he stated. Each family receive a package consisting of a refrigerator, oven, blankets, carpets and dishes, worth 50 million rials (nearly $1,200), Mokhber noted, SNN reported on Sunday. The Foundation planned to provide 18,000 packages to send in other flood-ravaged provinces, as well, he added. Providing financially struggling families with household appliances, compensating for lost livestock, and generating jobs for people in the flood-hit areas are among the important measures taken by the Foundation, he highlighted. Some 3,000 jobs are scheduled to be created in Golestan province immediately after the incident, he added.
