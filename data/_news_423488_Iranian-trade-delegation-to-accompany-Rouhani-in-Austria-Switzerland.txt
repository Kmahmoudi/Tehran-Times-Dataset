TEHRAN- A delegation from Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) will accompany Iranian President Hassan Rouhani in his trip to Switzerland and Austria on July 1-6, the portal of ICCIMA reported. The delegation is led by ICCIMA Head Gholam-Hossein Shafeie, the report added.
