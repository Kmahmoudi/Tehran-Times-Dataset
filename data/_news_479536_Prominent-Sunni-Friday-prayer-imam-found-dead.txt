TEHRAN - Molavi Abdul-Wahid Rigi, a Sunni Friday prayer preacher of the Imam Hussein Mosque in Khash city was kidnapped and martyred by unknown people on Thursday afternoon. The prosecutor of Sistan-Baluchestan Province said that a special investigator has been assigned to follow the case while attending the crime scene.
