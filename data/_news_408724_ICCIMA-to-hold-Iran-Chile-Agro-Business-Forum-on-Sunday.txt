TEHRAN- Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) plans to hold Iran-Chile Agriculture-Business Forum in Tehran on Sunday. As the portal of ICCIMA announced, Head of Trade Office of PROCHILE for the Middle East Sharif Chacoff and Charge d’ Affaires of Chile in Tehran Fernando Berguno as well as some other Chilean officials will participate in the event at the ICCIMA building. 
The attendees will confer on Iran-Chile cooperation capacities in agriculture and business sectors as well as suitable ways to develop mutual trade and joint ventures. HJ/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
