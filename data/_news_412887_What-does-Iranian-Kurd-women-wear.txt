The costume of Iranian Kurd women is composed of gowns, base layers, head covers, and some ornaments.
 Jafi: A loose trousers, like what men wear. Kolonjeh: A vest either made from plain fabric, velveteen fabric, or sequin fabric. Gown: This is usually the dazzling masterpiece of the outfit. The dress is ornamented with many beads, sequins, or both. The embroidery can be the same color as the fabric, or multicolored to create a bright dress. Shawl: A beautiful colorful piece of cloth, which is used as waistband. Kelaw: A scullcap, which is decorated with colorful sequins.
