TEHRAN- The 1st Iranian Industries Exhibition is to be held in Iraq-Holy Karbala from December 25 to 28, IRNA reported. According to Iran’s Commercial Attaché to Iraq Naser Behzad, the exposition will be held at permanent place of international exhibition of Karbala and with participation of Iranian companies and factories providing 20 different groups of products and services including foods, medical herbs, carpets, heritage industries, household and administrative furniture, all kinds of plastics, building materials, alabaster and ceramic.
