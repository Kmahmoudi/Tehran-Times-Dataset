TEHRAN - The 7th International Exhibition of Office Furniture of Iran (HOFEX 2018) is due to be held on January 11-14, 2018 at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds. As reported by IRIB news, foreign exhibitors from different countries including China, South Korea, Italy and Taiwan along with renowned Iranian companies are going to showcase their latest products and achievements in this four-day event.
