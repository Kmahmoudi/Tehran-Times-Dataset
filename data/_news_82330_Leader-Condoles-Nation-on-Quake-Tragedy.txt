The Leader said that the tragic event which caused many deaths and injuries in the provinces of Qazvin and Hamedan saddened him. Ayatollah Khamenei called on all relief organizations to rush to the assistance of the quake-stricken people and asked the Iranian nation to help the officials in their relief efforts.
