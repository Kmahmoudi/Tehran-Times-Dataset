TEHRAN – The head of the Reformists’ Policy Making Council has said the body has not yet arrived at a final choice as presidential candidate.

 The council held a meeting on Sunday as every week, but it is too soon yet to speak about a candidate, Mohammad Reza Aref said on Tuesday, Etemad Online reported. He said the council will hold a press conference in the near future.  SP/PA
