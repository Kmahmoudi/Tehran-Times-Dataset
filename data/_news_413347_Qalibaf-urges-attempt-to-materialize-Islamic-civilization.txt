TEHRAN – “Let us all try and materialize the Islamic civilization,” presidential candidate Mohammad Baqer Qalibaf said on Wednesday in a meeting with ethnic chiefs and elites of Khuzestan province.

 The Islamic revolution is up to build an Islamic system and pave the way for worshiping God, he said. Also, the IRIB reported on Wednesday that Qalibaf’s campaign headquarters had filed a suit against Central Bank of Iran head Valiollah Seif for having used a governmental tribune to make accusations against the candidate.
