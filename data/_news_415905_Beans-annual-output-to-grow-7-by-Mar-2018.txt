TEHRAN- Iran’s production of beans is expected to grow seven percent by the end of the present Iranian calendar year (March 20, 2018), an official from the country’s ministry of agriculture announced on Sunday.
 As ILNA quoted Kaveh Khaksari as saying, Iran plans to produce 737,000 tons of beans by the said time, while its last year’s output stands at 690,000 tons. HJ/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
