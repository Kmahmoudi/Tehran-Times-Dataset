TEHRAN – Presidents Hassan Rouhani on Wednesday appointed two new vice presidents and an aide, IRIB reported.

 He reappointed Sorena Sattari as vice president and head of the Department of Science and Technology. Also, Rouhani appointed Mohammad Ali Shahidi as vice president and chief of the Veterans’ Affairs Organization. He also repositioned Masoud Nili as his special aide for economic affairs and secretary of the economic coordination headquarters. SP/PA
