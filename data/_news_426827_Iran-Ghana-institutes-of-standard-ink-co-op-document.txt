TEHRAN- Institute of Standards and Industrial Research of Iran (ISIRI) and Ghana Standard Authority (GSA) signed a roadmap document on standard cooperation in Tehran on Saturday, IRNA reported. The document was signed by Moslem Bayat, a deputy director at ISIRI, and Charles Amoako, a deputy director at GSA. On the sidelines of the signing ceremony of the cooperation document, Bayat told IRNA that a memorandum of understanding (MOU) had been signed between the two countries some three years ago for the expansion of trade between Iran and Ghana, based on it ISIRI and GSA were charged with investigating the common fields for cooperation and preparing a roadmap. “The roadmap which was signed today has scheduled each single program on the bilateral trade between the two countries”, he added.
