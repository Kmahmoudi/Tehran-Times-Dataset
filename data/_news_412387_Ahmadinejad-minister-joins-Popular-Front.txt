TEHRAN – A minister of the Ahmadinejad administration has joined the Popular Front of Revolution Forces, ILNA news agency reported on Wednesday.

 Ali Nikzad, who used to serve as minister of transport, accepted calls to join the front. The news agency reported Nikzad as having said he did so with the hope to facilitate the choice of the fittest candidate for president. SP/PA
