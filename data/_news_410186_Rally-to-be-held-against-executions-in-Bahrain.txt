TEHRAN – The International Union of Unified Ummah on Tuesday issued a call for demonstrations in front of the UN office in Tehran to protest the execution of three political activists.
  All popular activists are therefore invited to show their sympathy with the people of Bahrain to hold peaceful demonstrations in front of the UN office in Tehran on Wednesday at 15:00, it read.
