TEHRAN- Iran and Serbia have reached an agreement to launch direct flights between the two countries in near future, IRIB news quoted the head of Public Relations Department of Iran’s Civil Aviation Organization (CAO).
 According to previous talks and also a Monday meeting held between CAO Head Ali Abedzadeh and Serbia’s Ambassador to Iran Dragan Todorovics, regular flights between Iran and Serbia are to be launched in near future, Reza Ja’farzadeh announced. Iran Air, Qeshm Air and Air Serbia are the airlines which are going to operate charter and regular flights between the two countries, he added.
