TEHRAN - Iran and China’s Chambers of Commerce will jointly hold an Iran-China economic cooperation seminar on April 5, Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) reported. According to ICCIMA, Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) is also contributing to this event. A 57-member Chinese delegation comprised of senior managers from renowned companies active in areas including clean energy, technology, construction, iron and steel, coal, oil and gas, banking and insurance, investment, communications, aerospace, industrial equipment, automotive, transportation, and agriculture will attend the event in Tehran.
