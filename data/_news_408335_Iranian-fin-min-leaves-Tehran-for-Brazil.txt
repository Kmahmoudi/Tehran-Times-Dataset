TEHRAN- Iranian Finance Minister Ali Tayyebnia left the Iranian capital for Brazil on Tuesday to attend the 4th meeting of Iran-Brazil Joint Economic Committee, IRIB news reported. Tayyebnia will visit the Brazilian minister of development, industry and foreign trade, minister of mines and energy, and minister of finance on Thursday.
The meeting will be wrapped up on Friday and an MOU on further economic cooperation is planned to be signed in the closing ceremony. HJ/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
