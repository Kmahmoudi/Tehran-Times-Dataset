TEHRAN – The Iranian Navy commander has underlined that his forces along with the IRGC Naval Force are monitoring any moves inside and outside the country’s territorial waters and will not allow alien vessels to approach Iran’s borders.

 “The Iranian Navy, fully supported by people and taking orders from the Supreme Leader, doesn’t fear any threats and will be present in all arenas to safeguard the water borders,” Rear Admiral Habibollah Sayyari said on Friday, Nasim reported. SP/PA
