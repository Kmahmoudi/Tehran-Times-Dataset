TEHRAN – Iran’s Supreme National Security Council has approved a proposal to file a complaint with the International Court of Justice (ICJ) against Washington over a U.S. court ruling on the confiscation of the Central Bank of Iran's assets, the justice minister announced on Tuesday.
 Mostafa Pourmohammadi said that Iran will also try other options, including “overt and covert political ways”, to pursue the case. SP/PA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
