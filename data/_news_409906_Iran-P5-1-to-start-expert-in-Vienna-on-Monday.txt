TEHRAN – Iran and the P5+1 group will hold an expert-level meeting in Vienna, Austria, on Monday.
 The meeting will come as a preparation for a Tuesday meeting at the deputy foreign ministerial meeting, which will address Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif’s recent letter to EU foreign policy chief Federica Mogherini over U.S. extension of the Iran Sanctions Act (ISA) for another 10 years.
Iran’s deputy foreign ministers Abbas Araqchi and Majid Takht-Ravanchi headed to Vienna on Sunday to attend the meetings. SP/PA
