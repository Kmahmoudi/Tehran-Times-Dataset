TEHRAN- “Iran-Ghana Joint Economic Committee meeting is to be held in two months,” a managing director from Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) announced after a meeting between high ranking officials from the two countries, the official website of TPO reported. Farzad Piltan informed of the joint committee on the sidelines of the meeting between TPO Head Mojtaba Khosrotaj, who is also Iranian Deputy Minister of Industry, with some top officials from Ghana including the country’s minister of finance and economic planning.
