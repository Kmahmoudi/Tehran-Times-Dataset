TEHRAN- The 15th edition of International Exhibition of Poultry, Livestock, Dairy and Related Industries of Iran (IRNAPLEX 2016) kicked off on Thursday at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds. The four-day event is hosting 360 Iranian and 120 foreign companies from 20 countries, IRNA reported.
This edition of the event witnesses 20 percent growth in the number of Iranian participants and 15 percent rise in the number of foreign exhibitors compared to the previous edition, Hassan Rokni, the Iranian deputy agriculture minister for livestock products, announced on the sidelines of the exhibition.
