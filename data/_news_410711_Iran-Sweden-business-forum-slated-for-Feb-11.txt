TEHRAN – An Iran-Sweden business forum is scheduled to be held February 11 in Tehran, the portal of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) reported. A Swedish trade delegation comprised of entrepreneurs and business people active in various areas including auto industry, packaging, information and communication technology, materials technology, banking and investment will attend this event. There will be also business meeting on the sideline of the forum to provide an opportunity for the two sides’ businessmen to hold talks and discuss further cooperation.
