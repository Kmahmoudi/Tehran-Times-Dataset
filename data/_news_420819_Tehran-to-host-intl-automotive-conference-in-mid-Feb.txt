TEHRAN- The 5th Iran Automotive Industry International Conference (IAIIC) will be held in Milad Tower of Tehran on February 13 and 14, ISNA reported on Monday. As reported, representatives from the world’s renowned carmakers including Peugeot, Renault, Nissan, Mercedes Benz, Volkswagen, Hyundai, Volvo and Scania will participate in the conference. Chinese carmakers including Brilliance Auto and Changan Automobile will also attend the event. Meanwhile, the auto part associations chairmen of Germany, France and Turkey are going to participate in the conference.
