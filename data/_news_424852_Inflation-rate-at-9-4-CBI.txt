TEHRAN- The Central Bank of Iran (CBI) announced that the inflation rate in the twelve-month period ended on the last day of the third Iranian calendar month of Khordad (June 21) stood at 9.4 percent, IRNA reported. CBI had put the inflation rate in the twelve-month period ended on the last day of the previous Iranian calendar month of Ordibehesht (May 21) at 9.1 percent.
