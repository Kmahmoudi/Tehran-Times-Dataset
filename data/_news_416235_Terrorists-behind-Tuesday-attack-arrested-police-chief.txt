TEHRAN – The terrorists behind Tuesday attack in the southeastern province of Sistan-Balouchestan have been arrested, provincial police commander, General Mohammad Qanbari, said on Friday. According to IRIB News, Qanbari said the attack had occurred on Tuesday morning when two assailants stormed a government building, killing a soldier as well as a Basiji militiaman.   “Two suspects were arrested soon after the incident, as a result of close cooperation between police and the IRGC,” he said, adding that the suspects confessed their crime later on.
