TEHRAN – The commander of the Intifada and Quds Central Headquarters has announced the schedule of Quds Day.

 Ramazan Sharif on Wednesday said the rallies will begin on Friday at 10:30, ISNA reported. In Tehran, he said, the marchers will start from a number of pre-defined locations and end on the University of Tehran campus. The Friday prayer leader will be Ayatollah Ahmad Khatami, Sharif said, adding that before Khatami’s sermon, IRGC Second-in-Command Hossein Salami will speak about the recent regional developments, those of Palestine in particular.
