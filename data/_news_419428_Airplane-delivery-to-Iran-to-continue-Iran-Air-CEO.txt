TEHRAN- Iran Air CEO denies a circulating rumor that foreign aircraft manufacturers have stopped delivering Iran its ordered airplanes, ISNA reported on Sunday.
 According to Farzaneh Sharafbafi, foreign aircraft makers will not stop their deliveries to Iran for sure and new impediments put by U.S. treasury will not hinder implementation of the signed contracts between Iran and international plane makers.
