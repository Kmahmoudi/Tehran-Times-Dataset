The Head of the Cultural Heritage Office of the provincial city of Marvdasht, Mosayeb Amiri, told IRNA on Thursday that 21,000 Iranian and foreign visitors viewed the Persepolis Museum. He put the number of visitors who came to view the Naqsh-e Rostam at about 16,000, comprising Iranian and foreign tourists.
