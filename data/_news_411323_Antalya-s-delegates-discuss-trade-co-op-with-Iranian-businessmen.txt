TEHRAN- In a Monday meeting in the building of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) in Tehran, a trade delegation form Antalya discussed broadening trade and investment ties with Iranian traders and businessmen, the website of ICCIMA reported. Addressing the joint meeting, Gultekin Gencer, the president of Antalya Association of Industrialists, who headed the delegation, reiterated close ties between Iran and Turkey and called for creation of necessary infrastructure for the expansion of trade bonds between the two sides.
