Iran sells kerosene to Iraq at the oil price supplied in the Persian Gulf, Ziae Kashani told the Mehr News Agency, adding, the exported fuel is to be stored for winter. The contract to export Iranian kerosene to Iraq is being implemented for a month and will terminate next month, Kashani noted but did not elaborate further.
