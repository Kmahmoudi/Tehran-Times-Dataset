TEHRAN – The second edition of Iran’s International Exhibition of Horticulture, Inputs, Greenhouses, Related Industries, Machinery and Equipment (HIGEX 2017) kicked off on Tuesday at Boostan Goftegoo's Exhibition and Conference Centre in Tehran.
 According to IRIB news, during the four-day event, exhibitors from Italy, Germany, Turkey, China, Taiwan and the Netherlands are showcasing their latest products and services in the agriculture, forestry and planting industries alongside the Iranian participants.
