TEHRAN – The Iranian Air Force warplanes smashed simulated enemy targets by using optimized missiles with a high precision on the last day of Fadaeeyan-e Harim-e Velayat-6 drills on Wednesday.

 The F5, Mig-29 and Sa’eqeh fighter jets fired air-to-ground missiles against the specified targets after tracing them during the war games in the central province of Isfahan, Fars reported. Also, the strategic Sukhoi 24 jets targeted simulated enemy positions with optimized Kh-29 and Kh-25 missiles. SP/PA
