TEHRAN – Twenty-five works by a number of Iranian photographers will be showcased in an exhibition, which will open at the Vernadsky State Geological Museum in Moscow on January 31.
 The weeklong exhibition entitled “Beautiful Iran” will display some tourist attractions of Iran. The showcase has been organized by Iran’s Cultural Office in Moscow to celebrate the 38th anniversary of the Islamic Revolution. ABU/YAW
