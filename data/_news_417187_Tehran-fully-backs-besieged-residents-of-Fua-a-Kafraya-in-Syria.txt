TEHRAN – Hossein Amir-Abdollahian, a senior advisor to the Majlis speaker, has underlined that Tehran will do its best to provide the needed support for the Syrian residents of the Shiite towns of Fua'a and Kafraya who have long been under the terrorists' siege.

 
Speaking in a meeting with Hussein Ragheb al-Hussein, a member of the Syrian parliament and deputy chief of the Syrian National Dialogue Forum on Tuesday, Amir-Abdollahian said Tehran has always supported people and government of Syria in the fight against terrorism. SP/PA
