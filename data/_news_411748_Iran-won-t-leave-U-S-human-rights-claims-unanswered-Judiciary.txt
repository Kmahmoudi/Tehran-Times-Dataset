TEHRAN – The Judiciary chief has refuted U.S. allegations that Iran is violating human rights, saying Tehran will not leave such claims “unanswered.”
 Ayatollah Sadeq Amoli Larijani said U.S. reports about human rights situation in Iran are based on false reports provided by members of the anti-Iran terrorist Mujahedin-e Khalq Organization (MKO), Press TV reported on Tuesday. He dismissed as routine the U.S. Department of State’s move to level such allegations against Iran. SP/PA
