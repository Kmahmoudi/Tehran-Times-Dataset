TEHRAN – A member of the Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee has said the Arak heavy water reactor will continue to run in full force.

 “The Arak heavy water production factory goes on to work nonstop as it did before the JCPOA,” Mojtaba Zonnuri said after visiting the facility along with some other members of the committee, Fars reported. Iran’s commitments under the JCPOA to redesign the reactor have also been implemented, Zonnuri pointed out. SP/PA
