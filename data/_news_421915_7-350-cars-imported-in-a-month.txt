TEHRAN- Iran imported 7,350 cars in the eleventh Iranian calendar month of Bahman (January 21-February 19), according to the latest data announced by the Islamic Republic of Iran Customs Administration (IRICA). The IRICA data also put the number of cars imported to the country during the 11-month period ended on February 19 at 66,196, valued at $1.756 billion, Tasnim news agency reported on Friday.
