The Office of rural women of the Ministry of Agriculture Jihad has announced that women from the provinces of Ardebil, Isfahan, Tehran, Khorasan, Khuzestan, Sistan-Baluchestan, Semnan, Qazvin, Kerman, Kermanshah, Gilan, Lorestan, Hormozgan, Hamedan, and Yazd will display and sell their products at the exhibition.
