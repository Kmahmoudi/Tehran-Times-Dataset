TEHRAN – A new announcement by the secretariat of the Expediency Council said on Monday that Mohsen Rezaee, currently secretary of the council, has as of yet no decision to contest 2017 presidential election.

 Rezaee has yet made no decision as to run for president in the upcoming election and would like to invite revolutionary forces to beware of schism and unite around the “guardianship and clergy”, the statement said on Monday. SP/PA
