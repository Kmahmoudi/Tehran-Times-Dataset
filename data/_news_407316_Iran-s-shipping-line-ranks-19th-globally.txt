TEHRAN- The Islamic Republic of Iran Shipping Lines (IRISL) ranks 19th among the world's international shipping lines according to the latest report released by Clarksons Research's Container Intelligence Monthly, Tasnim news agency reported on Saturday. IRISL currently has the capacity of 92,674 twenty-foot-equivalent unit (TEU), the report says.
