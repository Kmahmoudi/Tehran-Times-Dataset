TEHRAN- Iran Association of Water and Wastewater Experts (IAWWE) and L'Association Internationale de L'Eau (IWA) signed an agreement on the sidelines of the 13th Iran International Water and Wastewater Exhibition (Watex 2017) in Tehran, IRNA reported on Wednesday.  The agreement which focuses on cooperation on renovation, expertise, and development in water sector between the two sides was signed by IWA’s representative Emilie Fillol and Qanbar-Ali Rajabi, the managing director of IAWWE. As reported, the agreement also seeks to strengthen cooperation on international research projects as well as international water expositions in Iran and France.
