The Iranian Army on Sunday started military exercises dubbed “Security and Power” in the Caspian Sea.

 The massive drills are underway in five stages, the commander of Iran's First Naval Zone Ahmadreza Baqeri said, IRNA reported. The first stage consists of planning and designing, the second one deals with holding briefing session and launching the exercise, he explained. The commander went on to say that after dividing the units into two groups, all exercises and joint operations will start.  SP/PA
