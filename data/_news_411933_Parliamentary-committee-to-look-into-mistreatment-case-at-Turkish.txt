TEHRAN – An Iranian lawmaker has said the Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee will follow up on the case of insults against Iranian travelers at a border crossing with Turkey, Tasnim reported on Monday.

 However, Hossein Naqavi Hosseini, the committee spokesman, said Majlis has not yet received any official report from the Foreign Ministry about the incident. The lawmaker also called on Iranians to avoid traveling to Turkey, citing security concerns. SP/PA
