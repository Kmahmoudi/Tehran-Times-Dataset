TEHRAN — Representatives of pupils’ unions met President Ebrahim Raisi on Wednesday. The pupils expressed their views on the educational system and political and cultural issues in the country. The meeting took place on the eve of Nov. 4, a date marking the seizure of the U.S. embassy in Tehran in 1980.
