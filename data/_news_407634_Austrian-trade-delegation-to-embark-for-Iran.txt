TEHRAN- A high-ranking trade delegation from Austria’s Vorarlberg Commerce Chamber is to make a trip to Tehran in early November, the portal of Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industry, Mine, and Agriculture (TCCIMA) reported on Monday.   The delegation comprised of Austrian businessmen and industrialists active in various fields such as house construction, textile, petrochemicals, machineries, services, and etc., will meet their Iranian counterparts in TCCIMA building on November 7 to negotiate future cooperation.
