TEHRAN- Over 200,000 enterprises were established in Iran during the past Iranian calendar year, which ended on March 20, IRNA reported on Tuesday, quoting Iran Chamber of Guilds Chairman Ali Fazeli. It shows that there is high potential for employment in the small-sized businesses, Fazeli noted.
He referred to preserving stability in the business environment and facilitating condition for starting new businesses as some of the necessities for small-sized enterprises and said the mid-term plans of Iran Chamber of Guilds in this due will come to result this year.
