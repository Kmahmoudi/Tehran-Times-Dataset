TEHRAN – On Sunday Iran will unveil a state-of-the-art tank dubbed “Karrar” which has been fully designed and manufactured by domestic military experts.

 Karrar will be unveiled in a ceremony to be attended by Defense Minister Brigadier General Hossein Dehqan, Tasnim reported. Concurrent with the unveiling ceremony, the production line of the tank will be inaugurated in Bani Hashem Defense Industrial Complex in Doroud, western province of Lorestan. Karrar is a class of Iranian tanks that enjoys modern technologies. SP/PA
