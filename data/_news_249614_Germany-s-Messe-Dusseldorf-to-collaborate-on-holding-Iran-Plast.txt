TEHRAN – Germany’s trade fair organizer Messe Dusseldorf has signed a cooperation with the Iranian state-owned National Petrochemical Company (NPC) to collaborate on the upcoming ""Iran Plast"" fair, to be held in Tehran from April 13-17, 2016, according to the Plasteurope.com website.
