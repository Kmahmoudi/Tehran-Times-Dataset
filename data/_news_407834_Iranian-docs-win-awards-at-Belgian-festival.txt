TEHRAN – Acclaimed Iranian documentaries “Starless Dreams” by Mehrdad Oskui and “Sonita” by Rokhsareh Qaem-Maqami won awards at the Festival des Libertés, which was held in Brussels, Belgium from October 20 to 29. “Starless Dreams” won the SMart prize while “Sonita” received the RTBF prize, Belgian National Television prize, at the festival. The German movie “#MyEscape” by Elke Sasse won the Grand Prix of the festival.  ABU/YAW All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
