TEHRAN- According to the portal of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA), the Iranian private body will dispatch its trade delegation to Georgia on October 8, 2017 to attend the 6th meeting of Iran-Georgia Economic Committee in Tbilisi.
 The delegation is led by Hossein Salah-Varzi the deputy head of ICIMMA from October 8 to 11 to the Georgian capital, the report added. Iranian Labor Minister Ali Rabiei will also attend the mentioned meeting. HJ/MA
