TEHRAN – Presidential hopefuls and city council candidates can continue their campaigns to 8:00 in the morning of Thursday and then they will have to stop it according to the law, the secretary of the state elections headquarters said on Monday.

 Ali Pourali Motlaq also said that 63,429 polling stations are being staged across the country for presidential and council elections and there is “no problem”.  According to Pourali Motlaq, there are also 1060 executive bodies to carry out the affairs related to the Friday elections, Fars reported.
