TEHRAN – Iranian Minister of Cooperatives, Labor and social Welfare Ali Rabiei said Croatia is willing to invest in Iran’s power industry and also collaboration in making electric rail wagons in the country, IRNA reported. The minister who visited the Eastern European country heading a trade-social delegation met with senior Croatian officials including Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Economy, Entrepreneurship and Crafts Martina Dalic, and Minister of Labor and Pension System Marko Pavic.
