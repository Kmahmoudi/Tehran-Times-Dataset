Iran and Canada volleyball federations signed memorandum of understanding (MoU) on Monday.  Mohammad Reza Davarzani, head of Iran’s Volleyball Federation and Mark Eckert, President of Canadian Volleyball Federation, signed the deal during the 35th FIVB World Congress in Buenos Aires, Argentina.
The cooperation protocol will strengthen ties between the two countries and increase in the rate of collaborations at various levels.
“This agreement will strength the relations between the two federations, in addition to boosting the volleyball standards in both countries,” Davarzani said.
Iran Volleyball Federation has already signed MoU with the U.S., Serbia, France, Germany, Poland and Russia federations.
