TEHRAN – Iranian Majlis speaker Ali Larijani condemned on Friday the Friday terrorist blast in Afghanistan, saying that Iran stands beside the Afghan government and people in fighting terrorism.
 Iran spares no effort in helping the Afghan government and people to fight terrorism and establish peace in their country, he said in separate messages to Speaker of the Afghanistan’s House of the People Abdul Rauf Ibrahimi and Fazel Hadi Muslimyar, the speaker of Afghanistan’s Upper House. A huge bomb blast in Kabul, Afghanistan, on Wednesday killed at least 90 people and injured 300 others.  Daesh claimed responsibility for the suicide bombing, which struck Kabul’s diplomatic quarter.
