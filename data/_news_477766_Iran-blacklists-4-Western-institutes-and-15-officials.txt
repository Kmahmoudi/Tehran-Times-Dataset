TEHRAN - Foreign Minister Hossein Amir Abdollahian announced in a press conference on Wednesday that at least 4 Western institutes and 15 officials will be added to the Iranian Foreign Ministry’s blacklist involved in sanctions against the Islamic Republic and also inciting violent and extremist actions in the recent unrest in the country. The institutes and individuals are from the European Union and the United States. Amir Abdollahian said the details of the list will be announced soon.  He broke the story while visiting epidermolysis bullosa (EB) patients in Tehran.
