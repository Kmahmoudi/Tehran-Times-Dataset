TEHRAN – In a blast on Sunday two IRGC members were injured in southeastern province of Sistan-Baluchestan.

 The incident occurred on morning while an explosive trap was blasted in the Kouhak region near Saravan city, Tasnim reported. One of the wounded guards is reported to be in serious condition. Sistan-Baluchestan province has witnessed frequent clashes between forces and drug smugglers as well as terror gangs venturing into the Iranian territory in the past few years. SP/PA
