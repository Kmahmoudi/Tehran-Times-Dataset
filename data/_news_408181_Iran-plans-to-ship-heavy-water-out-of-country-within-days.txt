TEHRAN – Iran plans to ship sensitive material out of the country within days to bring it back under a soft limit set under its nuclear deal, Reuters quoted an unnamed diplomat as having said on Wednesday.

 A report by the IAEA on Wednesday said that Iran was just above the limit of 130 tons on its heavy water stock set by the deal, and that the agency had expressed its concern to Tehran over the issue. SP/PA
