TEHRAN – Iran will establish an international center for reporters, an official at the Ministry of Culture and Islamic Guidance announced on Friday, IRNA reported. The center will be established in the capital Tehran to facilitate exchange of news among reporters, said Alireza Shiravi, director of the foreign media department at the ministry. He added that the center will hold training courses for reporters and will facilitate their visit to Iranian provinces. Holding news conferences and assisting the reporters to do their interviews in the best way will be the other aims of the international center, Shiravi said.
