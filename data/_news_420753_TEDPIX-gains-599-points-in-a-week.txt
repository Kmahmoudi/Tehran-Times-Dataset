TEHRAN- Tehran Stock Exchange (TSE)’s main index (TEDPIX) gained 599 points to 99.522 during the five dealing days (Saturday-Wednesday) of the past Iranian week ended on Friday, IRIB news reported. Some 7.231 billion securities worth 21.864 trillion rials (about $481.47 million) were traded through 338,000 deals in TSE, indicating 41 percent and 73 percent rise in the number and value, respectively, while 0.3 percent rise in the number of deals. The first market’s index rose 94 points to 70.804 and second market’s index added 3 points to 211.346, showing 0.1 percent and 1.4 percent growth, respectively, in the mentioned week. TEDPIX gained 3,309 points or 3.5 percent to 98.817 in the tenth Iranian calendar month of Dey (December 22, 2017-January 20, 2018), according to Tasnim news agency.
