TEHRAN – New UAVs will be unveiled by the Army’s ground forces in the current Iranian year, which comes to an end on March 20, the ground forces commander said on Thursday.  Brigadier General Kiomars Heidari said the UAVs will be employed in new flight links, Mehr reported. Heidari underlined the uniqueness of the Army’s drones for defense and military purposes, adding the ground forces are capable of producing drones.
