TEHRAN – The Iranian ICT Guild Organization invites startups to cooperate with sports federations in order to develop sports websites and applications, Shanbepress reported on Wednesday. A number of sports federations plan to provide information and education courses through applications and websites with the help of startups, Iranian ICT Guild Organization announced. The federations plans to give information about some professional job vacancies like referee and coaching through apps and websites. According to the Iranian ICT Guild Organization it paves the way for the startups to cooperate in sport-economy, which has remained disregarded in Iran’s startup ecosystem up to now.
