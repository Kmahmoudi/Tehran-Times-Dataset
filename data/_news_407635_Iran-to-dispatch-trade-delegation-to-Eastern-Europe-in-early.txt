TEHRAN – According to an announcement by Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA), a high ranking trade delegation headed by Iran’s Minister of Foreign Affairs Mohammad Javad Zarif will leave Tehran for Eastern Europe on November 7. Aiming for development of economic relations and the realization of post-JCPOA economic goals, the delegation will visit Bulgaria, Hungary, Slovakia and Czech Republic during November 7 to 11.
Senior officials and representatives of Iranian companies in various spheres such as banking, energy, transportation, medical and pharmaceutical products, mining, automobile and agricultural machinery will be accompanying the minister.
