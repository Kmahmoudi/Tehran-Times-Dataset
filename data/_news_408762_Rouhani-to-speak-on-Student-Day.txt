TEHRAN – President Hassan Rouhani is scheduled to visit the University of Tehran on December 6 on the occasion of Student Day in Iran. The president is expected to answer questions from student union representatives as has been customary in previous years, ISNA reported on Saturday. Last year Rouhani visited the Sharif University of Technology on December 6. The day commemorates the murder of 3 students in 1953 by police in the Pahlavi era. SP/PA
