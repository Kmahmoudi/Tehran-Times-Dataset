TEHRAN – Russian President Vladimir Putin arrived in Tehran on Wednesday to attend a trilateral Iran-Russia-Azerbaijan summit meeting.
 Azeri President Ilham Aliyev arrived in Tehran earlier on Wednesday and held talks with President Rouhani of Iran.  Rouhani, Putin and Aliyev held their first tripartite summit in August 2016 in Azerbaijan’s capital Baku. The leaders are scheduled to discuss regional cooperation. NA/PA
