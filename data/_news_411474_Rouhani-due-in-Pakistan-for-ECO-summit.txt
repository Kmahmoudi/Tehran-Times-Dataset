TEHRAN – President Hassan Rouhani is slated to visit Pakistan on Tuesday to attend the 13th meeting of the Economic Cooperation Organization. Parviz Esmaeili, the deputy for communications and information at the president’s office, said on Sunday that Rouhani’s two-day trip will come at the invitation of Pakistani Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif, President.ir reported. Esmaeili added that Rouhani is planned to deliver a speech at the summit, which will be held in Islamabad on March 1. SP/PA
