TEHRAN- Tehran Stock Exchange (TSE)’s main index (TEDPIX) gained 1,783 points to end Sunday’s trade at 180,625, IRNA reported. As reported, 2.121 billion shares worth 6.156 trillion rials (about $146.5 million) were traded through 160,684 deals in the stock market. The first market’s index gained 1,308 points and the second market’s index added 3,582 points on Sunday. Also, IFX, the main index of Iran's over-the-counter (OTC) market known also as Iran Fara Bourse (IFB), rose four points to 2,013 on Sunday, as 570 million securities worth 4.181 trillion rials (about $99.5 million) were traded.
