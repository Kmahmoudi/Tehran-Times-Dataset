TEHRAN – Iran has taken big strides in manufacturing top attack weapons and advanced anti-armor missiles, a senior military official said on Tuesday.

 “Important measures have been taken in the field of top attack (weapons) and producing smart rockets and anti-armor missiles and with different extra capabilities,” Ground Force’s Research and Self-Sufficiency chief Massoud Reza Zavarei told Fars. “We have adopted proper measures in the field of anti-armor and new weapons will be unveiled gradually,” he added.
