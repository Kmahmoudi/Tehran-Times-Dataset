TEHRAN – The Iranian navy plans to unveil its achievements by the end of the current calendar year which ends on March 20, 2018. Speaking on Saturday, Navy Commander Rear Admiral Habibollah Sayyari said Iran does not need to buy military equipment from other countries. “We’re in a place where we can manufacture all navy equipment including submarines and destroyers” “We need to carry out a number of tests on the new equipment before unveiling them,” the commander added.
