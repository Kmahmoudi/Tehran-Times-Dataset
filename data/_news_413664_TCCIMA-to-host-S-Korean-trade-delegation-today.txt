TEHRAN- Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) will host a South Korean trade delegation in its building today, the official website of TCCIMA announced. The delegates led by the chairman of Busan Chamber of Commerce and Industry are active in the fields of maritime, ship building, marine structures,  oil, gas, and petrochemicals as well as offshore projects.
