TEHRAN- Managing Director of Ports and Maritime Department of Iran’s Hormozgan Province announced on Wednesday that tens of projects worth 30 trillion rials (about $799 million) are underway in the country’s southern Shahid Rajaee Port, IRNA reported. Among these projects, Allah-Morad Afifipour, referred to establishment of three oil terminals as well as access roads at the value of 600 billion rials (about $15.9 million). Having the docks constructed within the next two years, Shahid Rajaee’s capacity of oil products exports will increase up to 34 million tons.
