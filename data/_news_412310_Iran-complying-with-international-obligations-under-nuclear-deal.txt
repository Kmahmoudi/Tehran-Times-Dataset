TEHRAN – Iran’s vice president for parliamentary affairs has said the Islamic Republic has been and will be complying with all its obligations under the international nuclear deal, press TV reported

 Speaking in a meeting with Hungarian Deputy Prime Minister Zsolt Semjén in Tehran on Monday, Hossein Ali Amiri pointed out that some countries are still stonewalling the implementation of the nuclear deal, warning that such measures “would undoubtedly not be in the interests of the international community”.  SP/PA
