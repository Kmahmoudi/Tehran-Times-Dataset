TEHRAN- Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO) is to dispatch a trade, marketing, and investment delegation to Azerbaijan from May 31 to June 3, the official website of TPO announced.  The visit will be paid at the same time as the 24th Caspian International Oil & Gas Exhibition and seems a proper chance for Iranian companies to boost their oil and gas ties with countries attending the exhibition.
