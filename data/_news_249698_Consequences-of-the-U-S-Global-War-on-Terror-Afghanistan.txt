Within a month of the attacks on the United States of America, which occurred on 11 September 2001, Washington launched its Global War on Terror (GWOT), which remains ongoing to this day. The official purpose of the GWOT is to confront and combat terrorism anywhere in the world that threatens the United States, its interests, or its allies.
