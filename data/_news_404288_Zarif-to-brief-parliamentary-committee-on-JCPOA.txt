TEHRAN – Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zari will be briefing the Parliament National Security and Foreign Policy Committee on the quality of the implementation of the nuclear deal, officially called JCPOA.  
According to Hajar Chenarani, a member of the committee, the session will affect future decisions by the Islamic Republic regarding to the nuclear deal signed last year on July 14.
