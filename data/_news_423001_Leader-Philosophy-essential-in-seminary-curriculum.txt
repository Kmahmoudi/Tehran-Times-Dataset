Ayatollah Ali Khamenei made the remarks at a meeting with the organizers of a national philosophy conference on April 23, the content of which was published on Wednesday. In the gathering, Ayatollah Khamenei expressed delight over the promotion of rational sciences in seminary schools after the Islamic Revolution of 1979, describing critical thinking and innovation as factors in promotion of philosophy, according to Khamenei.ir.
