TEHRAN – The chairman of the Iranian Majlis National Security and Foreign Policy Committee has reiterated Tehran’s support for a political solution to Iraq’s dilemmas.

 “Iran supports opportunities for negotiations to settle problems in Iraq,” Alaeddin Boroujerdi said on Sunday, addressing a conference titled “Baghdad Dialogue Conference and Post-Daesh Iraq”, Fars news agency reported. The senior legislator, in the meantime, voiced Iran’s readiness to share parliamentary experiences with Iraq. SP/PA
