TEHRAN – The 17th edition of Iran’s International Paint, Resin, Coating and Composites Fair (IPCC 2017) kicked off on Friday at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds. According to IRIB news, during this four-day event the latest products and services of more than 250 domestic companies and over 140 foreign participants active in the field of paint and related industries are being presented. Switzerland, Germany, Austria, Italy, Indonesia, Taiwan, Ukraine, the United Arab Emirates, Turkey, China, Spain, India, France, the Netherlands, Sweden, Malaysia, Britain, South Korea, the United States, India, Russia, Belarus, Denmark, Australia and Belgium are among the countries participating in this year’s event.
