TEHRAN – The 10th Tehran International Tourism Exhibit, adopting the motto of “See Iran Differently”, will open its doors to the public from Feb. 6 to 9. Organized by the Cultural Heritage, Tourism and Handicrafts Organization, fair is to bring the country under the spotlight in terms of its lavish natural and historical sights. “The number of foreign participants [this year] has increased 158 percent compared to the year before,” Farhad Aminian who is secretary of the exhibit said in Oct. 2016.
