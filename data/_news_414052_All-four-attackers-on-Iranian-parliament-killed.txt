TEHRAN – Security forces succeeded to kill all the four terrorists who attacked the Iranian parliament on Wednesday morning. The two terrorists who also attacked the shrine of Imam Khomeini in southern Iran were killed, one by security forces and the other while detonating a suicide vest. In the two coordinated attacks, 12 Iranians were killed and 42 others injured, emergency officials said. ISIS has claimed responsibility for the attacks.
