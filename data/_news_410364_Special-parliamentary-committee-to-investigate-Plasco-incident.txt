TEHRAN – A special committee of parliamentarians from the Tehran constituency will investigate the collapse of the 17th-story Plasco building in a huge inferno on January 19, top MP Mohammad Reza Aref said on Monday.

 “We will hold sessions with organizations which are engaged with the incident and review their measures,” Aref stated, Mehr reported. Interior Minister Abdolreza Rahmani Fazli also said on Monday that a special committee of ministers has decided to launch operations to identify vulnerable buildings similar to Plasco and include them in a list of buildings in need of structural reinforcement.
