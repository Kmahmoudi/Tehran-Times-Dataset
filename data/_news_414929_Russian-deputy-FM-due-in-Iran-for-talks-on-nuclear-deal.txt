TEHRAN – Russia’s deputy foreign minister plans to pay a visit to Tehran to hold talks with Iranian officials ahead of a scheduled meeting of the Iran-5+1 Joint Committee tasked to monitor the implementation of the international nuclear deal, officially called the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA).

 Heading a political team, Sergey Ryabkov will arrive in Tehran on Tuesday and meet with Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister for Legal and International Affairs Abbas Araqchi, Press TV reported. During his day-long stay in Tehran, Ryabkov would hold talks on the JCPOA and Tehran-Moscow relations. SP/PA
