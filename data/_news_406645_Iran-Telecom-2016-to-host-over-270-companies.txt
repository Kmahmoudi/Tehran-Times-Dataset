TEHRAN- Over 200 Iranian and 70 foreign companies will participate in 17th International Exhibition of Telecommunications, Information Technology & Innovative CIT Solutions of Iran (Iran Telecom 2016), ISNA reported on Friday quoting Davoud Zare’ian, the spokesman of Telecommunication Company of Iran. The exhibition will be inaugurated in the presence of Iranian Communications and Information Technology Minister Mahmoud Vaezi at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds on September 25 and will run for four days. 
Zare’ian also announced that three new areas namely e-government, national data base, and fiber-optic communication will be added to this year’s edition of the event. MA/MG All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
