TEHRAN- The 19th International Exhibition of Household Appliances of Iran kicked off at Tehran Permanent International Fairground on Tuesday, IRNA reported. As reported, 394 Iranian and 27 foreign companies are showcasing their latest products and technology during the four-day event. Germany, China and Turkey are among the countries attending this edition of the international exhibition.
