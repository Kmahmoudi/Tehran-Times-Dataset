TEHRAN- Iran will set up a pavilion in Oman’s 8th International Exhibition for Infrastructure and Industrial Projects (Infra Oman 2018) which is planned to be held in Oman Convention and Exhibition Center in Muscat from October 8 to 10, Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) of Iran announced. Infra Oman, as one of the largest building and construction expos in the Middle East, is an ideal starting point not only for the local companies, but also for foreign entities that are more determined to be part of Oman’s growing market.
