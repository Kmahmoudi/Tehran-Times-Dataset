TEHRAN - Brigadier General Hossein Salami, the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps (IRGC)’s second-in-command, said on Thursday that enemies have become united to undermine the Islamic Revolution and its system.
 “It has been for many years that all of our enemies have become united and active and have adopted strategies and policies to defeat our revolution and system,” he remarked. The general added, “It has been for many years that we are in a full-scale war against all the evils in the world.” NA/PA   All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
