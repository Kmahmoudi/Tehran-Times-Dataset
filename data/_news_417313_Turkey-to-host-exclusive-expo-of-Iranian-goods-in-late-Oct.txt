TEHRAN - An exclusive exhibition of Iranian goods and services is due to be held in Turkish capital Istanbul on October 25-28, IRNA reported on Wednesday.
 According to the report, the opening ceremony is going to be attended by Iranian and Turkish industry ministers. Iranian companies active in various areas including electricity, oil and gas, petrochemicals, automotive and spare parts, wood industries, as well as food industry, agricultural products, minerals and mining industries, construction materials, banking services, transportation and tourism will participate in this exhibition.
