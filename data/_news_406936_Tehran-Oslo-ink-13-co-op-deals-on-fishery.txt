TEHRAN- Iran and Norway on Saturday signed 13 deals on fishery cooperation, Tasnim news agency reported. The deals inked by Iranian Agriculture Minister Mahmoud Hojjati and Norwegian Minister of Fisheries Per Sandberg include cooperation between the two countries companies on the supply of equipment and building infrastructures for the farming of 20,000 tons of fishes.
Signing of the mentioned deals came on the sidelines of Iran’s Fisheries, Aquaculture and Fishery Industry Exhibition (IRAN SEA FEX 2016) which was inaugurated in Tehran on Saturday and will run until Monday.
