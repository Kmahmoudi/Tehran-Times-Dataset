Sunjay Sudhir, joint secretary for international cooperation at India’s oil ministry, heading a delegation, met with Amir Hossein Zamaninia, Iranian deputy oil minister for international affairs, in Tehran on Tuesday.  After the meeting, Zamaninia said the Indian government is determined to cooperate on developing Iran’s Farzad-B offshore gas field.
