TEHRAN – Iranian and Chinese warships are to hold a joint military maneuver in the Strait of Hormuz and the Sea of Oman on Sunday, IRNA reported on Thursday.

 Commander of the First Naval Base Rear Admiral Amir Hossein Azad in a joint press conference with commander of the visiting Chinese Fleet-150 said that the maneuver will be held in the east of the Hormuz Strait and north of the Indian Ocean. SP/PA
