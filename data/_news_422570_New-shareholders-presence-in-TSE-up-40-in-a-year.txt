TEHRAN- Presence of new shareholders in Tehran Stock Exchange (TSE) rose 40 percent in the past Iranian calendar year 1396 (ended on March 20), compared to its preceding year, according to TSE CEO Hassan Qalibaf-Als. The official said 174,719 trading codes were issued in TSE in the past year, showing 40 percent rise year on year, IRNA reported.
