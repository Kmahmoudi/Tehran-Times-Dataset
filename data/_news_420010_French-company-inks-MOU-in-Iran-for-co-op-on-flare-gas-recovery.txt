TEHRAN- France’s energy company Entrepose Group signed a trilateral memorandum of understanding (MOU) with two Iranian parties for cooperation on recovery and utilization of flare gas from Iran’s South Pars gas field phases 14, 17 and 18 of development. According to a report by IDRO, the MOU was signed between Entrepose Group, Industrial Projects Management Company of Iran (IPMI) and Industrial Development and Renovation Organization (IDRO) in Tehran.
