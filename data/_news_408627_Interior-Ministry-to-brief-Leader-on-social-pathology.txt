TEHRAN – The Interior Ministry will brief the Supreme Leader on social pathology in April-May, the interior minister said on Saturday.

 Abdolreza Rahmani Fazli, speaking on the sidelines of a Social Council’s meeting, said in a recent meeting with the Leader on the same issue, various organizations presented their reports, ISNA reported. The minister also ordered related organizations to each introduce a six-month plan for their approach to social pathology.
