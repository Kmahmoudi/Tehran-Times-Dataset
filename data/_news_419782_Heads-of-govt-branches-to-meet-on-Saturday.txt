TEHRAN – Heads of the Iranian government branches will meet in the parliament on Saturday to discuss important domestic and regional issues, Islamic Republic of Iran Broadcasting news reported on Wednesday. President Hassan Rouhani, Parliament Speaker Ali Larijani and Judiciary Chief Sadeq Amoli Larijani are scheduled in particular to discuss during the meeting the possible ways to resolve monetary issues of investment institutions, which are heavily in debt to the investors. They will also exchange views on the latest regional and international developments. MH/
