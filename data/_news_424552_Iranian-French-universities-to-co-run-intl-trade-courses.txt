TEHRAN â€” Ferdowsi University of Mashhad and University Clermont Auvergne of France inked a memorandum of understanding (MOU) to co-run international trade courses in Iran, ISNA reported on Tuesday. According to the MOU, all courses will be taught in English and applicants will be able to join the courses after their English proficiency has been tested. The 10-month courses will be held in Ferdowsi University of Mashhad as of September, some 50 percent of the content will be taught by Iranian faculties, and the rest will be taught online by University Clermont Auvergne faculties. According to the Ferdowsi University of Mashhad public relations, graduates will receive certificates after completing the course which are internationally recognized in the field of international trade.
