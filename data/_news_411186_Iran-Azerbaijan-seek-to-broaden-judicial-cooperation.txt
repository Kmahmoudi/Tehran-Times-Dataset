TEHRAN – Iran’s justice minister met with Azeri President Ilham Aliyev in Baku on Friday, where they underlined the need for the expansion of mutual cooperation.

 During the meeting, Mostafa Pourmohammadi and Aliyev explored ways for bolstering and reinvigorating bilateral ties in the judicial field, Fars reported. During the meeting, President Aliyev pointed to successful development of relations between Baku and Tehran in all areas and said there are good grounds for cooperation between the two countries in legal and judicial fields as well.
