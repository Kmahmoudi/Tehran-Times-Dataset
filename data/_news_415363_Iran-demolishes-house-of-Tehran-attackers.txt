TEHRAN – A house was demolished in the western province of Kermanshah which Iranian security forces relate to twin terrorist attacks by Daesh in Tehran on June 7.

 The house had been used as a hideout for terrorists related to the twin attacks that targeted the Majlis and the Imam Khomeini shrine, Mehr quoted an unnamed security official as having said on Monday. The source added the house had been used to store ammunition and explosives. SP/PA
