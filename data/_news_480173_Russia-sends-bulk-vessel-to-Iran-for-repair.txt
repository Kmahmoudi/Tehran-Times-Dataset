TEHRAN – Iran Marine Industrial Company (known as SADRA) is repairing a Russian vessel that has hit large pieces of ice in the Volga River, an official with the company said on Wednesday. Head of the SADRA Company’s Caspian Complex Meysam Ra'yat Azad said this is the first time that a Russian vessel is being repaired by Iran Marine Industrial Company. Specialists of the SADRA company are currently trying to fix some of the damages caused to this bulk ship, the official said.
