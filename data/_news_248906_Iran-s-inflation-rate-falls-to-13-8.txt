TEHRAN - The Statistical Center of Iran has announced that the inflation rate for the 12-month period ended the last day of the fifth Iranian calendar month of Mordad (July 23-Aug 22) decreased to 13.8 percent compared to 14.1 percent for the same period ended the last day of the fourth Iranian calendar month of Tir (June 22-July 22).
