TEHRAN- Iran is seeking new target markets for the export of its liquefied petroleum gas (LPG), according to Iranian Gas Commercial Company (IGCC) Managing Director Mohammad-Ali Barati. Barati said the production and exports of LPG from some development phases of South Pars gas field (which Iran shares with Qatar in the Persian Gulf) has experienced a 100-percent growth in the past Iranian calendar year (ended on March 20), IRIB news reported.
