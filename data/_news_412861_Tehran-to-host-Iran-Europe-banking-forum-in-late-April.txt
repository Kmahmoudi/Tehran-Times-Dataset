TEHRAN – The fourth edition of Iran-Europe banking and business forum is due to be held at IRIB international conference center in Tehran on April 29 and 30, Mehr news agency reported. According to Ali Divandari, the head of Iran’s Monetary and Banking Research Institute (MBRI), representatives of more than 35 European banks, companies and financial firms will be attending this event which is mainly focused on “development of banking and financial processes” and “investment opportunities in Iran”.  EF/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
