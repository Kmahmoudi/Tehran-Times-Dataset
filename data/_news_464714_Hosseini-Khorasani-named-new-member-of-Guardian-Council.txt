TEHRAN — In the wake of Sadegh Amoli Larijani’s resignation from his post as a Guardian Council member, the Leader of the Islamic Revolution Ayatollah Seyed Ali Khamenei appointed Seyyed Ahmad Hosseini Khorasani as a replacement. In the decree issued late on Saturday, the Leader thanked Larijani for his services and wished success for Hosseini Khorasani. Larijani had been a member of the council for 11 years. The council consists of 12 members. Six of them – religious scholars - are appointed by Leader and six others – law experts, are named by Judiciary chief.
