TEHRAN – Iran and Qatar discussed ways of facilitating maritime trade and cooperating in ports and transport areas, IRIB news reported. The two sides explored the matters in a bilateral meeting in Doha on Monday. The meeting was attended by the Managing Director of Iran’s Ports and Maritime Organization (PMO) Mohammad Rastad and Qatari Minister of Transport and Communications Jassim Saif Ahmed Al Sulaiti as well as directors of the Arab country’s renowned companies active in ports and maritime.
