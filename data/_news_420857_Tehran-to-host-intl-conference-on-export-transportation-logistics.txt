TEHRAN- Iran’s 2nd International Conference for Export Transportation and Logistics will be held at the Tehran Permanent International Fairground on February 25, the portal pf Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) reported on Tuesday. TPO Head Mojtaba Khosrotaj, who is the chairman of policy making council of the event, said directors from transportation sectors of different countries will participate the conference.
