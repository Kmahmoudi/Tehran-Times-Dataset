TEHRAN- Production of copper cathode and anode in Iran rose 62 percent and 65 percent, respectively, in the first four months of the present Iranian calendar year (March 21- July 22, 2018), in comparison with the same time span in the preceding year, IRIB reported. Giant copper cathode production units in Iran could produce 96,920 tons of the product in the said time this year while their production of anode stood at 58,685 tons, the report said.
