TEHRAN- Some 400 kilometers of railways are under construction throughout the country for connecting mines as well as industrial and cargo loading/unloading hubs to the national railway network, according to an official in the Islamic Republic of Iran Railways (known as RAI). Maziyar Yazdani, the deputy head of RAI for infrastructure and technical affairs, also mentioned linking Iran’s railway network to the other countries’ railway networks as the other main plans of RAI, Mehr news agency reported.
