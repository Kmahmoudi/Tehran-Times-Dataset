TEHRAN- Exports of goods via railway increased 55% in Iran during the first 11 months of the current Iranian calendar year (March 20, 2016-Februray 18, 2017), compared to the same period of time in the previous year, IRNA quoted a railway official, Hossein Ashouri. As he said, in the said time, 10 million tons of goods including cement, iron ore, coal, and tiles were exported majorly to Turkmenistan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Pakistan, and Turkey.
