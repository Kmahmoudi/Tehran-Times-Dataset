TEHRAN- Iran-Bulgaria business forum is scheduled to be held at the place of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) on Monday, the portal of ICCIMA reported. The forum will be held simultaneous with the visit to be paid by the Minister of Economy of the Republic of Bulgaria Emil Karanikolov to Iran, the report said. As reported earlier, in a meeting between Bulgarian President Roumen Radev and Iranian Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif in late February, the president announced that his country is interested in deepening partnership with Iran in areas of trade, energy and science.
