TEHRAN- Deputy head of Iranian National Tax Administration (INTA) announced that during the first five months of the present Iranian calendar year (March 21- August 22, 2018) 4.8 trillion rials (about $114.2 million) has been earned as value added tax (VAT) and been paid for implementation of development projects, IRNA reported on Tuesday. The paid amount has remarkably increased in comparison with that of the same period in the preceding year, Mohammad Masihi told IRNA.
