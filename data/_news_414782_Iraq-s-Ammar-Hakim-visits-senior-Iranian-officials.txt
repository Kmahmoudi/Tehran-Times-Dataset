TEHRAN – Ammar Hakim, chief of the Islamic Supreme Council of Iraq, traveled to Tehran on Monday and held talks with senior Iranian officials.

 In a meeting with Iranian Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif, Hakim discussed the latest developments in Iraq, IRNA reported. Zarif and Hakim conferred on the most recent political and field developments in Iraq, particularly the fight against Daesh. Later the day, Hakim met with Majlis Speaker Ali Larijani as well to discuss topics of mutual concern. SP/PA
