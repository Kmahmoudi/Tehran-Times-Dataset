TEHRAN- The 6th International Exhibition of Elevators, Industrial and Related Equipment of Iran (Iran LiftEx 2017) is hosting 280 Iranian and foreign companies.
 The event, which started operation at the Tehran Permanent Fairground on Tuesday, will finish its work on Thursday, IRIB news reported. Some 190 Iranian companies and 90 foreign ones from Germany, Italy, Taiwan, Turkey, Japan, China, Switzerland, Croatia, and South Korea are showcasing their latest products at this exhibition.
