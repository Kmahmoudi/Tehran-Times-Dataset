TEHRAN -- Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) plans to hold the first Iran high-tech business expo on June 26.
Sponsored by the Iran-Italy Joint Chamber of Commerce, the event will be held at the venue of ICCIMA in Tehran.  It aims to provide an opportunity for knowledge enterprises and investors to support Iranian products, the portal of ICCIMA reported. Companies active in different fields of textile, chemical products, packaging, automobile, high-tech, and petrochemical industries will attend the event. Meanwhile, an exhibition will be held on the sidelines of the business expo and ICCIMA will offer different services including free of charge consultation to participants.
