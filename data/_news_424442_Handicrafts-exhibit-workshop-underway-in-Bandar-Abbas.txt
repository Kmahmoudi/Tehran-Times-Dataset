TEHRAN – A sales exhibition of Iranian handicrafts opened its doors to the public on Monday in the port city of Bandar Abbas, southern Iran. It also hosts several crafts workshops yet offers a 20 percent discount on various handcrafted products, CHTN reported.  The one-week event is held in commemoration of the national handicrafts week and under the auspices of the Cultural Heritage, Handicrafts and Tourism Organization.
