BEIRUT (AFP/The Daily Star) -- Lebanese President Michel Sleiman on Monday urged Saudi entrepreneurs to invest in his country during his first visit to oil-rich Saudi Arabia since his election in May. Addressing business leaders at the Jeddah Chamber of Commerce and Industry, Sleiman urged them to “boost their investments” in Lebanon, which offers “guarantees and facilities” for investors, the official Saudi Press Agency (SPA) reported.
