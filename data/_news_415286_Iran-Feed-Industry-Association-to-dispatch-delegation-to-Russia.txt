TEHRAN – Iran Feed Industry Association (IFIA) plans to dispatch a trade delegation to Russia from August 1 to 9 aiming for familiarity with the country’s feed industry and technology.
 As Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) portal reported, the delegation comprising representatives of companies and businessmen active in this industry will be visiting Saint Petersburg and Moscow to meet with their Russian counterparts and explore new ideas and technology in this area.
