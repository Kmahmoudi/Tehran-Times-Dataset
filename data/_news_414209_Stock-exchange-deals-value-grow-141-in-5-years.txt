TEHRAN- The value of deals in Iranian stock exchange market rose 141 percent during the past five Iranian calendar years 1391-1395 (March 20, 2012- March 20, 2017), IRIB news reported on Monday. As reported, the value of deals grew 141 percent from 264 trillion rials (about $7.07 billion) in the beginning of 1391 to 368 trillion rials (about $17.1 billion) in 1395 yearend. HJ/MA
