TEHRAN – Pouya Mahmoudian has been appointed as the new deputy director of the Cultural Heritage, Handicrafts and Tourism Organization for handicrafts. She has served as the organization’s director for exports over the past couple of years. CHHTO Director Ali Asghar Mounesan installed Mahmoudian in the position on Monday, replacing Bahman Namvar-Motlaq who resigned earlier in February, Mehr reported.
