TEHRAN – During the last session of the Expediency Council Akbar Hashemi Rafsanjani stressed that the government and Majlis should coordinate their actions with the Leader, the council’s secretary said on Friday.

 Speaking before the Tehran Friday sermons, Mohsen Rezaee also said after the end of his presidency in 1997, Rafsanjani cooperated with Supreme Leader in the capacity of a consultant for 20 years until death took him away, IRIB reported.
