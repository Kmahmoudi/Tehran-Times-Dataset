TEHRAN – The speaker of Pakistan’s National Assembly said on Sunday that Tehran and Islamabad are able to tackle common concerns through joint efforts.

 Sardar Ayaz Sadiq also said that Iran is a big country in Asia and extremely important neighbor of Pakistan, Tabnak reported. “I think the active role they (Iranians) are playing, being a democracy, is something that needs to be reflected in other countries also,” he stated SP/PA
