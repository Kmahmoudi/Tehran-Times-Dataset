TEHRAN- Some 220 Iranian and foreign companies will take part in the 25th International Exhibition of Chandeliers & Decorative Lights from December 24 to 26 in Tehran, IRIB news reported.
 According to the report, 201 Iranian companies and 19 foreign companies from Turkey, China and Taiwan, will showcase their latest products at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds, the report said. HJ/MA
