TEHRAN - The Islamic Revolution Guards Corps unveiled on Wednesday anti-armor missile Azarakhsh and inaugurated an aviation training center.
 Azarakhsh weights 70kg and can destroy targets 10km away at a maximum speed of 550m/s. The anti-armor missile has been developed to be used in low-altitude air operations and against ground targets.  According to Fars news agency, a well-equipped aviation center, a helicopter research center and a center for specialized helicopter overhaul center were also inaugurated near Tehran.
