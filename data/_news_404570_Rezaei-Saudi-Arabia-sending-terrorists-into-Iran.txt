TEHRAN – The secretary of Iran’s Expediency Council on Saturday rebuked Saudi Arabia for its destructive moves in the region, saying Riyadh is supporting and sending terrorists into Iran.

 “The Saudi consulate in Northern Iraq helps the Komala Party (a terrorist group active in the Kurdistan region) to interfere in Iran and Iraq’s affairs and sends terrorists into Iran,” Mohsen Rezayee said, ILNA reported. “The Saudis are the most evil government on Earth and are stirring turmoil in the region because of their insanity,” stated Rezayee who was the commander of the IRGC from 1980–1997.
