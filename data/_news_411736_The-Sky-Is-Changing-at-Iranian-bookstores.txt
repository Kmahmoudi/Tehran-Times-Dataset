TEHRAN – London-based Swiss writer Zoe Jenny’s 2010 novel “The Sky Is Changing” has recently been published in Persian by Saless Publications in Tehran.

 Translated by Sahar Tavakkoli, the book is about Claire, her husband and a circle of middle class friends. They believe that they are in full control of their perfect lives and destinies but their dreams can be easily destroyed. ABU/YAW
