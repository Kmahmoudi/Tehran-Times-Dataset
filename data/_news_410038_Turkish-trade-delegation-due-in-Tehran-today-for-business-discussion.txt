TEHRAN – A 20-member Turkish trade delegation dispatched by Antalya Chamber of Commerce and Industry (ACCI) will discuss business opportunities with the Iranian side in Tehran today, the portal of Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) announced. Headed by ACCI’s Chairman Davut Çetin, the delegation is due to hold talks with Iranian businessmen and entrepreneurs at TCCIMA building. Businessmen and entrepreneurs from Antalya, active in the areas like construction, interior design, industrial machinery, equipment, furniture, tourism, agriculture, real estate, sport complexes, transportation, petrochemicals, food and textile will accompany Çetin. The two sides are to get familiarized with each other's activities and discuss future cooperation and expansion of trade relations in the event.
