TEHRAN- Opportunities for finance and investment in Iran’s mining sector will be introduced in the International Mining and Resources Conference (IMARC) running in Australia’s Melbourne from October 30 to November 2.  Head of Iranian Mines and Mining Industries Development and Renovation Organization (IMIDRO) Mehdi Karbasian will give a lecture on these opportunities in the conference on Wednesday, IMIDRO website announced. Karbasian met directors of some renowned global mining companies and discussed the possible cooperation opportunities with them in the first day of the event. IMARC is the fourth cornerstone in global mining conferences covering the Asia Pacific region. The conference, with workshops on 30 October and the conference and exhibition from 31 October to November 2, provides four days of learning, deal-making opportunities and networking. The 2017 program will cover all aspects of mining from exploration to investment, production to optimization through technology, future energy, policy and governance. The exhibition will feature over 100 exhibitors showcasing the latest innovations, technology and services for the mining industry.
