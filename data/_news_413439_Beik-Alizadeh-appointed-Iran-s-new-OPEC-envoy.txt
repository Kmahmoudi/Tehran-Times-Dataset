TEHRAN- Behrouz Beik Alizadeh was appointed as Iran’s new representative at the Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries (OPEC), Shana reported on Sunday. Iran’s Oil Minister Bijan Namdar Zanganeh appointed Beik Alizadeh to the post, replacing Mehdi Asali, who retired last June when Beik Alizadeh was appointed as the acting representative of Iran in OPEC.
