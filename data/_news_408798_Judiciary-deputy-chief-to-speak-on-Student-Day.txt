TEHRAN – The deputy Judiciary chief is going to address students at the University of Tehran on the occasion of Student Day which falls on December 6.

 Qolamhossein Mohseni Ejei will deliver his speech at Ferdowsi Auditorium of the School of Literature and Humanities starting 14:00, Tasnim reported on Sunday. News outlets on Saturday reported that President Hassan Rouhani was going to address students at the university. SP/PA
