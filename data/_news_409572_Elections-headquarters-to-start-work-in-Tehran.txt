TEHRAN – Presidential election headquarters is to start its activities in Tehran next week, Tehran Province’s Deputy Governor for Social and Political Affairs Shahabeddin Chavoshi told IRNA on Wednesday.

 Chavoshi said the headquarters will focus on preparing necessary grounds for massive turnout in the next presidential elections in the country. The headquarters is using well-qualified experts who are eligible for this capacity to encourage a higher voter turnout, the official said.
