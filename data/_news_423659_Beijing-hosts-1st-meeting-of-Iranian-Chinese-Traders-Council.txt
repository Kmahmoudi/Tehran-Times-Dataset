TEHRAN- The first meeting of Iranian and Chinese Traders Council was held in Beijing with participation of the representatives of 170 Iranian and Chinese companies, the website of Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) reported on Tuesday. Addressing the meeting, Ali-Asghar Khaji, Iran’s ambassador to China, expounded upon his country’s potentials and capabilities in agriculture, mining, industry, high tech, and foreign trade.
