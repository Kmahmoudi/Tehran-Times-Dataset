TEHRAN - The 6th edition of Iran’s International Exhibition of Office Furniture (HOFEX 2017 office section) is due to be held 25-28 December at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds, the Public Relations Department of Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO) announced. Aiming to lay the groundwork for joint investments, the four-day exhibition will showcase the latest products and services of 90 domestic companies and dozens of foreign exhibitors.
All kinds of office furniture including amphitheater furniture, desks, library furniture, accessories, office decoration and etc. will be put to show in the exhibition.
China, Taiwan and Italy are among the countries that will have the most participants in this year’s event. EF/MA
