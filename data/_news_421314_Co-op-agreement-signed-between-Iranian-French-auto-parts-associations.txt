TEHRAN- The auto parts associations of Iran and France signed a cooperation agreement on the sidelines of the 5th edition of Iran’s Automotive Industry International Conference (IAIIC).
 The agreement was signed by the chairmen of the associations in the presence of Iranian Industry, Mining and Trade Minister Mohammad Shariatmadari, IRIB news reported on Thursday.  As reported, representatives from the world’s renowned carmakers including Peugeot, Renault, Nissan, Mercedes Benz, Volkswagen, Hyundai, Volvo and Scania participated in IAIIC which was held in Milad Tower of Tehran on February 13 and 14.
