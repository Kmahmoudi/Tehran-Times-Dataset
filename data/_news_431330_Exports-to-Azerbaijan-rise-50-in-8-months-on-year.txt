TEHRAN- The value of Iran’s exports to Azerbaijan rose 50 percent during the first eight months of the current Iranian calendar year (March 21-November 21, 2018) compared to the same period of time in the previous year, IRNA reported on Monday citing the data released by Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO). The TPO data put Iran’s exports to the neighboring country at $301 million during the mentioned time span while its imports from that country was $12 million.
