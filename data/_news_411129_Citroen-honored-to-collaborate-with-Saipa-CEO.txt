TEHRAN- PSA Group's Citroen voiced its readiness to start cooperation with Iran’s Saipa Group about a year ago and the cooperation is our honor, IRIB news quoted CEO of the French company Linda Jackson. As she said the two companies will collaborate on almost all fields including designing, engineering, marketing, manufacturing, and exports. Jackson informed that via its cooperation with Saipa, Citroen seeks to gain 30 percent of the regional export market. HJ/MA
