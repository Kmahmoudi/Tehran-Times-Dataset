TEHRAN- Implementation of 45 airport projects has been started in the country in the current Iranian calendar year and they will be tried to be complete by 80 percent by the yearend (March 20, 2018), according to an Iranian aviation official. As Gholam-Hossein Baqerian, a board member of Airports and Air Navigation Company of Iran, told IRNA on Monday, constriction of passenger terminals, development of runways, and construction of administrative buildings are among the most important airport projects underway.
