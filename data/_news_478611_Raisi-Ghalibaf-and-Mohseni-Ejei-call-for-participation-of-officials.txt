TEHRAN - President Ebrahim Raisi, Parliament Speaker Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf and Judiciary Chief Gholamhossein Mohseni Ejei, who met on Saturday afternoon, said it is necessary that officials with different positions from the executive, legislative and judicial powers to attend public meetings, especially those arranged by the youth. The three top officials also called for the need to accelerate presentation of the seventh development plan by the Planning and Budget Organization (PBO) to the government and parliament.
