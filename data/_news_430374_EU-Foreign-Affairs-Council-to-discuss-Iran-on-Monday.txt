TEHRAN – The EU Foreign Affairs Council (FAC) will meet in Brussels on December 10 to exchange views on the relations between the EU and Iran among other issues including Ukraine, EU-African Union cooperation, Western Balkans, and Venezuela. According to Finland’s Foreign Ministry, the ministers “will look at Iran’s nuclear deal (Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action, JCPOA) in light of the latest developments.” Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister Abbas Araqchi said on Tuesday that European financial mechanism to save the JCPOA now that the U.S. has exited the agreement will be announced in the near future.
