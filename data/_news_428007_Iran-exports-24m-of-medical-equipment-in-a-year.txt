TEHRAN- Iran exported $24 million of medical equipment to European, Asian and African countries in the past Iranian calendar year (ended on March 20, 2018), according to the vice chairman of the association of Iranian medical equipment exporters. Mohammadreza Compani said that Iran is exporting medical equipment to 50 countries, adding that the country has achieved many progresses and entered global markets of these products over the past ten years”, ISNA reported.
