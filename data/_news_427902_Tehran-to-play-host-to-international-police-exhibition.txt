TEHRAN — The 17th International Police, Safety, and Security Equipment Exhibition (IPAS 2018), will open in Tehran on October 1, Mehr news agency reported on Tuesday. With the theme of “security and safety support domestic production”, the event will run until October 4 at Imam Khomeini Mosalla. Increasing the quality and upgrading police equipment in order to reduce accidents and minimize the loss they incur and supporting inventors active in field of designing and devising police equipment are of the objectives of the exhibition, IPAS secretary, Alireza Aliabadi said.  Companies from 20 countries worldwide will showcase their products in 350 pavilions on a land area of 35,000 square meters, Aliabadi explained.  MQ/MG All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
