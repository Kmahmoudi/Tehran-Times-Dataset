TEHRAN- A recent report released by the Central Statistics Office of Ireland (CSO) shows that the value of trade between Iran and Ireland has doubled in 2017, IRNA reported. Ireland’s exports to Iran rose to €143.218 million in 2017 from €71.845 million in 2016 and Iran’s exports to Ireland increased to €3.51 million from €1.629 million, according to the CSO report.
