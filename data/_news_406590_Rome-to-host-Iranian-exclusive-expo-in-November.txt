TEHRAN - Rome is going to host an Iranian exclusive exhibition from November 22 to 26, Public Relations Department of Iran’s Trade Promotion Organization (TPO) reported. According to an invitation form released by TPO’s Public Relations Department, all companies active in various areas including petrochemicals, construction, utilities, engineering and technical services, urban development, construction materials, stone and glass, handicrafts, packing and processing machinery, marine industries, medical equipment, etc. are welcomed to participate in the event.  
This exhibition aims to pave the way for Iranian companies to enter Italy’s market and also to acquaint Iranian traders to new technologies in the European country. 
According to the latest report released by Iran’s Customs Administration, Italy has been the major customer of Iranian non-oil products in the first five months of the current Iranian calendar year (March 21-August 21). It imported $261.5 million of non-oil goods from Iran in the said time, showing a 15.76 percent increase from its previous $226 million in the same time last year.  EF/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
