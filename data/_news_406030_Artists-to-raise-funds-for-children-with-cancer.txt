TEHRAN – An exhibition of visual arts by contemporary artists opened at Tehran’s Milad Tower on Friday to raise funds for children with cancer. Entitled “Color of Love II”, the exhibit has been organized by Mahak Charity Society, a Tehran-based major medical center for safeguarding children with cancer. “This exhibit has been set up to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the establishment of the center,” director of Mahak Arasb Ahmadian said at the opening ceremony. He added that the works donated to the center over the past 25 years have also been put on display in this exhibit. Ahmadian also expressed thanks to the artists who have always been available to help children with cancer. The exhibit will be running until Friday September 9.
