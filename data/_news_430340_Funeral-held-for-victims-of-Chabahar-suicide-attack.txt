TEHRAN – A funeral procession for two victims of the Chabahar suicide terrorist attack was held on Friday. According to IRNA, Dariush Ranjbar and Naser Dor-Zadeh were martyred on Thursday morning after a suicide car bomb went off at Chabahar’s police headquarters. The slain soldiers were transported to their hometowns after the ceremony. Culture Minister Abbas Salehi and military commanders participated at the funeral. At least 43 individuals were also injured during the attack, 10 of whom were police forces and others were ordinary people. Women and children were also among the injured.
