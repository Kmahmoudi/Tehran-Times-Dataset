TEHRAN – The chairman of the Councils and Interior Affairs Committee of the Majlis has said the comprehensive bill on elections will be delivered to the legislative body in one month.

 There are four laws for four types of elections, i.e. council, parliamentary, Assembly of Experts, and presidential elections, Mohammad Javad Kulivand told ILNA on Friday. Each law has its special place in the bill and each includes a part for financial transparency of electoral campaigns, he said. SP/PA
