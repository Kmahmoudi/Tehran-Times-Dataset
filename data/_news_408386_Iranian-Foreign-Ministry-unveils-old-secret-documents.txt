TEHRAN – The Iranian Foreign Ministry on Tuesday unveiled some old secret documents.

 The documents, belonging to the Qajar Dynasty and First Pahlavi periods, were unveiled by Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif, IRINN reported. The most notable document that Zarif personally unveiled during the ceremony was titled “Iranian Ambassador to Washington’s Secret Account on the Governmental Arrangements of America”. Zarif said publishing foreign relations documents will help nations better retrieve their historical memory.
