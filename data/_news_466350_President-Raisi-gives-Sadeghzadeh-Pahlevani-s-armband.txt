TEHRAN – Iran President Ebrahim Raisi gave Jaber Sadeghzadeh special Bazouband (Armband). Sadeghzadeh won Iran’s Pahlevani Wrestling Championship for the seventh time on Sunday. Pahlevan of Iran is an annual Pahlevani Wrestling competition held in Iran, in which athletes from across the country participate to achieve the title.
