TEHRAN – Zahedan prosecutor Ali Movahedi-Rad announced on Saturday that four suspects have been arrested over the Chabahar terrorist attack. Movahedi-Rad said the suspects have been arrested in different towns of Sistan-Baluchestan Province and the case is due to be reviewed in the Special Assembly of Security Crimes in Zahedan, the provincial capital, ISNA reported. “Investigating the suspects’ roles in the terrorist attack as well as identifying other suspects will continue,” he said. On Thursday, a vehicle laden with explosives went off close to a police headquarters in Chabahar, Sistan-Baluchestan Province, killing two soldiers and injuring at least 43 individuals.
