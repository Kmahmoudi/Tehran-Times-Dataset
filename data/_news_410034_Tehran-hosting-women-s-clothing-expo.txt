TEHRAN — The fifth national exhibition on Iranian and Islamic style of clothing and fashion for women is being held at Tehran’s Goftogu Park. Organized by Tehran Municipality, the exposition opened on January 11 and will last until January 18, Mehr news agency reported.  Along with 80 well-known women clothing brands attending the event breadwinner women are also showcasing their products, Elahe Ataei, director general for Tehran Municipality women affairs said.  Ataei further highlighted that not only all the items of clothing are available at a reasonable price but also they are on sale and visitors can get 30 to 50 percent off.  “The women wear being exhibited at the exposition cater for all tastes and can meet all women’s needs,” she added.
