TEHRAN - Leader of the Islamic Revolution Ayatollah Seyed Ali Khamenei prayed on Friday for the late Mrs. Azam Rouhani, the wife of martyr Ayatollah Morteza Motahhari. He also recited Surah Fatihah for her.
