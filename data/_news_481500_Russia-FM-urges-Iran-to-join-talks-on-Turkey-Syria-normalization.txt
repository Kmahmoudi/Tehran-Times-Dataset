TEHRAN- The Russian Foreign Minister said on Tuesday that there is a general understanding that Tehran would join the efforts of Moscow to normalize relations between Turkey and Syria. Sergey Lavrov stated that there is a basic agreement to include Iran in the trilateral consultations involving Russia, Turkey, and Syria.
