TEHRAN – Moscow hosted a joint Iran-Russia business forum on investment opportunities in Iran on Tuesday in which the two sides discussed Iran’s economic potentials, IRNA reported. The event was attended by Iran’s Economic Attaché to Moscow Davood Mirzakhani and over 70 businessmen and entrepreneurs active in various economic areas.
