TEHRAN – Britain has expressed delight in the improvement of diplomatic ties with the Islamic republic of Iran. On Monday, new British Ambassador to Tehran Nicholas Hopton wrote on twitter: “Delighted to be the new UK Ambassador to #Iran.  I look forward to the next phase of cooperation between our countries.” Foreign Secretary Boris Johnson also wrote: “Pleased to announce upgrade of relations with #Iran to Ambassador level.” SP/PA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
