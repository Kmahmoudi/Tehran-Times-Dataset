TEHRAN - The 4th International Exhibition of Building Industry, Façade, Structure, and Innovative Technologies (IRAN BuildEX) will be held at the Tehran International Permanent Fairground from February 24 to 27. Some 250 Iranian and 70 foreign companies from 15 countries are to showcase their latest products and achievements in the construction industry, ILNA reported on Monday. The exhibition seeks to provide a platform for Iranian and international companies to learn and share about the latest developments of building industry in Iran and the global markets. HJ/MG
