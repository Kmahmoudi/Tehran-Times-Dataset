TEHRAN — The southwestern city of Sisakht, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad province, was hit by another earthquake measuring 4.8 on Richer scale within days after the magnitude 5.2 tremor on May 2.  The quake struck at about 3 a.m. local time on Monday and left some 86 injured, IRIB reported.  Last week’s quake also injured some 76.  MQ/ All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
