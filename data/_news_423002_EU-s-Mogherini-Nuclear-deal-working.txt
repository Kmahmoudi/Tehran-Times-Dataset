TEHRAN – The European Union foreign policy chief, Federica Mogherini, said on Wednesday that the nuclear deal, officially called the JCPOA, is working. “The deal we have in place now, the only existing deal for the moment is working,” Mogherini told reporters, according to AP. She added, “The deal is working. President Macron stressed that yesterday in the White House very clearly that we believe that full implementation of the JCPOA is essential for European security and for the security of the region and the Europeans will stick to that.” SP/PA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
