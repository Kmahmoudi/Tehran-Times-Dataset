TEHRAN – The first national congress on successful Iranian women is to be held in Tehran on January 24, 2017.
 The congress focuses on women’s presence in decision-makings in different levels of the social and administrative organizations, Mehr news agency reported.
Successful women in national and international fields and female managers who are active in executive organizations will participate in the congress.
Specialized workshops and meetings on different management fields will be held on the sidelines of the event and participants will be granted certificates. NM/MG
