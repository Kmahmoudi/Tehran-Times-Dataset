TEHRAN- Singaporean economic delegation will arrive in Tehran on Saturday to take part in Iran-Singapore business forum on Sunday, the portal of Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) reported on Monday. The Singaporean delegation which seeks expansion of trade ties and economic cooperation with Iranian private sector is active in the fields of agricultural products, chemical fertilizers, foodstuff, cigarettes, investing on and implementing joint projects, and etc.  HJ/MA
