TEHRAN- Iran exported 1.44 million tons of goods during the first two months of the present Iranian calendar year (March 22- May 21, 2017) via its land and maritime borders, showing 8.6 percent increase compared to the same period in the previous year. As IRNA reported, 357,923 tons of the transited goods in the said time were oil products and 1,081,877 tons were non-oil products.
