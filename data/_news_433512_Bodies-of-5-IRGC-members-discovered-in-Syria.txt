TEHRAN – Public Relations of the Islamic Revolution Guard Corps (IRGC) has announced that it had discovered the bodies of five missing members of the IRGC in Syria, IRNA reported on Wednesday. “With round-the-clock efforts of the research team of the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps’ Quds Force, the bodies of five Iranian soldiers, who had been martyred during an advisory mission in Syria, were discovered and they were identified after DNA tests”, the statement said. It also named the martyrs as: Saeed Ansari and Meysam Nazari from Tehran Province, Hamid Mohammad Rezaee from Qazvin Province, Mohammad Qanbarian from Semnan Province and Seyyed Javad Asadi from Mazandaran Province.
