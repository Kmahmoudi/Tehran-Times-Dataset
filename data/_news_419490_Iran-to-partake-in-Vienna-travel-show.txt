TEHRAN – Iran will demonstrate its vast potentials at the Ferien-Messe Wien -- Austria’s largest holiday and travel show. The Touring & Automobile Club of the Islamic Republic of Iran along with 12 travel companies will hold a wide variety of exhibits, presentations and workshops at the country’s pavilion, Mehr reported on Sunday. More than 800 exhibitors from over 70 countries are set to attend the event, which will be held in Vienna from January 11 to 14.
