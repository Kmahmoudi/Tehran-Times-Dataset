TEHRAN – The Iranian Navy commander announced on Tuesday that 25 naval exercises have been planned to be held by the end of the current Iranian year, which ends on March 20, 2018.

 Rear Admiral Habibollah Sayyari underlined that the naval exercises will be held in the Persian Gulf and Caspian Sea, leading ultimately to the massive Velayat-96 naval drills, Fars reported. SP/PA
