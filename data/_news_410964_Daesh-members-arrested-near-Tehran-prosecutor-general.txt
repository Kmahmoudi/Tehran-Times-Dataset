TEHRAN – Iran's Prosecutor General Mohammad Jafar Montazeri announced on Friday that security forces have arrested some members of Daesh terrorist group near Tehran, Tasnim news agency reported. Montazeri said the detained members of Daesh wanted to carry out terrorist attacks on the anniversary of the victory of the Islamic Revolution. Attending the rallies marking the revolution anniversary in Kashan, he also said, “If Iran had not supported the resistance movement, Daesh would have reached the country’s borders by now.”  MH/PA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
