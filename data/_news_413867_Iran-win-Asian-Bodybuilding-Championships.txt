Iran won the title of the 51st Asian Bodybuilding Championships in Mongolia on Sunday. In the competition held in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia, the Iranian team claimed 12 gold medals, four silvers and two bronze medals. In the men’s category, Iran finished in first place with 265 points, followed by Mongolia (175 points) and China (166 points). In the women´s category, China claimed the title, bagging 116 points. Silver medal went to Japan with 80 points and South Korea won the bronze medal with 70 points.
