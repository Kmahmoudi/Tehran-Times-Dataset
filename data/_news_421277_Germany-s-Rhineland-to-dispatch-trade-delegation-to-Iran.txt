TEHRAN- A German trade delegation to be dispatched by the Federal State of Rhineland-Palatinate is scheduled to visit Iran on February 25 to discuss mutual trade cooperation, Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) announced. To be headed by a director general with the Ministry of Economy, Transport and Agriculture of Rhineland-Palatinate, the delegation will be comprised of the representatives of several renowned companies active in various areas, including new irrigation systems, water and wastewater management, renewable energies and urban waste management.
