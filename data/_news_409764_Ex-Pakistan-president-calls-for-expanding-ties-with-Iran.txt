TEHRAN - Asif Ali Zardari, the former president of Pakistan and leader of Pakistan’s People Party, said on Wednesday that his country seeks expansion of relations with Iran.
 During a meeting with Iran’s Consul General Mehdi Sobhani in Islamabad, Zardai said that many good steps were taken to deepen ties during his time in office. Sobhani said that Iran attaches great importance to ties with Pakistan, especially in economic area. NA
