TEHRAN – Iran’s Foreign Ministry will submit its fourth quarterly report on the nuclear deal with world powers to the parliament in mid-January, the fourth one over the past year. “The Foreign Ministry will submit the 4th quarterly report on the nuclear deal to the Majlis (parliament) on January 16,” Hossein Naqavi Hosseini, spokesman for the parliamentary national security and foreign policy committee, said on Wednesday.   The committee will assess the report on the basis of how good the deal has been implemented so far.  AK/PA   All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
