TEHRAN – The first phase of the gas supply project to Chabahar power plant in southeastern Iran is due to be completed by May 22 in which 1 million cubic meters (mcm) of gas will be supplied to the plant on a daily basis. The total project is defined in two phases and in the second phase 2 mcm of gas will be supplied to the plant in a day, Shana reported quoting National Iranian Gas Company (NIGC)’s gas distribution director Saeid Momeni.
