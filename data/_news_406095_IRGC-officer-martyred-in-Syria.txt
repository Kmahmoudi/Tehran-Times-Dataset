TEHRAN – The IRGC has lost a military adviser in Syria, Mizan reported on Sunday. 
 Dariush Dorosti was martyred by the Jaysh al-Ezzah militant group in the northern part of the Syrian province of Hama.
The body of the officer was still held by the terrorist group when the report came late Sunday. SP/PA
