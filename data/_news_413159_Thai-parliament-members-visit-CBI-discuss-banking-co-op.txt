TEHRAN – Representatives of Thailand’s parliament expressed willingness for banking cooperation with Iran, Central Bank of Iran (CBI) official website announced. According to CBI, A Thai delegation comprised of members of national assembly of Thailand visited the bank on April 3 to discussed banking cooperation with Iranian officials.
Peyman Qorbani, CBI’s vice governor for economic affairs, met with the Thai delegation and presented Iran’s latest banking and economic achievements.  EF/MA All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
