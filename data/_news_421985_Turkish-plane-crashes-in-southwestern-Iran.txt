TEHRAN-- A Turkish aircraft with 11 passengers aboard heading from Sharjah in the United Arab Emirates to Istanbul, Turkey, crashed around the city of Shahr-e Kord, southwestern Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari province . According to Civil Aviation Organization spokesman Reza Jafarzadeh, the aircraft, which was a private jet, disappeared from the radars. Iranian officials said all passengers aboard were young girls.
