TEHRAN -- The Museum of the Moving Image, a media museum located in Astoria, New York, (in New York City Borough of Queens) is screening “Starless Dreams” by Iranian filmmaker Mehrdad Oskui. The screening program began on January 20 and will be running until January 29. The documentary, which has been awarded at many international events, shows the lives of seven young teenage girls sharing temporary quarters at a rehabilitation and detention center on the outskirts of Tehran.
