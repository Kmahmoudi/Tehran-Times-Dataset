TEHRAN – The government spokesman has said that President Hassan Rouhani and First Vice President Es’haq Jahangiri have already reported their properties to the Judiciary, responding to their presidential candidacy rival Mohammad Baqer Qalibaf who asked that they do so in a live TV show.

 He also said according to the 6th Development Plan, tax exemption have been revoked and therefore the Imam Reza custodianship (under Ebrahim Raisi, another candidate) would have to pay taxes. SP/PA
