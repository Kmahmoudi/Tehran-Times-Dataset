TEHRAN – Iran’s copper concentrate production in the first eight months of the current Iranian calendar year (March 20-November 20) reached 715,078 tons, registering a 14 percent rise compared to the same period last year, IRIB news reported on Wednesday. According to the report, the country has produced 626,130 tons of copper concentrate in the first eight months of the previous year.
