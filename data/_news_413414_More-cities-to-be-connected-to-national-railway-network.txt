TEHRAN – Capital cities of five more provinces will be connected to the country’s national railway network in the current Iranian calendar year (March 2017- March 2018), IRNA reported quoting a railway official. According to Saeid Mohammadzadeh, the managing director of Islamic Republic of Iran Railways (IRIR), Hamedan, Tabriz, Kermanshah, Orumieh and Rasht are the cities to be connected to the national railway network.
