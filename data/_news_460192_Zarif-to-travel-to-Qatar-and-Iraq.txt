TEHRAN — Iranian Foreign Minister Mohammad Javad Zarif will travel to Qatar and Iraq respectively on Sunday. 
 Foreign Ministry spokesman Saeed Khatibzadeh said the trips to Qatar and Iraq will take place within the framework of developing relations between the countries and sharing views on regional and trans-regional issues.
