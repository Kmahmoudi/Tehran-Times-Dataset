TEHRAN – Interior Minister Abdolreza Rahmani Fazli said the said on Tuesday that Iranian police forces have managed to disband some 11,000 drug rings over the past four years, Tasnim reported.

 Addressing a ceremony held on the occasion of the International Day Against Drug Abuse and Illicit Trafficking, Rahmani Fazli said the country’s law enforcement forces and judicial and intelligence organizations have had “an acceptable performance” in campaign against during abuse and trafficking.   SP/PA
