Over 200 producers of paint, resin and related machinery from 24 countries have already registered in this four-day event and several workshops are to be held on the sideline. The names of Egypt, Dubai, Oman, UK, Japan, Korea, Spain, Turkey, India, Italy, Belgium, Saudi Arabia, China, Austria, France, Canada, Switzerland, Holland, Taiwan, UAE, Germany Ukraine, Greece and Norway can be seen on the list. The TPIF is also to host the 13th International Packing & Printing Machinery Exhibition from 14-17 February, 2007.
