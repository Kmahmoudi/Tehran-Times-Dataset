TEHRAN – The first International Exhibition of Agricultural Machineries, Irrigation Systems and Related Industries of Iran (Agritech 2017) kicked off at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds on Friday. According to IRNA, More than 100 domestic and foreign companies from Spain, China, Greece, Turkey, Italy and Germany are participating in the exhibition which will run until January 9. 
The latest technologies and achievements in the field of agricultural machinery, irrigation and related industries including seeding machines, harvesting machines, land leveling equipment, irrigation pipes and fittings, seeds, fertilizers, pesticides and seedlings and etc. are being showcased in this event.
