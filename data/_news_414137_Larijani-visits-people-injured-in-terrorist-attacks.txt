TEHRAN – Majlis Speaker Ali Larijani on Saturday visited those injured in the terrorist attacks in Tehran.
 In two simultaneous terrorist attacks on the administrative building of Majlis and the shrine of Imam Khomeini in Tehran on Wednesday 17 people were killed and 56 injured. A funeral procession was held on Friday for those killed in the attacks. Daesh claimed responsibility for the attacks. NA/PA
