TEHRAN- Petri Peltonen, the under-secretary of state at the Ministry of Employment and Economy of Finland, mentioned mining and energy sectors as the areas proper for cooperation between Finland and Iran, IRNA reported. The Finnish official made the remarks in Iran-Finland Opportunities Gathering held in the building of Iranian Mines and Mining Industries Development and Renovation Organization (IMIDRO) on Sunday.
