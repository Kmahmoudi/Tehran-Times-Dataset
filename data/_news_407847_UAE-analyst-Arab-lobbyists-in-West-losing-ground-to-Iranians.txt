TEHRAN – A political analyst from the UAE has said Arab lobbyists in Western countries are losing ground to Iranians.

 “Iran is actively spreading its lobby network in the West, the U.S. in particular,” Khalaf Ahmad al-Hebtoor wrote on Saturday, Tabnak reported. Arabs should take quick action to save themselves from this situation, he wrote, adding that the National Iranian American Council is one of the greatest forces behind Iran’s moves in the U.S. SP/PA
