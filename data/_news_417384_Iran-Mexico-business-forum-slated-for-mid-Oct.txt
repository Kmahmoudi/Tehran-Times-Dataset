TEHRAN – An Iran-Mexico business forum is due to be held on October 15 at Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) building in Tehran. According to the ICCIMA, a Mexican trade delegation is going to visit Tehran on the mentioned date to hold talks with their Iranian counterparts and discuss mutual cooperation. EF/MA
