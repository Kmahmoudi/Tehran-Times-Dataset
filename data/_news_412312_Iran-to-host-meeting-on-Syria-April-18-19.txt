TEHRAN – Iran will host the next round of Syria talks on April 18-19, ISNA news agency reported on Monday.

 The expert-level meeting will be held in Tehran following an agreement that was made in a previous meeting in Astana, Kazakhstan. The next high-level international meeting on Syria will reportedly be held in Astana on May 3-4. SP/PA
