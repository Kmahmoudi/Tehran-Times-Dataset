The bill was rejected during the open session of the parliament with 83 votes in favor, 120 votes against and 9 abstentions, media reported. The bill will be returned to a joint parliamentary committee for revisions. The joint commission is a legislative body responsible for reviewing budget bills as well as the five-year development plans. Parliament Speaker Ali Larijani said the joint committee must implement the proposed amendments within the next three days.
