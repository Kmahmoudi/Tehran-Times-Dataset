TEHRAN - Senior officials from Iran and the European Union are scheduled to hold talks in Tehran on Monday to discuss ties.
 Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister for Legal and International Affairs Abbas Araqchi and Helga Schmid, the secretary general of European External Action Service (EEAS), planned to chair the third round of Iran-EU talks. Araqchi, Schmid and Behrouz Kamalvandi, the spokesman for the Atomic Energy Organization of Iran, are also scheduled to hold a seminar at the Isfahan Nuclear Technology Center (INTC) on prospects and progress of nuclear cooperation.
