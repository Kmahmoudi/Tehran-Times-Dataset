TEHRAN – A member of the Islamic Coalition Party has said the party makes attempt to promote relations between Iran and China. Speaking in a meeting with Iranian ambassador to China Ali Asqar Khaji on Friday, Hamid Reza Taraqi said his party’s policy is to defend Iran’s dignity and interests in political talks, ISNA reported. He said his party’s meetings with members of the Chinese ruling party have yielded good results in areas of economy, politics, and fight on corruption. SP/PA
