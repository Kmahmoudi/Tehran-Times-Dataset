TEHRAN- “Thanks to its experts and industrialists, the Islamic Republic of Iran could reach 70 percent self-sufficiency in manufacturing equipment needed in petrochemical industry,” Managing Director of Iran’s National Petrochemical Company (NPC) Marziyeh Shahdaie announced on Monday. As IRNA reported, making the remarks in the 8th oil, gas, refining and petrochemical exhibition in the Iranian southern port city of Asalouyeh, NPC director noted that currently, there is no need for foreign experts to help with designing and manufacturing the required equipment in Iran’s petrochemical industry and the held exhibition is set to help domestic companies with marketing.
