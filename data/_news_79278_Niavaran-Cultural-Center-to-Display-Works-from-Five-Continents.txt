According to the information office of the Cultural Heritage Organization, the exhibition will open on May 18, which is World Museum Day, and continue until May 31. The International Council of Museums (ICOM) has invited all the museums of the world to hold exhibitions on cultural subjects and this exhibition is part of this program. The exhibition will display various cultural and artistic works such as African masks, Native American pottery, European paintings, and Asian ivory work and engravings.
