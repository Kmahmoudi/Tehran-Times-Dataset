TEHRAN – The Islamic Revolution Guards Corps is ready to engage in more economic cooperation with the government, the IRGC chief said on Thursday.

 The IRGC cannot stand apathetic to poverty and joblessness, Mohammad Ali Jafari said, Mizan news agency reported. The new approach adopted by the Guards is to focus on offering services, he added, underlining as an example that the water management projects in western Iran are among the brilliant services rendered so far.
