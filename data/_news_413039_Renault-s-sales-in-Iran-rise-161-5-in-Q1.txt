TEHRAN- Renault’s sales in Iran rose sharply (161.5%) for a market share of 9%, up 4.9 points, in the first quarter of 2017 compared to the same period of time in 2016, the company’s international website reported. According to the report, the increase has occurred thanks to the success of Tondar and Sandero in Iran.
