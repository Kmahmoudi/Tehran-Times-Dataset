TEHRAN – Construction of a new hospital with a capacity of 540 beds started in Chabahar, southeastern province of Sistan-Baluchestan, Fars reported on Sunday. Over a ceremony attended by Health Minister Hassan Qazizadeh-Hashemi, the project was officially launched. The 13-storey hospital will be constructed on a land measuring 230,000 square meters during a 3-year period, according to the report. Several health care and medical projects will be also inaugurated during the minister’s trip to the province.
