TEHRAN- The value of Iran’s non-oil exports to Indonesia during the three-month period from March 21 to June 21 stood at $250 million, increasing 86 percent compared to the same period of time in the past year, according to Iran’s commercial attaché to Indonesia. Anvar Kamari also announced that Iran’s weight of non-oil exports to Indonesia rose 52 percent during the mentioned quarter on year, Shata reported on Wednesday.
