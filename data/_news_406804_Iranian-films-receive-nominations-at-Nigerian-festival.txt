TEHRAN – Two Iranian films are on the nominees list of the 13th edition of the Abuja International Film Festival, which will be held in the Nigerian city from October 4 to 7. Ahmad Ramezanzedeh’s “The Tale of Love” has received two nominations in the Outstanding Feature Film and Outstanding Music Score categories while Sadeq Daqiqi’s “Until Ahmad Returns” is nominated for Outstanding Film in the Directing category. The nominees list has been selected from over 700 films from 47 countries. ABU/YAW All Content by Mehr News Agency is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
