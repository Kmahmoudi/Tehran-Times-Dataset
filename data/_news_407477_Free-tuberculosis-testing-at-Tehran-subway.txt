The units are designed to heighten public awareness with experts explaining TB risk factors, its diagnosis, prevention and reasons for developing such a disease using brochures. The scheme is co-planned by Tehran subway company department for social and cultural affairs and western Tehran healthcare center. The units will offer free of charge services for three days at Shahid Beheshti, Sharif University, and Sadeqiyeh stations.
