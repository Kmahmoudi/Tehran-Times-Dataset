TEHRAN – Polish private sector is ready for investment in the shipping and maritime industries of Iran’s southwestern Khuzestan Province, IRIB news reported quoting a Polish official as saying. According to Iran’s Maritime News Agency (MANA), in a meeting with members of Chamber of Commerce and Industries of Ahvaz (center city of Khuzestan), Polish energy minister’s counselor mentioned Khuzestan’s great geographical advantages including long coasts, active ports and access to the Persian Gulf waters and said Polish private companies are ready to contribute to maritime projects in this region.
