TEHRAN – The Intelligence Ministry announced on Tuesday evening that its forces have arrested the killer of two citizens in Dorud, Lorestan province. On December 31, during protests in Dorud a group of rioters stole a fire truck by using force. The arrested individual had put the vehicle in neutral, causing it to go hurtling into a sedan, killing a father and his son inside the car. The individual, who was arrested in Tehran province, had a record in vandalism, sexual harassment, and robbery.  MH/PA
