TEHRAN- An oil and gas delegation from Poland will take a trip to Tehran to discuss bolstering bonds with Iranian private sector during an Iran-Poland business forum on Saturday, the Portal of Tehran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (TCCIMA) reported. The delegation will attend the said forum at the place of Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Mines and Agriculture (ICCIMA) in Tehran.
