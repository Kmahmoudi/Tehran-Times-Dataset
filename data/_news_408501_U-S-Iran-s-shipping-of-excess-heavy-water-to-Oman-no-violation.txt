TEHRAN – U.S. State Department spokesman John Kirby said on Tuesday that Iran’s decision to send its excess heavy water to Oman is no violation of the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA).

 Elsewhere in his press conference, Kirby said he would not expect the United States’ government to purchase any Iranian heavy water in the near future, according to a State Department script of the presser.
