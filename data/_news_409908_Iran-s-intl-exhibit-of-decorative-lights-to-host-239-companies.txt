TEHRAN- The 24th International Exhibition of Chandeliers and Decorative Lights of Iran will host 184 Iranian and 55 foreign companies from China and Italy, the official website of Trade Promotion Organization of Iran (TPO) announced on Sunday. The exhibit will be held at the Tehran Permanent International Fairgrounds from January 16 to 19.
The event is aimed at introducing manufacturing capabilities of the exhibitors, finding new export markets, getting acquainted with the newest technology and equipment of the world, boosting production and creating jobs. MA/MG
