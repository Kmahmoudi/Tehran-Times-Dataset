The French site has praised the Iranian women who play an increasing role in sport. Whether it's playing chess on the world stage, or clashing sticks in a game of hockey, the women of Iran are playing an increasing role in sport, France24 reported. While they always abide by the strict rules and codes of the Islamic Republic - including wearing the hijab - many are using their sporting prowess to fly the nest, leave their homeland and see the world.
