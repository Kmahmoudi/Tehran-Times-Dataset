TEHRAN – Iran has exported over 3,000 tons of clothing worth $39 million to other countries during the first nine months of the current Iranian calendar year (March 21-December 21, 2017), IRNA reported on Tuesday. Afghanistan, Iraq, Turkmenistan, Tajikistan, Kyrgyzstan, Pakistan, the United Arab Emirates, Turkey, Oman, Azerbaijan, Kuwait, Armenia, Georgia, Yemen, Germany, the Netherlands, Canada, United Kingdom, Lebanon, India, Norway, Japan, Spain, and Australia were some of the destination markets for Iranian clothing items. Meanwhile, Iran exported $48 million of the mentioned commodity during the last [calendar] year. EF/MG
