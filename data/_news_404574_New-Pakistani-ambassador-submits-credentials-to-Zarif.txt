TEHRAN – Pakistan’s newly-appointed ambassador to Tehran on Saturday submitted a copy of his credentials to Foreign Minister Mohammad-Javad Zarif.

 Calling for “good and closer ties” during the meeting, Zarif expressed hope that relations between the two neighboring countries will expand in all dimensions, IRNA reported. Zarif highlighted the importance of regional security, calling for cooperation among Iran, Afghanistan and Pakistan to maintain stability and security especially around borders. SP/PA
