TEHRAN – Majlis speaker Ali Larijani has said he does not plan to run for presidency next year, ILNA reported. 

 While on an official trip to Kuhgiluyeh-Boyer Ahmad on Friday to inspect development projects in the province, Larijani also said, “The current composition of the Majlis is good.”  He added, “The 10th Majlis is more active than the 9th Majlis used to be.” SP/PA
