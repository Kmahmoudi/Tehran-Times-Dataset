TEHRAN – During a meeting in Tbilisi on Wednesday, Iranian and Georgian officials agreed on the continuation of the visa waiver agreement between the two countries, ILNA reported. The agreement was reached during the meeting, which was between an Iranian delegation, headed by the Foreign Ministry Consular General Ali Asghar Mohammadi, and Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Alexander Khotisiashvili.  The two sides discussed the consular relations and recent developments and underscored the importance of friendly relations as well as continuation of the existing agreement on visa waiver.
