TEHRAN – The Iranian embassy in Ankara has recommended Iranians visiting Turkey to increase their security consciousness. “Iranians who plan to visit Turkey are required to stay away from going to busy, insecure places,” the embassy said in an announcement, the Iranian Foreign Ministry said on Monday. “With regard to incidents that have happened to our fellow nationals, Iranians in Turkey are required to heed the warnings of Islamic Republic of Iran’s consulate,” the message read.
